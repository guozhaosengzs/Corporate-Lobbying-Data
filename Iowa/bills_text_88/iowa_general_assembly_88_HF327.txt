85.55
Franchisor-franchisee



relationship.



1.
For

523H.1.



2.

87,

franchisee

following

a.
The
franchisor

to

to

the
employer
of
the
franchisee
or
of
the
employees

the



franchisee.



b.

franchisor

workers’

a
type
or
degree
of



control
over

franchisee

franchisee’s

that



is
not
customarily
exercised
by
a
franchisor
for
the
purpose
of



protecting
the
franchisor’s
trademarks
and
