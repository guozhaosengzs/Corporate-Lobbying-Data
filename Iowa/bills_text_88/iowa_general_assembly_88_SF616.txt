Sec.
2.
CIVIL
TRIALS
——
LOCATION.
Notwithstanding
any



provision
to
the
contrary,
for
the
fiscal
year
beginning
July



1,
2019,
and
ending
June
30,
2020,
if
all

a
case



agree,
a

including
a
jury

may
take

in
a



county

the
county
with
proper
jurisdiction,
even



if
the
contiguous
county
is
located
in
an
adjacent
judicial



district

judicial
election
district.
If

trial
is
moved



pursuant
to
this
section,
court
personnel
shall
treat
the
case



as
if
a
change
of
venue
occurred.
However,
if
a
trial
is
moved



to
an
adjacent
judicial
district
or
judicial
election
district,



the
judicial
officers
serving
in
the
judicial
district
or



judicial
election
district
receiving
the
case
shall
preside



over
the
case.
