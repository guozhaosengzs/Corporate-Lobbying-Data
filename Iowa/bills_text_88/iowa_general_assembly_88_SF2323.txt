Section
1.
REPORTS
AND
POWERS
OF
GUARDIANS
AND

TRANSITION

1.
Minor
guardianships.
Notwithstanding
the
provisions



of
section
232D.501,
subsection
1,
paragraph
“a”,
designating



a
verified
initial
care
plan
be
filed
by
the
guardian
within



sixty
days
of
appointment,
for
all
minor
guardianship
cases



wherein
the
guardianship
was

the

2020,

required

section
232D.501,
subsection
1,
paragraph
“a”,



shall

with
the
previously
scheduled
annual
report.




Senate
File
2323,

2


The

must

with
the
requirements
set
forth



in
section
232D.501,
subsection
1,
paragraph
“a”.
Guardians



appointed
prior
to
January
1,
2020,

continuing
authority



to
perform
acts
concerning
the
protected
person
that
were



authorized
prior
to
January
1,
2020,
through
the
date
of
the



guardian’s
previously
scheduled
annual
report.
