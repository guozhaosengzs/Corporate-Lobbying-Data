.

Iowa

——

1.
The

United

highway



20

from
Sioux
City

Dubuque

“Iowa
Medal
of
Honor
Highway”.



2.
The
department
shall
adopt
rules
pursuant
to
chapter
17A



to
provide
for
an
application,

and
inspection
process



for
the

of

“Iowa

Honor

The

All

The

The

and
air

whether
