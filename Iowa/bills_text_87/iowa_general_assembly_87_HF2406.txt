PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 257.11, subsection 5, paragraph a,
  1  2 subparagraph (1), Code 2018, is amended to read as follows:
  1  3    (1)  In order to provide additional funding to increase
  1  4 student opportunities and redirect more resources to student
  1  5 programming for school districts that share operational
  1  6 functions, a district that shares with a political subdivision
  1  7 one or more operational functions of a curriculum director or
  1  8 school counselor, or one or more operational functions in the
  1  9 areas of superintendent management, business management, human
  1 10 resources, transportation, or operation and maintenance for at
  1 11 least twenty percent of the school year shall be assigned a
  1 12 supplementary weighting for each shared operational function.
  1 13 A school district that shares an operational function in
  1 14 the area of superintendent management shall be assigned a
  1 15 supplementary weighting of eight pupils for the function. A
  1 16 school district that shares an operational function in the area
  1 17 of business management, human resources, transportation, or
  1 18 operation and maintenance shall be assigned a supplementary
  1 19 weighting of five pupils for the function. A school district
  1 20 that shares the operational functions of a curriculum director                                     APPROPRIATIONS
