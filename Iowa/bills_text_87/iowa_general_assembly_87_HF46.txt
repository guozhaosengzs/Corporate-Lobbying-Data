  2 22                           EXPLANATION
  2 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 25    This bill relates to the determination of the regular
  2 26 program state cost per pupil and the determination of the
  2 27 regular program district cost per pupil for school districts.
  2 28 Those amounts are used to determine supplemental state aid
  2 29 amounts for each school budget year, determining the budget
  2 30 authority for each school district, and determining amounts
  2 31 of preschool foundation aid for schools participating in the
  2 32 statewide preschool program for four=year=old children under
  2 33 Code chapter 256C.
  2 34    Current Code section 257.9 provides that for the budget
  2 35 year beginning July 1, 1992, and succeeding budget years, the
  3  1 regular program state cost per pupil for a budget year is the
  3  2 regular program state cost per pupil for the base year plus the
  3  3 regular program supplemental state aid for the budget year.
  3  4    Under the bill, for the budget year beginning July 1, 2017,
  3  5 and succeeding budget years beginning before July 1, 2025,
  3  6 the regular program state cost per pupil for a budget year is
  3  7 the regular program state cost per pupil for the base year
  3  8 plus the regular program supplemental state aid for the budget
  3  9 year, plus $20. Additionally, for the budget year beginning
  3 10 July 1, 2025, the regular program state cost per pupil is the
  3 11 regular program state cost per pupil for the base year plus the
  3 12 regular program supplemental state aid for the budget year,
  3 13 plus $15. With the budget year beginning July 1, 2026, and
  3 14 succeeding budget years, the regular program state cost per
  3 15 pupil for a budget year will again be calculated as the regular
  3 16 program state cost per pupil for the base year plus the regular
  3 17 program supplemental state aid for the budget year, without any
  3 18 additional amount added.
  3 19    The bill also provides that if the regular program district
  3 20 cost per pupil for a budget year calculated for any school
  3 21 district is less than the regular program state cost per pupil
  3 22 for that budget year, the department of management shall
  3 23 increase the regular program district cost per pupil of that
  3 24 district to an amount equal to the regular program state cost
  3 25 per pupil for the budget year.
  3 26    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
