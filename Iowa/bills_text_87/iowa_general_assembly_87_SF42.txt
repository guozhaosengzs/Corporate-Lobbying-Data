  2 21                           EXPLANATION
  2 22 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 23 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 24    This bill makes jurisdictional changes to small claims
  2 25 court cases. The bill provides that a small claims court case
  2 26 commenced on or after July 1, 2017, shall not involve damages
  2 27 or value in excess of $10,000. Under existing law, a small
  2 28 claims court case shall not involve damages or value in excess
  2 29 of $5,000. The bill further provides that the jurisdictional
  2 30 amount shall revert to $5,000 if a court finds the $10,000
  2 31 amount unconstitutional. By increasing the jurisdictional
  2 32 amount for small claims court the bill expands the jurisdiction
  2 33 of a magistrate or district associate judge to hear and
  2 34 assess judgment on certain actions, including county and city
  2 35 violations.
