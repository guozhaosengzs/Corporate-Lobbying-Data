 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act requesting an interim committee relating to
  2    confidentiality provisions under Iowa's open records law.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 6068YH (5) 87
    ec/rh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  INTERIM STUDY COMMITTEE ==== PUBLIC RECORDS
  1  2 AND CONFIDENTIAL RECORDS UNDER IOWA'S OPEN RECORDS LAW.  The
  1  3 legislative council is requested to establish a legislative
  1  4 interim committee to meet during the 2018 interim to study and
  1  5 discuss issues relating to the statutory designation of certain
  1  6 public records as confidential records under chapter 22 (Iowa's
  1  7 open records law).  The committee shall work to develop a clear
  1  8 statutory categorization of the terms "government records",
  1  9 "public records", and "confidential records" to facilitate the
  1 10 assignment of particular government records to each category,
  1 11 and to provide a framework to better inform and provide
  1 12 guidance to public record custodians relating to their rights
  1 13 and responsibilities under chapter 22. In conducting the study
  1 14 and developing recommendations, the committee shall consider
  1 15 testimony from interested stakeholders, including but not
  1 16 limited to representatives from the attorney general's office,
  1 17 the Iowa public information board, the Iowa state association
  1 18 of counties, the Iowa league of cities, and state and local
  1 19 government agencies.  The committee shall submit its findings
  1 20 and recommendations to the general assembly by December 31,
  1 21 2018.
  1 22                           EXPLANATION
  1 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 25    INTERIM STUDY ==== PUBLIC RECORDS AND CONFIDENTIAL RECORDS.
  1 26  This bill requests the legislative council to establish
  1 27 a legislative interim committee to meet during the 2018
  1 28 interim to study and discuss issues relating to the statutory
  1 29 designation of certain public records as confidential records
  1 30 under Code chapter 22 (Iowa's open records law). The committee
  1 31 is directed to develop a clear statutory categorization of the
  1 32 terms "government records", "public records", and "confidential
  1 33 records" to facilitate the assignment of particular government
  1 34 records to each category, and to provide a framework to
  1 35 better inform and provide guidance to public record custodians
  2  1 relating to their rights and responsibilities under Code
  2  2 chapter 22. The committee is directed to submit its findings
  2  3 and recommendations in a report to the general assembly by
  2  4 December 31, 2018.
