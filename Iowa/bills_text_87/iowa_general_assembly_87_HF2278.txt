  4 29 revenues and the performance results achieved for those
  4 30 expenditures. Additionally, the bill provides that it is the
  4 31 intent of the general assembly to direct the department of
  4 32 management to create and maintain a searchable budget database
  4 33 and internet site detailing where school district tax dollars
  4 34 and other funds are expended, the purposes for which tax
  4 35 dollars and other funds are expended, and the results achieved
  5  1 for all taxpayer investments in school districts.

  5  3 department of management shall develop and make publicly
  5  4 available a database internet site for searching, accessing,
  5  5 and processing data, including the data required in the bill,
  5  6 for the most recent school budget year. The internet site is
  5  7 required to be developed in such a way that the information can
  5  8 be provided to other software applications, including internet
  5  9 software applications, in a manner and format that allows such
  5 10 software applications to access and interpret the data using
  5 11 the internal programming of the software applications.  The
  5 12 searchable internet site developed pursuant to the bill is
  5 13 required to allow the public at no cost to search and compile
  5 14 such information. Each school district shall provide the
  5 15 following to the department of management: (1) the name of the
  5 16 entity or recipient of school district funds; (2) the amount of
  5 17 school district funds expended; (3) the funding or expending
  5 18 school district; (4) the funding source; (5) the budget
  5 19 program or activity of the expenditure; (6) the descriptive
  5 20 purpose for the funding action or expenditure; (7) the expected
  5 21 performance outcome for the funding action or expenditure; (8)
  5 22 the past performance outcomes achieved for the funding action
  5 23 or expenditure; (9) the state audit or report relating to the
  5 24 entity or recipient of school district funds or the budget
  5 25 program or activity or agency; and (10) any other relevant
  5 26 information specified by the director of the department of
  5 27 management.
  5 28    Effective July 1, 2020, the bill requires the internet
  5 29 site to be updated regularly as new data and information
  5 30 become available, but shall be updated no less frequently than
  5 31 quarterly thereafter as new data becomes available. All school
  5 32 districts are required provide to the director data that is
  5 33 required to be included on the internet site not later than
  5 34 60 days following the close of each quarter during the school
  5 35 budget year.  The bill requires the director to ensure that all
  6  1 data added to the internet site remain accessible to the public
  6  2 for a minimum of 10 years.  The bill also establishes criteria
  6  3 to determine the director of the department of management's
  6  4 compliance with the bill.

  6  6 section 25B.3.  The bill requires that the state cost of
