 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act making an appropriation to the department of corrections
  2    for certain costs associated with establishing a home
  3    building program.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 6030XS (5) 87
    ec/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  REBUILD IOWA INFRASTRUCTURE FUND ==== DEPARTMENT
  1  2 OF CORRECTIONS == HOME BUILDING PROGRAM APPROPRIATION.  There
  1  3 is appropriated from the rebuild Iowa infrastructure fund to
  1  4 the department of corrections for the fiscal year beginning
  1  5 July 1, 2018, and ending June 30, 2019, the following amount,
  1  6 or so much thereof as is necessary, to be used for the purposes
  1  7 designated:
  1  8    For costs associated with site development and improvement,
  1  9 construction, and related equipment purchases necessary for
  1 10 establishing a home building program to provide low=cost homes
  1 11 for income=qualified buyers, notwithstanding section 8.57,
  1 12 subsection 5, paragraph "c":
  1 13 .................................................. $  2,000,000
  1 14    In developing the home building program, Iowa prison
  1 15 industries shall be authorized to contract with a nonprofit
  1 16 organization to operate the program and to provide an inmate
  1 17 workforce for the program consistent with the objectives of
  1 18 section 904.801.
  1 19                           EXPLANATION
  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    This bill appropriates project funding for FY 2018=2019
  1 23 from the rebuild Iowa infrastructure fund to the department
  1 24 of corrections for certain costs associated with establishing
  1 25 a home building program to provide low=cost homes for
  1 26 income=qualified buyers.  The bill authorizes Iowa prison
  1 27 industries to contract with a nonprofit organization to operate
  1 28 the program and to provide an inmate workforce for the program
  1 29 consistent with Code section 904.801 relating to the objective
  1 30 of prison industries to provide inmates with meaningful jobs
  1 31 and rehabilitative work.
