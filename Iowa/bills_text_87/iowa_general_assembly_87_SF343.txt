 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to timely filing of claims under Medicaid
  2    managed care.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2274XS (3) 87
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  MEDICAID MANAGED CARE == TIMELY FILING OF
  1  2 CLAIMS.  The department of human services shall adopt rules
  1  3 pursuant to chapter 17A and shall amend Medicaid managed care
  1  4 contracts to require that Medicaid managed care organizations
  1  5 allow Medicaid providers a timely filing period of 365 days
  1  6 from the date of service for submission of claims. The rules
  1  7 and contract amendments shall also provide that a claim may be
  1  8 resubmitted or adjusted if the claim is submitted within 365
  1  9 days from the last date of adjudication.
  1 10                           EXPLANATION
  1 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 12 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 13    This bill directs the department of human services to
  1 14 adopt administrative rules and amend Medicaid managed care
  1 15 contracts to require Medicaid managed care organizations to
  1 16 allow Medicaid providers a timely filing period of 365 days
  1 17 from the date of service for submission of claims.  The rules
  1 18 and contract amendments are also to provide that a claim may be
  1 19 resubmitted or adjusted if the claim is submitted within 365
  1 20 days from the last date of adjudication.
       LSB 2274XS (3) 87
       pf/nh
