  1 16                           EXPLANATION
  1 17 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 18 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 19    This bill relates to prohibited provisions in rental
  1 20 agreements.
  1 21    Current law provides that a landlord cannot willfully use
  1 22 provisions that the landlord knows are prohibited in a rental
  1 23 agreement for a rental property. Current law also provides
  1 24 that a landlord or a tenant cannot knowingly use provisions
  1 25 that are known to be prohibited in a rental agreement for a
  1 26 space at a mobile or manufactured home park.
  1 27    The bill provides that landlords cannot willfully enforce
  1 28 provisions that the landlord knows are prohibited in a rental
  1 29 agreement for a rental property. The bill also provides that a
  1 30 landlord or a tenant cannot knowingly enforce provisions known
  1 31 to be prohibited in a rental agreement for a space at a mobile
  1 32 or manufactured home park.
