  1 18 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 19 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 20    This bill extends the immunity from tort liability, absent
  1 21 an express statute, granted to municipalities for claims
  1 22 based upon or arising out of an act or omission in connection
  1 23 with emergency response services to nonprofit corporations
  1 24 providing the same services pursuant to a written contract with
  1 25 a city, county, township, or benefited fire district. Any such
  1 26 contract and any modifications thereto shall be filed with the
  1 27 secretary of state within 30 days of their effectiveness.
