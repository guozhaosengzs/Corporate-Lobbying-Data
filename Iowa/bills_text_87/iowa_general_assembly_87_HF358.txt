  3 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3 21    This bill relates to attempt to commit murder against a peace
  3 22 officer.
  3 23    The bill defines "peace officer" to mean the same as defined
  3 24 in section 801.4.
