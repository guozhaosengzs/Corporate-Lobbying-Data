  1 27 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 28 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 29    This bill relates to permits issued by the department of
  1 30 transportation to allow all=terrain vehicle trails to cross
  1 31 primary highways.
  1 32    The bill provides that a city street or a county roadway that
  1 33 has been designated by the city or county for the operation of
  1 34 all=terrain vehicles shall be considered an all=terrain vehicle
  1 35 trail for the purposes of issuing such permits.
  2  1 The bill requires the department of transportation to
  2  2 issue a permit to allow an all=terrain vehicle trail to cross
  2  3 a primary highway if the intersection of the trail and the
  2  4 highway is regulated by an official traffic=control signal as
  2  5 defined in Code section 321.1.  Code section 321.1(47) defines
  2  6 "official traffic=control signal" as any device, whether
  2  7 manually, electrically, or mechanically operated, by which
  2  8 traffic is alternately directed to stop and to proceed.
