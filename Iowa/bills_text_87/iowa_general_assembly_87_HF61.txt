  2 11 makes a payment in good faith under the policy or contract

  2 14 health maintenance organization, managed care plan, health care
  2 15 plan, preferred provider organization, or other third=party
  2 16 payor, the payment for coverage of dental services shall be
  2 17 made directly to the designated beneficiary of the policy
  2 18 or contract.  The section shall not be construed to prevent
  2 19 reconciliation of duplicate payments.

  2 24 dental services provided regardless of whether there is

  2 26 paid does not exceed 100 percent of the amount of the claim.
