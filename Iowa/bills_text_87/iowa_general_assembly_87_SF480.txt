  1  3    A person licensed by a board under this subtitle shall not
  1  4 be subject to discipline under this chapter or the board's
  1  5 enabling statute based solely on the licensee's recommendation
  1  6 or provision of a treatment method for Lyme disease or other
  1  7 tick=borne disease if the recommendation or provision of such
  1  8 treatment meets all the following criteria:
  1  9    1.  The treatment is provided after an examination is
  1 10 performed and informed consent is received from the patient.
  1 11    2.  The licensee identifies a medical reason for
  1 12 recommending or providing the treatment.
  1 13    3.  The treatment is provided after the licensee informs the
  1 14 patient about other recognized treatment options and describes
  1 15 to the patient the licensee's education, experience, and
  1 16 credentials regarding the treatment of Lyme disease or other
  1 17 tick=borne disease.
  1 18    4.  The licensee uses the licensee's own medical judgment
  1 19 based on a thorough review of all available clinical
  1 20 information and Lyme disease or other tick=borne disease
  1 21 literature to determine the best course of treatment for the
  1 22 individual patient.
  1 23    5.  The treatment will not, in the opinion of the licensee,
  1 24 result in the direct and proximate death of or serious bodily
  1 25 injury to the patient.
  1 26    Sec. 2.  EFFECTIVE UPON ENACTMENT.  This Act, being deemed of
  1 27 immediate importance, takes effect upon enactment.

  2 11 licensee, result in the direct and proximate death of or
  2 12 serious bodily injury to the patient.
