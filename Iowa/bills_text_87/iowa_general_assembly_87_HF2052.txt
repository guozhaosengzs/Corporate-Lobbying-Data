  2  5    This bill provides for the participation of certain
  2  6 providers in the state family planning services program.  Under
  2  7 the state family planning services program, distribution of
  2  8 family planning services program funds are prohibited from
  2  9 being made to any entity that performs abortions or that
  2 10 maintains or operates a facility where abortions are performed.
  2 11 The bill provides that any entity that performs abortions or
  2 12 that maintains or operates a facility where abortions are
  2 13 performed is not to be interpreted to include a nonpublic
  2 14 entity that is a distinct location of a nonprofit health care
  2 15 delivery system, if the distinct location provides family
  2 16 planning services but does not perform abortions or maintain
  2 17 or operate as a facility where abortions are performed.  The
  2 18 bill directs the department of human services to adopt rules
  2 19 pursuant to Code chapter 17A  to require that as a condition of
  2 20 eligibility as a provider under the family planning services
  2 21 program, each distinct location of a nonprofit health care
  2 22 delivery system shall enroll in the program as a separate
  2 23 provider, be assigned a distinct provider identification
  2 24 number, and complete an attestation regarding non=provision
  2 25 of abortions at the distinct location.  The bill defines
  2 26 "nonprofit health care delivery system" as an Iowa nonprofit
  2 27 corporation that controls, directly or indirectly, a regional
  2 28 health care network consisting of hospital facilities and
  2 29 various ambulatory and clinic locations that provide a range
  2 30 of primary, secondary, and tertiary inpatient, outpatient, and
  2 31 physician services.
