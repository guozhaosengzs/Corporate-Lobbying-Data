  1 22 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 23 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 24    This bill creates new Code section 481A.56A to allow a
  1 25 hunter with a valid hunting license and deer hunting license
  1 26 who wounds a deer while hunting to use a dog to locate and
  1 27 retrieve the wounded deer. The hunter or any person in the
  1 28 company of the hunter shall not possess a firearm or bow while
  1 29 using a dog in this manner and shall have the dog leashed and
  1 30 under control at all times during the search. A person must
  1 31 obtain permission from the owner or tenant before using a dog
  1 32 to locate a wounded deer on private property. The natural
  1 33 resource commission shall adopt rules pursuant to Code chapter
  1 34 17A to implement this provision.
  1 35    A violation of the new provision is punishable by a scheduled
  2  1 fine of $25.
