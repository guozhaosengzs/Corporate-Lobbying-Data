  1 12 participants shall be forty or more.
  1 13    (3)  The award of prizes or other inducements for
  1 14 participation in the event.
  1 15    b.  For the purposes of this section, "bass fishing
  1 16 tournament" also includes a planned event on public water for
  1 17 the purpose of fishing for bass.
  1 18    2.  A person shall apply for a permit to hold a bass fishing
  1 19 tournament.  The commission shall, by rule, specify the
  1 20 requirements to obtain a permit including but not limited to
  1 21 the following:
  1 22    a.  Minimum requirements for weigh=in, handling, and release
  1 23 of live bass by tournament participants.
  1 24    b.  Measurement of bass to length and release from a vessel.
  1 25    c.  Allowance of up to five bass for weigh=in during the
  1 26 tournament.
  1 27    d.  Allowance of possession of bass of any length so long as
  1 28 the bass are kept alive and are released after weigh=in.
  1 29    e.  Cleaning of vessels used before and after the tournament
  1 30 in compliance with department guidelines to prevent the
  1 31 transportation of aquatic invasive species.
