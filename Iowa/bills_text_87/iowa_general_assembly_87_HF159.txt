  1 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 25    This bill modifies the penalties for persons 18 years of age
  1 26 or older who commit indecent exposure.
  1 27    The bill increases the penalties for indecent exposure from
  1 28 a serious misdemeanor to an aggravated misdemeanor for acts
  1 29 involving a person 18 years of age or older who exposes oneself
  1 30 or commits a sex act in the presence of or view of a third
  1 31 person and the viewer of the indecent exposure is under 13
  1 32 years of age.
  1 33    A person who commits indecent exposure must register as a sex
  1 34 offender for 10 years and is subject to an additional 10=year
  1 35 special sentence pursuant to Code section 903B.2 after the
  2  1 sentence for indecent exposure ends.
  2  2    A serious misdemeanor is punishable by confinement for no
  2  3 more than one year and a fine of at least $315 but not more than
  2  4 $1,875.  An aggravated misdemeanor is punishable by confinement
  2  5 for no more than two years and a fine of at least $625 but not
  2  6 more than $6,250.
