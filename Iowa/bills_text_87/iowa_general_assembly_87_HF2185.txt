  3 26                           EXPLANATION
  3 27 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3 28 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3 29    This bill creates a new requirement and procedure for
  3 30 disposing of waste tires in the state.
  3 31    Current law prevents a person from disposing of waste tires
  3 32 unless the tires undergo proper processing as determined by the
  3 33 department of natural resources. "Processing" means producing
  3 34 or manufacturing usable materials from waste tires.
  3 35    The bill requires waste tires to undergo conversion, as
  4  1 defined in the bill, if they originate within a waste tire
  4  2 conversion district. If a waste tire conversion center does
  4  3 not exist within a district, a person may dispose of waste
  4  4 tires at a waste tire conversion center in a different waste
  4  5 tire conversion district or dispose of the waste tires at a
  4  6 landfill after the waste tires undergo processing. A person
  4  7 may also dispose of waste tires according to current law when
  4  8 the waste tires collected at a waste tire conversion center
  4  9 exceed the capacity of the center. A person who violates the
  4 10 provisions of the bill is subject to a civil penalty pursuant
  4 11 to Code sections 455D.22 and 455D.25.
