  1  7 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  8 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  9    This bill relates to the criminal offense of invasion of
  1 10 privacy.
  1 11    The bill strikes, as an element of the offense, the
  1 12 requirement that the victim of an invasion of privacy offense
  1 13 does not have knowledge about being viewed, photographed, or
  1 14 filmed, while in a state of full or partial nudity, and with a
  1 15 reasonable expectation of privacy.
  1 16    A person who commits invasion of privacy commits an
  1 17 aggravated misdemeanor and is required to register as a sex
  1 18 offender.  A person who commits invasion of privacy is also
  1 19 subject to a 10=year special sentence under Code section
  1 20 903B.2.
