  1 27                           EXPLANATION
  1 28 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 29 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 30    This bill grants the medical cannabidiol board authority
  1 31 to recommend to the department of public health alteration
  1 32 of the definition of medical cannabidiol under the medical
  1 33 cannabidiol Act (Code chapter 124E), including the allowable
  1 34 tetrahydrocannabinol (THC) level. The bill also allows the
  1 35 medical cannabidiol board to recommend the alteration of
  2  1 the list of debilitating medical conditions for which the
  2  2 medical use of cannabidiol would be medically beneficial
  2  3 to the department of public health, which shall adopt such
  2  4 recommendations by rule. The bill requires the board of
  2  5 medicine to adopt recommendations made by the medical
  2  6 cannabidiol board regarding the form and quantity of allowable
  2  7 medical uses of marijuana. Current law allows the department
  2  8 of public health to do so following the approval of the board
  2  9 of medicine.
  2 10    The bill strikes current provisions in the law which allow
  2 11 the medical cannabidiol board to recommend increasing the THC
  2 12 level to more than 3 percent to the general assembly and that
  2 13 the general assembly shall have the sole authority to alter the
  2 14 definition of medical cannabidiol.
