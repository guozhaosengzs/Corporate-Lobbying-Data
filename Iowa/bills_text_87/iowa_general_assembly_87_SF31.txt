  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    This bill authorizes a school district to adopt, for the
  1 23 school district or for an individual school, a mandatory
  1 24 uniform policy, in addition to the dress code policy currently
  1 25 authorized by the Code, if the board determines that a
  1 26 mandatory uniform policy is necessary for the health, safety,
  1 27 or positive educational environment of students and staff in
  1 28 the school environment or for the appropriate discipline and
  1 29 operation of the school.
  1 30    The bill provides that a mandatory uniform policy is not
  1 31 a violation of Code section 280.22, which establishes that
  1 32 public school students have the right to exercise freedom of
  1 33 speech, if the mandatory uniform policy is viewpoint neutral,
  1 34 is reasonably related to legitimate pedagogical concerns, or
  1 35 protects students from sexually explicit, indecent, or lewd
  2  1 speech.
  2  2    The bill eliminates the general assembly's findings and
  2  3 declarations regarding a dress code policy.
