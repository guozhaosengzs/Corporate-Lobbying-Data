  1 32                           EXPLANATION
  1 33 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 34 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 35    This joint resolution proposes an amendment to the
  2  1 Constitution of the State of Iowa relating to the terms of
  2  2 supreme court justices.
  2  3    The resolution modifies the term of a supreme court justice
  2  4 from not less than eight years to not less than four years,
  2  5 beginning January 1, 2021. The initial term of a supreme court
  2  6 justice shall be for one year after appointment and until
  2  7 January 1 following the next judicial election. After standing
  2  8 for retention for the initial term, the supreme court justice
  2  9 shall stand for retention before the electorate every four
  2 10 years.
  2 11    The resolution, if adopted, would be published and then
  2 12 referred to the next general assembly (88th) for adoption,
  2 13 before being submitted to the electorate for ratification.
