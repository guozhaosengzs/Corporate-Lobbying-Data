  1  5    1.  The board of directors of a school district with a

  1  7 highest regular program district cost per pupil among all
  1  8 school districts in the state for the same budget year that
  1  9 wishes to receive the budget adjustment under this section may
  1 10 adopt a resolution by May 15 preceding the budget year and
  1 11 shall within ten days of adoption of the resolution notify the
  1 12 department of management of the amount of the budget adjustment
  1 13 to be received.
  1 14    2.  a.  For budget years beginning on or after July 1, 2017,
  1 15 but before July 1, 2020, each school district that satisfies
  1 16 the requirements of subsection 1 shall be eligible for a
  1 17 budget adjustment for that budget year in an amount not to
  1 18 exceed the difference between the school district's regular
  1 19 program district cost per pupil and the highest regular program
  1 20 district cost per pupil among all school districts in the state
  1 21 multiplied by the district's budget enrollment.  The resolution
  1 22 adopted under subsection 1 may specify a budget adjustment
  1 23 amount that is less than the maximum amount authorized under
  1 24 this paragraph "a".
  1 25    b.  The school district shall fund the budget adjustment
  1 26 either by using moneys from its unexpended fund balance or by
  1 27 using cash reserve moneys.
  1 28    3.  A budget adjustment received under this section shall
  1 29 not affect the eligibility for or amount of any other budget
  1 30 adjustment authorized by law for the same budget year. In
  1 31 addition, a budget adjustment under this section shall be
  1 32 limited to the budget year for which the adjustment was
  1 33 authorized and shall not be included in any computation of a
  1 34 school district's cost for any future budget year.

  2 28 budget adjustment.  The board of directors of a school district
  2 29 with a regular program district cost per pupil that is less
  2 30 than the highest regular program district cost per pupil among
  2 31 all school districts in the state for the same budget year may
  2 32 annually adopt a resolution to receive the budget adjustment
  2 33 authorized in the bill. Following adoption of the resolution
  2 34 the board of directors must, within 10 days of its adoption,
  2 35 notify the department of management of the amount of the budget
  3  1 adjustment to be received.
  3  2    For budget years beginning on or after July 1, 2017, but
  3  3 before July 1, 2020, each eligible school district is eligible
  3  4 for a budget adjustment for that budget year in an amount
  3  5 equal to the difference between the school district's regular
  3  6 program district cost per pupil and the highest regular program
  3  7 district cost per pupil among all school districts in the
  3  8 state multiplied by the district's budget enrollment.  The
  3  9 resolution authorizing the budget adjustment may specify a
  3 10 budget adjustment amount that is less than the maximum amount
  3 11 authorized under the bill. The bill requires the school
  3 12 district to fund the budget adjustment either by using moneys
  3 13 from its unexpended fund balance or by using cash reserve
  3 14 moneys.
  3 15    The bill specifies that the budget adjustment received does
  3 16 not affect the eligibility for or amount of any other budget
  3 17 adjustment authorized by law for the same budget year and that
  3 18 the budget adjustment under the bill is limited to the budget
  3 19 year for which the adjustment was authorized and shall not be
  3 20 included in any computation of a school district's cost for any
  3 21 future budget year.
  3 22    Under the bill, if a school district uses funds from its
  3 23 cash reserve during the base year to fund a budget adjustment
  3 24 authorized in the bill, the board of directors of the school
  3 25 district shall include in its general fund budget document
  3 26 information about the amount of the cash reserve used for such
  3 27 purpose.
  3 28    Notwithstanding the May 15 deadline for adopting a
  3 29 resolution to approve the budget adjustment provided for in the
  3 30 bill, for the school budget year beginning July 1, 2017, the
  3 31 resolution of the board of directors of a school district must
  3 32 be approved not later than June 10, 2017.
