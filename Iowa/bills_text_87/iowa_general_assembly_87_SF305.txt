  3 22 district cost per pupil among all school districts in the
  3 23 state multiplied by the district's weighted enrollment.  The
  3 24 budget adjustment amount may be less than the maximum amount
  3 25 authorized under the bill. The bill requires the school
  3 26 district to fund the budget adjustment using interest earned by
  3 27 the school district on cash reserve funds.
