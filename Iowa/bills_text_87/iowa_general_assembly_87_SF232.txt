  1 14 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 15 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.

  1 17 the attorney general of the potential need for establishment

  1 20 chapter 523A, if the preneed seller requests or consents to a
  1 21 receivership.

  1 23 attorney general of the potential need for establishment of a
  1 24 receivership for a cemetery subject to Code chapter 523I if the
  1 25 cemetery requests or consents to a receivership.
  1 26    As required under current law, the commissioner must
  1 27 continue to make such notifications if the commissioner finds
  1 28 that a preneed seller or cemetery meets one or more specified
  1 29 conditions.
