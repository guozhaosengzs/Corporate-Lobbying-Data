  1 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 12 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 13    This bill relates to the duty of the medical examiner to
  1 14 convene a child fatality review committee.  A child fatality
  1 15 review committee is appointed by the state medical examiner
  1 16 who appoints a medical examiner, a pediatrician, and a person
  1 17 involved with law enforcement to the committee to immediately
  1 18 review the child abuse assessments which involve the fatality
  1 19 of a child under 18.  The bill provides that the state medical
  1 20 examiner shall appoint a child fatality review committee upon
  1 21 the request of a member of the legislature or the governor
  1 22 in cases in which a report of suspected child abuse has been
  1 23 filed with the department of human services pursuant to Code
  1 24 section 232.70 within the two=year period immediately preceding
  1 25 the child's death. Code section 232.70 provides the reporting
  1 26 procedure for mandatory and permissive reporters of child abuse
  1 27 and the requirements of the department upon the receipt of such
  1 28 reports.
