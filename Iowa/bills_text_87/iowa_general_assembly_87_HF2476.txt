  1 28                           EXPLANATION
  1 29 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 30 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 31    Under current law, certain counties are authorized to issue
  1 32 commercial driver's licenses and administer commercial driver's
  1 33 license skills tests if certified to do so by the department of
  1 34 transportation. This bill provides that the county treasurer
  1 35 of a county authorized to issue commercial driver's licenses
  2  1 may charge a fee for the administration of commercial driver's
  2  2 license skills tests by the county.  For a person who resides
  2  3 in the county administering the test, the fee shall not exceed
  2  4 $50. For a person who resides outside the county administering
  2  5 the test, the fee shall not exceed $75. The bill requires the
  2  6 county treasurer to retain the fees charged under the bill for
  2  7 deposit in the county general fund. The bill provides that the
  2  8 provisions of the bill are repealed July 1, 2021.
