  1 17 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 18 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 19    This bill concerns the payment of court fees and other fees
  1 20 to the clerk of the district court by a city or county.
  1 21    The bill provides a city or county with the option to pay
  1 22 court costs or fees when the court filing requiring the payment
  1 23 occurs, upon receipt of a statement from the clerk disclosing
  1 24 the amount due, or upon adjudication of the civil or criminal
  1 25 case in district court if the city or county does not prevail
  1 26 in the civil or criminal case.  Current law provides that
  1 27 payment shall be made upon receipt of a statement from the
  1 28 clerk of the district court.
