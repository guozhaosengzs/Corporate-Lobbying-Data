 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to the nonreversion or reallocation of
  2    moneys appropriated to state departments, institutions, or
  3    agencies.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5160YH (6) 87
    jm/rn

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 8.33, Code 2018, is amended by adding the
  1  2 following new unnumbered paragraph:
  1  3 NEW UNNUMBERED PARAGRAPH  Notwithstanding this section,
  1  4 up to ten percent of the moneys appropriated to a state
  1  5 department, institution, or agency that remain unencumbered or
  1  6 unobligated at the close of the fiscal year shall not revert
  1  7 to the general fund but shall remain available for expenditure
  1  8 by the state department, institution, or agency in succeeding
  1  9 fiscal years unless the governing board or head of the state
  1 10 department, institution, or agency, as applicable, reallocates
  1 11 the unencumbered or unobligated moneys for other duties,
  1 12 functions, or purposes.
  1 13                           EXPLANATION
  1 14 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 15 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 16    This bill relates to the nonreversion or reallocation of
  1 17 moneys appropriated to state departments, institutions, or
  1 18 agencies.
  1 19    Under the bill, up to 10 percent of the moneys appropriated
  1 20 to a state department, institution, or agency that remain
  1 21 unencumbered or unobligated at the close of the fiscal year
  1 22 shall not revert to the general fund but shall remain available
  1 23 for expenditure by the state department, institution, or agency
  1 24 in succeeding fiscal years unless the governing board or head
  1 25 of the state department, institution, or agency, as applicable,
  1 26 reallocates the unencumbered or unobligated moneys for other
  1 27 duties, functions, or purposes.
