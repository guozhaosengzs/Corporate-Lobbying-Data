  2 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 25    This bill makes changes to the membership of the college
  2 26 student aid commission.
  2 27    Currently, one of the members of the commission is also
  2 28 a member of the board of regents or the executive director
  2 29 of the board of regents.  The bill strikes a provision that
  2 30 directs this member to convene the organizational meeting of
  2 31 the commission.
  2 32    The bill strikes provisions requiring that one member
  2 33 represent a lending institution and one member be an individual
  2 34 who is repaying or has repaid a student loan guaranteed by the
  2 35 commission, and adds provisions requiring that one member be
  3  1 a parent of a student enrolled in an accredited postsecondary
  3  2 institution in the state, and that one member represent
  3  3 practitioners licensed by the board of educational examiners.
  3  4 The bill increases the number of members who represent the
  3  5 general public from three to four members, and provides that
  3  6 none of the four shall be officers, board members, or trustees
  3  7 of an institution or association of institutions of higher
  3  8 learning.
  3  9    Under the bill, a vacancy occurs when the parent member no
  3 10 longer has a child enrolled in postsecondary education; the
  3 11 voting members of the commission shall elect a chairperson and
  3 12 vice chairperson; and meetings may be called by the chairperson
  3 13 or a majority of the voting members.
