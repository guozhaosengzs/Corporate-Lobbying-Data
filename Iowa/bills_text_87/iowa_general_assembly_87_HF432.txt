  1  9 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 10 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 11    Under current law, motor vehicles operating on a highway are
  1 12 required to display lighted headlamps at any time from sunset
  1 13 to sunrise, and at such other times when conditions such as
  1 14 fog, snow, sleet, or rain provide insufficient lighting to
  1 15 render clearly discernible persons and vehicles on the highway
  1 16 at a distance of 500 feet ahead.
  1 17    This bill requires motor vehicles operating on a highway
  1 18 that are equipped with automatic headlamps or headlamps lighted
  1 19 by default to display lighted headlamps at all times. By
  1 20 operation of law, a violation of this provision is punishable
  1 21 by a scheduled fine of $30.
