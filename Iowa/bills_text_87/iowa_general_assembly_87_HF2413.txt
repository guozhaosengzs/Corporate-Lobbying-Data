  1 12                           EXPLANATION
  1 13 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 14 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 15    This bill amends the definition of child abuse in Code
  1 16 chapter 232, relating to child abuse reporting and assessments.
  1 17    Under current law, child abuse is defined to include the
  1 18 commission of a sexual offense with or to a child as a result of
  1 19 the acts or omissions of the person responsible for the care of
  1 20 the child or of a person, regardless of the age of the person,
  1 21 who resides in a home with the child.
  1 22    The bill changes current law to provide that child abuse
  1 23 includes the commission of a sexual offense with or to a child
  1 24 as a result of the acts or omissions of the person responsible
  1 25 for the care of the child or of a person who is 14 years of age
  1 26 or older and resides in a home with the child.
