                                 (SUCCESSOR TO HSB 683)
       (As Amended and Passed by the House May 2, 2018)

 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to postsecondary institutions by exempting from
  2    the sales tax the sale of instructional materials required
  3    for use in a course of instruction at a postsecondary
  4    institution in this state and by excluding postsecondary
  5    institutions from the sales tax exemption for sales where
  6    the profits are used by or donated to certain nonprofit
  7    entities for educational, religious, or charitable purposes.
  8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    HF 2486 (2) 87
    mm/jh/md
