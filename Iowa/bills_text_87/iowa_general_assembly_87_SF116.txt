  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    This bill relates to the obtaining of a religious exemption
  1 18 from the required immunization of a person enrolling in any
  1 19 elementary or secondary school or a licensed child care center.
  1 20    Currently, an applicant, or if the applicant is a minor, the
  1 21 applicant's parent or legal guardian, must submit an affidavit
  1 22 signed by the applicant, or if the applicant is a minor, the
  1 23 applicant's parent or legal guardian, stating that the required
  1 24 immunization conflicts with the tenets and practices of a
  1 25 recognized religious denomination of which the applicant is an
  1 26 adherent or member in order to be exempt from the immunization
  1 27 requirement. The bill amends this provision to also require
  1 28 that, in addition to the applicant, or the applicant's parent
  1 29 or legal guardian, the leader of the local congregation of a
  1 30 recognized religious denomination of which the applicant is
  1 31 an adherent or member, sign the affidavit stating that the
  1 32 required immunization conflicts with the tenets and practices
  1 33 of the recognized religious denomination to which the local
  1 34 congregation belongs and that the applicant is a member in good
  1 35 standing of the local congregation.
