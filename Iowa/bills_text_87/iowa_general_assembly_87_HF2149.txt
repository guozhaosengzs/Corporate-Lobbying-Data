  1 34 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 35 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  1 This bill relates to fees collected by the county recorder
  2  2 for copies of vital records and applications for marriage
  2  3 licenses.
  2  4    Related to issuance of a certified copy of a birth record,
  2  5 death record, or marriage certificate by a county, the bill
  2  6 requires that the county collect a county fee equal to 40
  2  7 percent of the fee established by the Iowa department of public
  2  8 health for providing those records.
  2  9    Under current law, the fee for filing a marriage application
  2 10 and receiving a copy of the original marriage certificate is
  2 11 set at $35. The bill raises the portion of the fee retained
  2 12 by the county from $4 to $14. Current law also requires a
  2 13 district court to authorize the early validation of a marriage
  2 14 license without the payment of any fees upon showing that an
  2 15 applicant is unable to pay the fees.
