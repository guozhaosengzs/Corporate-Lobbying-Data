  1 13                           EXPLANATION
  1 14 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 15 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 16    Currently, when a city in which an abandoned building is
  1 17 located petitions the court to enter a judgment awarding title
  1 18 of an abandoned property to the city, the city must file its
  1 19 petition and a verified statement declaring that the property
  1 20 has an abandoned building with the county treasurer. Upon this
  1 21 filing, the county treasurer enters a cancellation of the sale
  1 22 of the property and refunds the purchase money to the tax sale
  1 23 certificate holder. Under this bill, the county treasurer
  1 24 instead cancels the sale and makes the refund when the title to
  1 25 the property is awarded to the city. The bill requires a city
  1 26 to notify the county treasurer of such an award.
