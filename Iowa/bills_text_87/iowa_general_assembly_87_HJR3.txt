 HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION

  1 A Joint Resolution proposing an amendment to the Constitution
  2    of the State of Iowa to provide home rule for school
  3    districts.
  4 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 1446YH (3) 87
    kh/rj/jh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  The following amendment to the Constitution of
  1  2 the State of Iowa is hereby proposed:
  1  3    Article III, Legislative Department, Constitution of the
  1  4 State of Iowa, is hereby amended by adding the following new
  1  5 section:
  1  6 School district home rule.SEC. 39B.  School districts are
  1  7 granted home rule power and authority, not inconsistent with
  1  8 the laws of the general assembly, to determine their local
  1  9 affairs and government, except that they shall not have power
  1 10 to levy any tax unless expressly authorized by the general
  1 11 assembly.
  1 12    If the power or authority of a school district conflicts with
  1 13 the power and authority of a municipal corporation, county,
  1 14 or joint county=municipal corporation government, the power
  1 15 and authority exercised by a municipal corporation, county, or
  1 16 joint county=municipal corporation government shall prevail
  1 17 within the jurisdiction of the municipal corporation, county,
  1 18 or joint county=municipal corporation government.
  1 19    The rule or proposition of law that a school district
  1 20 possesses and can exercise only those powers granted in express
  1 21 words is not a part of the law of this state.
  1 22    Sec. 2.  REFERRAL AND PUBLICATION.  The foregoing proposed
  1 23 amendment to the Constitution of the State of Iowa is hereby
  1 24 referred to the general assembly to be chosen at the next
  1 25 general election for members of the general assembly, and the
  1 26 secretary of state is directed to cause the proposed amendment
  1 27 to be published for three consecutive months previous to the
  1 28 date of that election as provided by law.
  1 29                           EXPLANATION
  1 30 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 31 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 32    This joint resolution proposes an amendment to the
  1 33 Constitution of the State of Iowa to provide home rule powers
  1 34 and authority for school districts.  The home rule powers
  1 35 cannot be inconsistent with state law and the power to levy
  2  1 any tax is limited to those taxes expressly authorized by
  2  2 the general assembly.  If the power or authority of a school
  2  3 district conflicts with the power and authority of a municipal
  2  4 corporation, county, or joint county=municipal corporation
  2  5 government, the power and authority exercised by a municipal
  2  6 corporation, county, or joint county=municipal corporation
  2  7 government shall prevail within the jurisdiction of the
  2  8 municipal corporation, county, or joint county=municipal
  2  9 corporation government.
  2 10    The resolution, if adopted, would be referred to the next
  2 11 general assembly before being submitted to the electorate for
  2 12 ratification.
