  2  1 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  2 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  3     This bill authorizes the adjutant general of Iowa to waive
  2  4 certain requirements and limitations of the national guard
  2  5 educational assistance program.
  2  6    Currently, to be determined eligible for the program,
  2  7 a national guard member must submit an application to the
  2  8 adjutant general of Iowa, and be enrolled as an undergraduate
  2  9 student in a community college, a regents university, or an
  2 10 accredited private institution.  An eligible member of the
  2 11 national guard shall not receive educational assistance for
  2 12 more than 120 semester, or the equivalent, credit hours of
  2 13 undergraduate study, and a national guard member who has met
  2 14 the educational requirements for a baccalaureate degree is
  2 15 ineligible for educational assistance.  The adjutant general's
  2 16 decision on eligibility is final.
  2 17    The bill authorizes the adjutant general, in determining the
  2 18 eligibility of a national guard member, to waive the provision
  2 19 requiring enrollment in an Iowa postsecondary institution and
  2 20 the limitations relating to semester hours or the equivalent
  2 21 and for national guard members who have met the educational
  2 22 requirements for a baccalaureate degree if the postsecondary
  2 23 institution in which the national guard member is enrolled is
  2 24 accredited by the higher learning commission and the degree
  2 25 program in which the national guard member is enrolled is
  2 26 included on the current critical manning list issued by the
  2 27 national guard or a branch of the armed forces of the United
  2 28 States.  The bill makes corresponding changes to direct the
  2 29 college student aid commission to pay the amounts due to a
  2 30 postsecondary institution other than a community college,
  2 31 accredited postsecondary institution in Iowa, or a regents
  2 32 university if the adjutant general issues a waiver authorizing
  2 33 a national guard member to enroll in such a postsecondary
  2 34 institution.
