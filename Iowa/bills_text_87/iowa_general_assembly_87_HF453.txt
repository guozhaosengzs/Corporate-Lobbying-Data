  1  1    Section 1.  MIDWIFERY LICENSURE == LEGISLATIVE FINDINGS
  1  2 AND INTENT.  The general assembly recognizes the need for a
  1  3 person to have the freedom to choose the manner, cost, and
  1  4 setting for giving birth. The general assembly finds that
  1  5 access to prenatal care and delivery services is limited by
  1  6 the inadequate number of providers of such services and that
  1  7 the practice of midwifery may help to reduce this shortage.
  1  8 The general assembly also recognizes the need for the safe and
  1  9 effective delivery of newborn babies and the health, safety,
  1 10 and welfare of their mothers in the delivery process. The
  1 11 general assembly, in the interest of public health, promotes
  1 12 the regulation of the practice of midwifery in Iowa for the
  1 13 purpose of protecting the health and welfare of women and
  1 14 infants. The general assembly recognizes that midwifery is a
  1 15 profession in its own right and that it is not the practice of
  1 16 medicine or nursing.
