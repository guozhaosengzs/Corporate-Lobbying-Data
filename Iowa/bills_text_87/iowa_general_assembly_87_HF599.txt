  2 11                           EXPLANATION
  2 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 13 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 14    This bill requires that new school buses ordered for
  2 15 purchase on or after July 1, 2017, that were manufactured on or
  2 16 after July 1, 2017, be equipped with safety belts and safety
  2 17 harnesses for every seating position.  The bill also requires
  2 18 the use of safety belts and safety harnesses by the driver and
  2 19 passengers on such school buses.  Current requirements for the
  2 20 use of child restraint systems for children under 18 years of
  2 21 age also apply to school buses equipped with safety belts and
  2 22 safety harnesses under the bill.
  2 23    Under current law, a violation of seat belt requirements is
  2 24 a scheduled violation punishable by a fine of $50. A violation
  2 25 of seat belt or restraint requirements for persons under 18
  2 26 years of age is a scheduled violation punishable by a fine of
  2 27 $100. Seat belt and restraint violations are not a factor in
  2 28 establishing grounds for license suspension or identifying a
  2 29 person as a habitual violator.
  2 30    The bill may include a state mandate as defined in Code
  2 31 section 25B.3.  The bill makes inapplicable Code section 25B.2,
  2 32 subsection 3, which would relieve a political subdivision from
  2 33 complying with a state mandate if funding for the cost of
  2 34 the state mandate is not provided or specified.  Therefore,
  2 35 political subdivisions are required to comply with any state
  3  1 mandate included in the bill.
