  1 33 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 34 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 35    This bill permits a person who is a peace officer or a
  2  1 retired peace officer to be qualified as a classroom driver
  2  2 education instructor if the person also meets the board of
  2  3 educational examiner's requirements for a classroom driver
  2  4 education instructor authorization.  Currently, only persons
  2  5 holding a teaching license with an endorsement to teach driver
  2  6 education may qualify to be a classroom driver education
  2  7 instructor.
  2  8    The bill directs the board of educational examiners to
  2  9 issue a classroom driver education instructor authorization
  2 10 to a person who completes a training program that meets the
  2 11 standards set by the board and who is either a peace officer or
  2 12 a retired peace officer.  The training program shall include
  2 13 but not be limited to a minimum of 15 contact hours in a program
  2 14 consisting of instruction relating to classroom management,
  2 15 strategies for learning, diversity, and ethics.
  2 16    Under the bill, the term "peace officer" is limited to
  2 17 sheriffs, deputy sheriffs, marshals and police officers of
  2 18 cities, and peace officer members of the departments of public
  2 19 safety and transportation.
