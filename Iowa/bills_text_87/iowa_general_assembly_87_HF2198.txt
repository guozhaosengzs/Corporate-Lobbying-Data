  1 31                           EXPLANATION
  1 32 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 33 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 34    In 2015, the general assembly amended Code sections 321.393
  1 35 (color and mounting) and 321.423 (flashing lights) to allow
  2  1 the use of blue and white lighting devices and reflectors in
  2  2 conjunction with amber lighting devices and reflectors on
  2  3 certain motor vehicles and equipment owned by the department of
  2  4 transportation and used for snow and ice treatment or removal.
  2  5 Those provisions are set to be repealed effective July 1, 2019.
  2  6 This bill strikes the future repeal provisions. The bill also
  2  7 repeals a provision requiring the department of transportation
  2  8 to submit a report to the general assembly prior to July 1,
  2  9 2019, documenting the effectiveness of displaying blue and
  2 10 white lighting devices on such vehicles and equipment.
