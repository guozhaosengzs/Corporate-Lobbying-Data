  1  7 unenforceable, if going armed with, carrying, or transporting

  1 16 transporting a firearm in a courthouse, and providing for an
  1 17 immediate effective date.

  1 19 issued prior to, on, or after the effective date of the bill
  1 20 prohibiting a person from going armed with, carrying, or
  1 21 transporting a firearm in a county courthouse, other than in
  1 22 a courtroom, shall be unenforceable, if going armed with,
  1 23 carrying, or transporting such a firearm is otherwise lawful
  1 24 under the laws of this state.
  1 25    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
