 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to an interest penalty imposed on unpaid
  2    Medicaid managed care claims.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2277XS (2) 87
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  MEDICAID MANAGED CARE == UNPAID CLEAN CLAIMS ==
  1  2 INTEREST PENALTY.  The department of human services shall adopt
  1  3 rules pursuant to chapter 17A and shall amend Medicaid managed
  1  4 care contracts to require that a managed care organization pay
  1  5 an interest penalty of one and one=half percent of the claim
  1  6 amount to the person submitting the claim for any clean claim
  1  7 not paid within sixty days of timely filing. For the purposes
  1  8 of this section, "clean claim" means a claim for which all
  1  9 information required for processing the claim is available.
  1 10                           EXPLANATION
  1 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 12 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 13    This bill requires the department of human services
  1 14 (DHS) to adopt rules pursuant to Code chapter 17A and amend
  1 15 Medicaid managed care contracts to require that a managed
  1 16 care organization pay an interest penalty of 1.5 percent of
  1 17 the claim amount to the person submitting the claim for any
  1 18 clean claim not paid within 60 days of timely filing. The bill
  1 19 defines "clean claim" as a claim for which all information
  1 20 required for processing the claim is available.
       LSB 2277XS (2) 87
       pf/nh
