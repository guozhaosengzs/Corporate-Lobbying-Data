  1  3    1.  "Semiautomatic assault weapon" means any of the
  1  4 following:
  1  5    a.  A semiautomatic rifle that has the capability to accept

  1 12 completely encircles the barrel, allowing the user to hold the

  1 14 excluding a slide that encloses the barrel.

  1 19 capability to accept more than ten rounds of ammunition, except
  1 20 for an attached tubular device designed to accept, and capable
  1 21 of operating only with, .22 caliber rimfire ammunition.
  1 22    c.  Any part, combination of parts, component, device,
  1 23 attachment, or accessory that is designed or functions to
  1 24 accelerate the rate of fire of a semiautomatic rifle but not
  1 25 convert the semiautomatic rifle into a machine gun.
