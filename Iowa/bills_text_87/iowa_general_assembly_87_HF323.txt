  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    This bill relates to the confidentiality of search warrant
  1 27 information in a criminal case resulting in an acquittal or a
  1 28 dismissal.
  1 29    Under current law, all information filed with the court for
  1 30 the purpose of securing a search warrant is a confidential
  1 31 record until a peace officer has executed the warrant and
  1 32 has filed a return of service with the court.  During the
  1 33 confidentiality period, the information shall be sealed by
  1 34 the court and the information cannot be disseminated to any
  1 35 person other than a peace officer, magistrate, or another court
  2  1 employee, in the course of official duties.
  2  2    The bill provides that all information filed with the court
  2  3 for the purpose of securing a warrant for a search or seizure
  2  4 of property in a criminal case that results in an acquittal or
  2  5 the dismissal of all criminal charges, including a dismissal
  2  6 relating to a deferred judgment under Code section 907.9, upon
  2  7 such acquittal or dismissal, shall be a confidential record,
  2  8 shall be sealed by the court, and shall not be disseminated to
  2  9 any person other than a peace officer, magistrate, or another
  2 10 court employee, in the course of official duties.
