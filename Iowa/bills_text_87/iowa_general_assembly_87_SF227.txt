  2 28 all=terrain vehicle with a loaded firearm, whether concealed or

  2 30 or rides on land owned by the person, and the person's conduct
  2 31 is otherwise lawful.

  2 33 or all=terrain vehicle with a loaded firearm, whether concealed
  2 34 or not, without a permit to carry weapons, if the person
  2 35 operates or rides on the snowmobile or all=terrain vehicle
