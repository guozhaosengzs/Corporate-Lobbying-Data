  1  1    Section 1.  
  1  1    Section 1.    1  1    Section 1.    1  2    1.  A county may adopt a confinement feeding operations
  1  3 siting ordinance. The purpose of the ordinance is to allow
  1  4 the county board of supervisors to approve or disapprove the
  1  5 location of a confinement feeding operation, including the
  1  6 construction of a confinement feeding operation structure
  1  7 within the county.
  1  8    2.  A person shall not construct a confinement feeding
  1  9 operation structure in a county that has adopted a confinement
  1 10 feeding operations siting ordinance until the county board
  1 11 of supervisors has approved the location of the confinement
  1 12 feeding operation in a manner and according to procedures
  1 13 required in the ordinance. The ordinance may provide for all
  1 14 of the following:
  1 15    a.  Conditions for the construction of a confinement feeding
  1 16 operation structure, the operation of the confinement feeding
  1 17 operation, or the application of manure originating from the
  1 18 confinement feeding operation.
  1 19    b.  That a person required to be issued a permit by the
  1 20 department for the construction of a confinement feeding
  1 21 operation structure as provided in section 459.303 be issued
  1 22 such permit prior to obtaining approval by the county board of
  1 23 supervisors.
  1 24    c.  That a county board of supervisors base its approval or
  1 25 disapproval on factors other than factors considered by the
  1 26 department when approving or disapproving an application to
  1 27 construct a confinement feeding operation structure.
  1 28    d.  A greater separation distance between a confinement
  1 29 feeding operation structure and a benefitted object or location
  1 30 than is required by this chapter.
  1 31    3.  A county may adopt county legislation necessary to
  1 32 effectuate the purposes of this section notwithstanding
  1 33 sections 331.304A and 335.2.
  1 34                           EXPLANATION
  1 35 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  1 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  2    This bill amends provisions in Code chapter 459 that
  2  3 authorize the department of natural resources to issue
  2  4 permits for the construction of structures associated with a
  2  5 confinement feeding operation.
  2  6    The bill provides that a county may adopt a confinement
  2  7 feeding operations siting ordinance which allows a county board
  2  8 of supervisors to approve the site where the construction is
  2  9 to occur.  The ordinance may authorize the board to place
  2 10 conditions on site approval if the conditions relate to the
  2 11 construction of a confinement feeding operation structure,
  2 12 the operation of the confinement feeding operation, or the
  2 13 application of manure originating from the confinement feeding
  2 14 operation. The bill provides that an ordinance may establish
  2 15 separation distance requirements from a benefitted object or
  2 16 location that are greater than are required by current Code
  2 17 chapter 459.
  2 18    The bill provides that the county may adopt the ordinance
  2 19 notwithstanding provisions in Code section 335.2 which
  2 20 prohibit a county from zoning farm structures and Code section
  2 21 331.304A which prohibit a county from adopting legislation that
  2 22 regulates animal agriculture.
