  2 25 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 26 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 27    This bill requires the natural resource commission to act
  2 28 to prevent, control, or eradicate an infectious or contagious
  2 29 disease in any wild animal. The bill allows the commission
  2 30 to authorize the taking of fish by means found advisable in
  2 31 order to salvage imperiled fish populations. The bill also
  2 32 authorizes the commission to establish a disease management
  2 33 zone for any other wild animal, upon the finding of an
  2 34 infectious or contagious disease in a population of that
  2 35 animal, and take certain actions to prevent the spread of
  3  1 disease through that animal population. The bill prohibits
  3  2 the importation of Cervidae carcasses into the state from any
  3  3 state, province, or country where chronic wasting disease has
  3  4 been confirmed within either captive or wild cervid populations
  3  5 except under certain conditions.
  3  6    A person who transports a Cervidae carcass into the state
  3  7 in violation of the provisions of the bill shall be assessed a
  3  8 minimum $100 fine.

  2 29 disease in any wild animal. The bill allows the commission
  2 30 to authorize the taking of fish by means found advisable in
  2 31 order to salvage imperiled fish populations. The bill also
  2 32 authorizes the commission to establish a disease management
  2 33 zone for any other wild animal, upon the finding of an
  2 34 infectious or contagious disease in a population of that
  2 35 animal, and take certain actions to prevent the spread of
  3  1 disease through that animal population. The bill prohibits
  3  2 the importation of Cervidae carcasses into the state from any
  3  3 state, province, or country where chronic wasting disease has
  3  4 been confirmed within either captive or wild cervid populations
  3  5 except under certain conditions.
  3  6    A person who transports a Cervidae carcass into the state
  3  7 in violation of the provisions of the bill shall be assessed a
  3  8 minimum $100 fine.
