 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to the services provided under Medicaid
  2    home and community=based services waivers, and including
  3    effective date provisions.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2198SS (2) 87
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  MEDICAID HOME AND COMMUNITY=BASED SERVICES
  1  2 WAIVER SERVICES ==== HOME DELIVERED MEALS.  The department of
  1  3 human services shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 17A to
  1  4 include home delivered meals as a  service available under all
  1  5 home and community=based services waivers. If necessary, the
  1  6 department of human services shall request federal approval
  1  7 of a medical assistance state plan amendment or waiver to
  1  8 implement this section effective July 1, 2017.
  1  9    Sec. 2.  EFFECTIVE UPON ENACTMENT AND CONTINGENT
  1 10 IMPLEMENTATION.  This Act, being deemed of immediate
  1 11 importance, takes effect upon enactment.  However, if federal
  1 12 approval is necessary, the department of human services shall
  1 13 implement this Act effective July 1, 2017, contingent upon
  1 14 receipt of federal approval of the state plan amendment or
  1 15 waiver request submitted under this Act.
  1 16                           EXPLANATION
  1 17 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 18 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 19    This bill directs the department of human services (DHS)
  1 20 to adopt administrative rules to include home delivered meals
  1 21 as a  service available under all home and community=based
  1 22 services waivers.  If necessary, DHS shall request federal
  1 23 approval of a medical assistance state plan amendment or
  1 24 waiver to implement the bill effective July 1, 2017. The bill
  1 25 takes effect upon enactment. However, if federal approval is
  1 26 necessary, implementation of the bill effective July 1, 2017,
  1 27 is contingent upon receipt of such federal approval.
