  2 25 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 26 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 27    This bill in part amends provisions in Code chapter 459 which
  2 28 authorize the department of natural resources to issue permits
  2 29 for the construction or expansion of structures associated with
  2 30 a confinement feeding operation.
  2 31    The bill provides that a county may adopt a confinement
  2 32 feeding operations siting ordinance which allows a county board
  2 33 of supervisors to approve the site where the construction is
  2 34 to occur.  The board may place conditions on site approval if
  2 35 the conditions relate to the construction or operation of the
  3  1 confinement feeding operation or to manure application.
  3  2    The bill provides that the county may adopt the ordinance
  3  3 notwithstanding provisions in Code section 331.304A which
  3  4 prohibits a county from adopting legislation that regulates
  3  5 animal agriculture and Code section 335.2 which prohibits a
  3  6 county from adopting an ordinance that provides for the zoning
  3  7 of farm structures.

  2 25 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with

  2 27    This bill in part amends provisions in Code chapter 459 which
  2 28 authorize the department of natural resources to issue permits
  2 29 for the construction or expansion of structures associated with
  2 30 a confinement feeding operation.
  2 31    The bill provides that a county may adopt a confinement
  2 32 feeding operations siting ordinance which allows a county board
  2 33 of supervisors to approve the site where the construction is
  2 34 to occur.  The board may place conditions on site approval if
  2 35 the conditions relate to the construction or operation of the
  3  1 confinement feeding operation or to manure application.
  3  2    The bill provides that the county may adopt the ordinance
  3  3 notwithstanding provisions in Code section 331.304A which
  3  4 prohibits a county from adopting legislation that regulates
  3  5 animal agriculture and Code section 335.2 which prohibits a
  3  6 county from adopting an ordinance that provides for the zoning
  3  7 of farm structures.
