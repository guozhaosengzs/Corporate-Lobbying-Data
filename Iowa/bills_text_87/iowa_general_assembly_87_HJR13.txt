 HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION

  1 A Joint Resolution proposing an amendment to the Constitution
  2    of the State of Iowa relating to the right of the people to
  3    acquire, keep, possess, transport, carry, transfer, and use
  4    arms.
  5 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 1397YH (5) 87
    jm/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  The following amendment to the Constitution of
  1  2 the State of Iowa is proposed:
  1  3    Article I of the Constitution of the State of Iowa is amended
  1  4 by adding the following new section:
  1  5 Right to acquire, keep, possess, transport, carry, transfer,
  1  6 and use arms.SEC. 1A.  The right of the people to keep and
  1  7 bear arms shall not be infringed.  The sovereign state of Iowa
  1  8 affirms and recognizes the fundamental right of the people to
  1  9 acquire, keep, possess, transport, carry, transfer, and use
  1 10 arms for all legitimate purposes.  Any and all restrictions of
  1 11 this right shall be subject to strict scrutiny.
  1 12    Sec. 2.  REFERRAL AND PUBLICATION.  The foregoing proposed
  1 13 amendment to the Constitution of the State of Iowa is referred
  1 14 to the general assembly to be chosen at the next general
  1 15 election for members of the general assembly and the secretary
  1 16 of state is directed to cause the same to be published for
  1 17 three consecutive months previous to the date of that election
  1 18 as provided by law.
  1 19                           EXPLANATION
  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    This joint resolution proposes an amendment to the
  1 23 Constitution of the State of Iowa providing that the right of
  1 24 the people to keep and bear arms shall not be infringed.  The
  1 25 sovereign state of Iowa affirms and recognizes the fundamental
  1 26 right of the people to acquire, keep, possess, transport,
  1 27 carry, transfer, and use arms for all legitimate purposes.  Any
  1 28 and all restrictions of this right shall be subject to strict
  1 29 scrutiny.
  1 30    The joint resolution, if adopted, would be referred to the
  1 31 next general assembly for adoption a second time before being
  1 32 submitted to the electorate for ratification.
