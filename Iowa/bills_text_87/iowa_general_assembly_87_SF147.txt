  1 27 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 28 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 29    Current law requires the commissioner of public safety
  1 30 to maintain a permanent record of all valid permits to carry
  1 31 weapons and of current permit revocations.  The bill provides
  1 32 that, notwithstanding any other law or rule to the contrary,
  1 33 the commissioner of public safety and any issuing officer
  1 34 (county sheriff) shall keep confidential any information that
  1 35 would personally identify holders of nonprofessional permits
  2  1 to carry weapons and permits to acquire pistols or revolvers,
  2  2 including but not limited to the name, social security number,
  2  3 date of birth, driver's license or other identification number,
  2  4 and residential or business address of the permit holder
  2  5 unless such confidentiality is waived in writing by the permit
  2  6 holder.  The bill does not prohibit the release of statistical
  2  7 information relating to the issuance, denial, revocation, or
  2  8 administration of nonprofessional permits to carry weapons and
  2  9 permits to acquire pistols or revolvers if such information
  2 10 does not reveal the identity of any individual permit holder,
  2 11 or the release of information to any law enforcement agency,
  2 12 or an employee or agent thereof, when necessary for an
  2 13 investigation of a possible violation of law or for conducting
  2 14 a lawfully authorized background investigation.
