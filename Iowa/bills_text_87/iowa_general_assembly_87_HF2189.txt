  1  3    a.  An otherwise eligible individual shall not be denied
  1  4 benefits for any week because the individual is in training

  1  9 this section relating to availability for work, and an active
  1 10 search for work or the provision of section 96.5, subsection
  1 11 3, relating to failure to apply for or a refusal to accept
  1 12 suitable work. However, an employer's account shall not be
  1 13 charged with benefits so paid.
