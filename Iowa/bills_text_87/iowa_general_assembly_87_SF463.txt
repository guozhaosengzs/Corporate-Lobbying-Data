  1 28 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 29 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 30    This bill provides that if the director of the department of
  1 31 administrative services establishes a tax=sheltered investment
  1 32 program under section 403(b) of the Internal Revenue Code, the
  1 33 program shall include investment vehicles from any insurance
  1 34 company or investment company recommended for inclusion in
  1 35 the program by a licensed insurance producer or registered
  2  1 securities agent or investment advisor representative.  The
  2  2 bill provides that to be eligible for inclusion in the
  2  3 program, the insurance company shall have filed with, and had
  2  4 the company's contract and forms approved by, the insurance
  2  5 division of the department of commerce, and an investment
  2  6 company shall be registered with the federal securities
  2  7 and exchange commission.  In addition, the bill directs the
  2  8 director of the department of administrative services to
  2  9 require each insurance company and investment company in the
  2 10 program to utilize a common remitter and to limit the total
  2 11 number of companies in the plan to no more than 30.
