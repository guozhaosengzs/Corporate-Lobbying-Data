  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    This bill relates to peace officers, reserve peace
  1 23 officers, and federal officers going armed with, carrying, or
  1 24 transporting a firearm on school grounds.
  1 25    The bill specifies that a peace officer or reserve peace
  1 26 officer including a peace officer or reserve peace officer
  1 27 who has not been certified, and a federal officer when the
  1 28 officer's employment requires going armed, may go armed with,
  1 29 carry, or transport a firearm on school grounds, whether or not
  1 30 the officer is acting in the performance of official duties.
  1 31    A person who goes armed with, carries, or transports a
  1 32 firearm on the grounds of a school in violation of Code section
  1 33 724.4B commits a class "D" felony. A class "D" felony is
  1 34 punishable by confinement for no more than five years and a
  1 35 fine of at least $750 but not more than $7,500.
