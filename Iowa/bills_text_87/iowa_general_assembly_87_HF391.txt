  1 22                           EXPLANATION
  1 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 25    This bill relates to the administration of the state forest
  1 26 nurseries.  The bill allows the department of natural resources
  1 27 to annually review market conditions and the expenditures
  1 28 and revenues of the state forest nurseries and to establish
  1 29 minimum ordering quantities and a range of prices for plant
  1 30 material grown at the nurseries.  The bill also authorizes the
  1 31 department to sell barerooted plants to private nurseries for
  1 32 resale.
  1 33    Currently, administrative rules adopted by the natural
  1 34 resource commission specify ordering quantities and prices
  1 35 for plants sold by the nurseries.  The rules also specify
  2  1 that plants can only be sold to private landowners and public
  2  2 agencies and that barerooted plants purchased cannot be resold.
  2  3 The bill rescinds all rules related to the sale of nursery
  2  4 stock to the public.
  2  5    The bill also repeals a provision in the Iowa Acts that
  2  6 requires the commission to adopt rules establishing prices for
  2  7 plant material grown at the state forest nurseries.
  2  8    The bill may cause a state agency or political subdivision to
  2  9 offer for sale to the public a service or product that competes
  2 10 with private enterprise.
