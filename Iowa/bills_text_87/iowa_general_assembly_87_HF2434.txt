                                     RESOURCES

                                 (SUCCESSOR TO HF 2289)

 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act to ensure appropriate discharge planning and
  2    post=hospital services for a patient discharged to the
  3    patient's residence.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5912HV (2) 87
    pf/rh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  DISCHARGE PLANNING == POST=HOSPITAL SERVICES
  1  2 AND SUPPORTS.  It is the intent of the general assembly that a
  1  3 patient discharged from a licensed hospital to the patient's
  1  4 residence be provided with appropriate discharge planning to
  1  5 ensure that necessary post=hospital services and supports are
  1  6 in place for a successful transition to the patient's residence
  1  7 and for implementation of the discharge plan.
  1  8                           EXPLANATION
  1  9 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 10 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 11    This bill provides that it is the intent of the general
  1 12 assembly that a patient discharged from a licensed hospital to
  1 13 the patient's residence be provided with appropriate discharge
  1 14 planning to ensure that necessary post=hospital services and
  1 15 supports are in place for a successful transition to the
  1 16 patient's residence and for implementation of the discharge
  1 17 plan.
