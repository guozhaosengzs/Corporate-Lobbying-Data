  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    This bill provides for sanctions for the filing of frivolous
  1 27 actions in certain circumstances.
  1 28    The bill provides that after a petition commencing an action
  1 29 is filed, the court must review the petition within 15 days
  1 30 of filing. If the court determines that the petition, on
  1 31 its face, appears to be frivolous, the court must stay the
  1 32 proceedings until the party commencing the action furnishes an
  1 33 undertaking secured by cash or approved sureties to pay all
  1 34 costs resulting to an opposing party including a reasonable
  1 35 attorney fee.
  2  1 The bill provides that if the party commencing the action
  2  2 was required to furnish an undertaking in order to proceed with
  2  3 the lawsuit and an opposing party prevails, the court shall
  2  4 award the opposing party all costs, including a reasonable
  2  5 attorney fee, if the court determines that the action was in
  2  6 fact frivolous.
