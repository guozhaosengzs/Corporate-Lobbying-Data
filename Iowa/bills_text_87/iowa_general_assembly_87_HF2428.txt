PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  MEDICAID DEMONSTRATION WAIVER REQUEST ==== WORK
  1  2 REQUIREMENTS FOR IOWA HEALTH AND WELLNESS PLAN MEMBERS.  The
  1  3 department of human services shall request federal approval
  1  4 from the centers for Medicare and Medicaid services of the
  1  5 United States department of health and human services (CMS)
  1  6 for a section 1115 demonstration waiver to require that as
  1  7 a condition of eligibility, Iowa health and wellness plan
  1  8 members participate in work activity. The department shall
  1  9 report receipt of such approval to the chairpersons and ranking
  1 10 members of the appropriations committees of the senate and
  1 11 house of representatives, the co=chairpersons and ranking
  1 12 members of the joint appropriations subcommittee on health and
  1 13 human services, and the legislative services agency.
  1 14                           EXPLANATION
  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    This bill requires the department of human services (DHS)
  1 18 to request federal approval from the centers for Medicare
  1 19 and Medicaid services of the United States department of
  1 20 health and human services (CMS) for a waiver to require that
  1 21 as a condition of eligibility, Iowa health and wellness plan
  1 22 members participate in work activity.  DHS is required to
  1 23 report receipt of federal approval to the chairpersons and
  1 24 ranking members of the appropriations committees of the senate
  1 25 and house of representatives, the co=chairpersons and ranking
  1 26 members of the joint appropriations subcommittee on health and
  1 27 human services, and the legislative services agency.
