  1  9                           EXPLANATION
  1 10 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 11 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 12    Current law prohibits a person from permitting a vehicle to
  1 13 stand unattended without first stopping the engine, or without
  1 14 effectively setting the brake and turning the front wheels to
  1 15 the curb or side of the highway when the vehicle is standing
  1 16 upon any perceptible grade. A violation of this provision is
  1 17 punishable by a scheduled fine of $20.
  1 18    This bill eliminates the prohibition on permitting a motor
  1 19 vehicle to stand unattended without first stopping the engine.
