  2 34                           EXPLANATION
  2 35 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3  1 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3  2    This bill relates to the court costs the clerk of probate
  3  3 court charges and collects in connection with probate matters.
  3  4    Under current law, Code section 633.31(2)(k) provides a
  3  5 sliding scale fee for services performed in connection with the
  3  6 settlement of an estate based upon the value of the decedent's
  3  7 estate. The bill provides that joint tenancy property,
  3  8 property transfers made during the person's lifetime, and
  3  9 assets payable to beneficiaries are to be excluded from the
  3 10 value of the decedent's estate for purposes of determining the
  3 11 fee, described as court costs in the bill, unless the proceeds
  3 12 of such assets are payable to the estate.
  3 13    Additionally, the bill provides that the court costs due
  3 14 to the clerk pursuant to Code section 633.31(2)(k) shall not
  3 15 be charged on any property transferred to an estate from a
  3 16 conservatorship that has been administered in Iowa and for
  3 17 which court costs have been assessed and paid.
  3 18    The bill applies to estates of decedents dying on or after
  3 19 January 1, 2018.
