  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    This bill prohibits a person from operating a motorcycle or
  1 27 motorized bicycle on a highway if the operator or any passenger
  1 28 is not wearing a safety helmet. The bill also prohibits a
  1 29 passenger from riding on a motorcycle or motorized bicycle
  1 30 if the operator or another passenger is not wearing a safety
  1 31 helmet. The safety helmet must be in compliance with federal
  1 32 regulations and must fit the person's head securely and be
  1 33 fastened with helmet straps.  The bill directs the department
  1 34 of transportation to establish guidelines for motorcycle safety
  1 35 helmets and to maintain and publish a list of approved helmets.
  2  1 The bill provides that a violation of this provision is a
  2  2 simple misdemeanor punishable by a scheduled fine of $35.
