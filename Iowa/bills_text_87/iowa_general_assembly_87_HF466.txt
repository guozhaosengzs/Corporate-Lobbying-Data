  2 34                           EXPLANATION
  2 35 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3  1 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3  2    This bill relates to continuation of or reenrollment of
  3  3 certain dependents of enrollees in group, public employee,
  3  4 individual, and health maintenance organization health care
  3  5 coverage.  The bill provides that a child of an enrollee who
  3  6 so elects can continue or reenroll in the parent's health care
  3  7 coverage until the date the child attains the age of 26.  This
  3  8 provision is consistent with federal law concerning coverage
  3  9 of adult children.
  3 10    The bill deletes a provision that requires such health
  3 11 care coverage to permit an unmarried child of an enrollee to
  3 12 continue or reenroll in such coverage until the child ceases to
  3 13 be a resident of this state or attains the age of 25 years old,
  3 14 whichever occurs first, or so long as the unmarried child is a
  3 15 full=time student in an accredited institution of postsecondary
  3 16 education.
  3 17    The bill is effective upon enactment. The provisions of
  3 18 the bill are applicable to policies, contracts, or plans
  3 19 of accident or health care coverage delivered, issued for
  3 20 delivery, continued, or renewed in this state, on or after
  3 21 January 1, 2018.
