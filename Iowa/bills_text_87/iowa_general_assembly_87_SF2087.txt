 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act requiring a study relative to providing portable
  2    benefits for nontraditional workers.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5261XS (3) 87
    ec/rn

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  DEPARTMENT OF WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT STUDY  ==
  1  2 PORTABLE BENEFITS FOR NONTRADITIONAL WORKERS.  The department
  1  3 of workforce development, in consultation with the Iowa
  1  4 insurance commissioner, shall conduct a study assessing the
  1  5 feasibility of establishing a portable benefits program for
  1  6 workers not classified as employees by their employer and
  1  7 who are therefore generally ineligible for health care and
  1  8 pension=related benefits otherwise provided to employees.  The
  1  9 study shall consider who should administer a portable benefits
  1 10 program, what benefits and what level of benefits to provide
  1 11 in the program, and a method of financing the benefits.  The
  1 12 department shall submit a written report regarding the results
  1 13 of the study, including its findings and recommendations, to
  1 14 the general assembly by November 15, 2018.
  1 15                           EXPLANATION
  1 16 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 17 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 18    This bill requires the department of workforce development,
  1 19 in consultation with the Iowa insurance commissioner, to
  1 20 conduct a study assessing the feasibility of establishing a
  1 21 portable benefits program for nontraditional workers.  The
  1 22 department shall submit a report, including its findings and
  1 23 recommendations, to the general assembly by November 15, 2018.
       LSB 5261XS (3) 87
