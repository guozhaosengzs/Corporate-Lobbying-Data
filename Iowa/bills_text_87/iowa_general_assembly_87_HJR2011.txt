 HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION

  1 A Joint Resolution authorizing the temporary sale of
  2    merchandise at a toy benefit for Iowa children on the state
  3    capitol complex grounds.
  4 WHEREAS, A Brotherhood Aimed Towards Education (ABATE) of
  5 Iowa District 4 holds a motorcycle rally toy run in October of
  6 each year to collect toys for Iowa children; and
  7 WHEREAS, all donations from this toy run benefit Iowa
  8 children; and
  9 WHEREAS, ABATE of Iowa wishes to sell commemorative
 10 t=shirts, sweatshirts, lapel pins, and patches for the further
 11 benefit of Iowa children and to defray the costs of a police
 12 escort; and
 13 WHEREAS, ABATE of Iowa District 4 plans to hold a toy run on
 14 or around October 14, 2018; and
 15 WHEREAS, because 11 IAC 100.4(4) and 11 IAC 100.5(2)
 16 prohibit sales to state employees or to the public on the state
 17 capitol complex grounds without prior approval, ABATE of Iowa
 18 District 4 may not be permitted to sell commemorative t=shirts,
 19 sweatshirts, lapel pins, and patches for the benefit of Iowa
 20 children during its toy run; NOW THEREFORE,
 21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 6231HV (1) 87
    ss/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.    Notwithstanding the prior approval requirements
  1  2 of 11 IAC 100.4(4) and 11 IAC 100.5(2), the department of
  1  3 administrative services shall permit ABATE of Iowa District
  1  4 4 to sell commemorative t=shirts, sweatshirts, lapel pins,
  1  5 and patches on the state capitol complex grounds during the
  1  6 toy run held on or around October 14, 2018, provided that
  1  7 ABATE of Iowa District 4 shall first provide the department of
  1  8 administrative services with a copy of an Iowa sales tax permit
  1  9 for the location of the sales, or proof of application for such
  1 10 a permit.
  1 11                           EXPLANATION
  1 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 13 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 14    This joint resolution authorizes ABATE of Iowa District
  1 15 4 to sell commemorative t=shirts, sweatshirts, lapel pins,
  1 16 and patches on the state capitol complex grounds during a
  1 17 motorcycle rally toy run hosted by ABATE of Iowa District 4
  1 18 on or around October 14, 2018, without first receiving prior
  1 19 approval from the department of administrative services but
  1 20 subject to proof of an applicable Iowa sales tax permit.
