  1 26                           EXPLANATION
  1 27 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 28 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 29    This bill requires the natural resource commission to adopt
  1 30 rules allowing the use of straight wall cartridge rifles to
  1 31 hunt deer during any established firearm deer hunting season
  1 32 except early muzzleloader season. The straight wall cartridge
  1 33 rifles allowed must be of the same caliber and use the same
  1 34 straight wall ammunition as is allowed for use in a pistol
  1 35 or revolver for hunting deer. In addition, the commission
  2  1 is required to adopt rules allowing the use of straight wall
  2  2 ammunition in a straight wall cartridge rifle that meets
  2  3 ballistics specifications similar to the requirements for
  2  4 straight wall ammunition allowed for use in a pistol or
  2  5 revolver for hunting deer.
  2  6    A violation of the bill's provisions is punishable with a
  2  7 scheduled fine of $100.
