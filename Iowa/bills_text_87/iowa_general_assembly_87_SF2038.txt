  1 22                           EXPLANATION
  1 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 25    This bill repeals the state inheritance tax and the state
  1 26 qualified use inheritance tax effective July 1, 2018, for
  1 27 property of estates of decedents dying on or after July 1,
  1 28 2018.  Inheritance tax will not be imposed on any property in
  1 29 the event of the death of an individual on or after July 1,
  1 30 2018.
  1 31    The bill repeals Code chapters 450 (inheritance tax) and
  1 32 450B (qualified use inheritance tax) effective July 1, 2028,
  1 33 and directs the Code editor to correct references in the Code
  1 34 and the Iowa Acts to those Code chapters and to the inheritance
  1 35 tax and qualified use inheritance tax, effective July 1, 2028.
