  2 12                           EXPLANATION
  2 13 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 14 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 15    This bill modifies the requirements for enacting the state
  2 16 percent of growth and the categorical state percent of growth
  2 17 under Code section 257.8.
  2 18    Current law requires the state percent of growth and the
  2 19 categorical state percent of growth to be established by
  2 20 statute within 30 days of the submission of the governor's
  2 21 budget in the year preceding the base year. The bill
  2 22 provides that if the statute establishing the state percent
  2 23 of growth for a budget year is not enacted within 30 days of
  2 24 the submission in the year preceding the base year of the
  2 25 governor's budget under Code section 8.21, the state percent of
  2 26 growth for that budget year is 4 percent.  The bill establishes
  2 27 a similar requirement for the failure to enact the statute
  2 28 establishing the categorical state percent of growth for a
  2 29 budget year.
  2 30    The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies to school
  2 31 budget years beginning on or after July 1, 2018.
