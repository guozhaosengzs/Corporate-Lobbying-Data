  1 22 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 23 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 24    Current law requires a corporation constructing or operating
  1 25 a railway to make and construct good, sufficient, and safe
  1 26 crossings and erect appropriate signs at all points where the
  1 27 railway crosses a public road. A railway company neglecting
  1 28 or refusing to comply with these provisions is liable for all
  1 29 damages sustained by reason of such refusal or neglect.
  1 30    This bill provides that a railway company is strictly liable
  1 31 for any damage, injury, or death arising from an accident
  1 32 involving a train and one or more motor vehicles if the
  1 33 accident occurred at any point where the company's railway
  1 34 crosses a public road.
