  1 13                           EXPLANATION
  1 14 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 15 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 16    Under current law, veterans of World War I are entitled
  1 17 to a property tax exemption of $2,778 in taxable value and
  1 18 honorably discharged veterans who served during other specific
  1 19 time periods are entitled to a property tax exemption of $1,852
  1 20 in taxable value. This bill increases the exemption amount for
  1 21 all eligible veterans to $3,700.
  1 22    Under current law, the state provides funding to local
  1 23 governments for the military service property tax exemption
  1 24 and credit up to $6.92 per $1,000 of assessed value of the
  1 25 exempt property. Code section 25B.7 provides that if a
  1 26 state appropriation made to fund a credit or exemption is not
  1 27 sufficient to fully fund the credit or exemption, the political
  1 28 subdivision shall be required to extend to the taxpayer only
  1 29 that portion of the credit or exemption estimated by the
  1 30 department of revenue to be funded by the state appropriation.
  1 31 The requirement for fully funding and the consequences of not
  1 32 fully funding under Code section 25B.7 apply to the military
  1 33 service property tax credit and exemption to the extent of
  1 34 $6.92 per $1,000 of assessed value of the exempt property.
  1 35    The bill applies to property taxes due and payable in fiscal
  2  1 years beginning on or after July 1, 2018.
