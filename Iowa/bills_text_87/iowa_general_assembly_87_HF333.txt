  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    This bill amends certain exceptions to the prohibition
  1 18 against the inspection and copying of vital statistics
  1 19 records in the custody of the county registrar or the state
  1 20 archivist for records of birth; marriage; divorce, dissolution
  1 21 of marriage, or annulment of marriage; and death. The bill
  1 22 reduces, from at least 75 years to at least 50 years, the age of
  1 23 the vital statistics record that may be inspected and copied.
  1 24 The bill also amends the reference to a record of death to
  1 25 eliminate the exclusion relating to records of death of a fetal
  1 26 death thereby allowing records of fetal death to be inspected
  1 27 and copied if the other requirements are met.
