  3  1                           EXPLANATION
  3  2 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3  3 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3  4    This bill relates to the state school foundation program.
  3  5    The bill establishes the state percent of growth and the
  3  6 categorical state percent of growth for the budget year
  3  7 beginning July 1, 2018.  The bill establishes a state percent
  3  8 of growth of 1 percent for the budget year beginning July 1,
  3  9 2018. The state percent of growth is used to calculate the
  3 10 amount of supplemental state aid for a budget year as part of
  3 11 the state school foundation program.
  3 12    The bill also establishes a categorical state percent of
  3 13 growth of 1 percent for the budget year beginning July 1,
  3 14 2018. The categorical state percent of growth is used to
  3 15 calculate the amount of supplemental state aid for each of the
  3 16 categorical funding supplements.
  3 17    Code section 257.16B provides for school district property
  3 18 tax replacement payments. For each budget year beginning on
  3 19 or after July 1, 2017, each school district's property tax
  3 20 replacement payment amount is equal to the school district's
  3 21 weighted enrollment for the budget year multiplied by the
  3 22 difference of the following: (1) the regular program state
  3 23 cost per pupil for the budget year beginning July 1, 2017,
  3 24 multiplied by 100 percent less the regular program foundation
  3 25 base per pupil percentage; and (2) the regular program state
  3 26 cost per pupil for the budget year beginning July 1, 2012,
  3 27 multiplied by 100 percent less the regular program foundation
  3 28 base per pupil percentage. The regular program foundation base
  3 29 per pupil percentage is 87.5 percent.
  3 30    The bill modifies the property tax replacement payment
  3 31 calculation for budget years beginning on or after July 1,
  3 32 2018. For budget years beginning on or after July 1, 2018,
  3 33 each school district's property tax replacement payment amount
  3 34 is equal to the school district's weighted enrollment for the
  3 35 budget year multiplied by the difference of the following: (1)
  4  1 the regular program state cost per pupil for the budget year
  4  2 beginning July 1, 2018, multiplied by 100 percent less the
  4  3 regular program foundation base per pupil percentage; and (2)
  4  4 the regular program state cost per pupil for the budget year
  4  5 beginning July 1, 2012, multiplied by 100 percent less the
  4  6 regular program foundation base per pupil percentage.
  4  7    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
