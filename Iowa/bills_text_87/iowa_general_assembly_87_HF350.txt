  1 22 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 23 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 24    This bill provides that an accredited private institution
  1 25 that is a specialized college offering health professional
  1 26 programs and a regents university that has a college of
  1 27 medicine or which offers medical programs must give priority
  1 28 for admission to applicants who are residents of Iowa. The
  1 29 institutions must publish notice of the priority given to Iowa
  1 30 residents on application for admission forms used for admission
  1 31 to colleges of medicine, health profession programs, and
  1 32 medical programs.
