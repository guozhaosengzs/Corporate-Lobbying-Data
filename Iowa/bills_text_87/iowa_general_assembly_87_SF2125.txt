  1  7 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  8 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  9    Current law allows a person licensed to operate a delayed
  1 10 deposit services business to charge certain fees, charges,
  1 11 and penalties in connection with a delayed deposit services
  1 12 transaction. This bill limits the annual percentage rate
  1 13 applicable to a delayed deposit services transaction to 36
  1 14 percent, as computed pursuant to the federal Truth in Lending
  1 15 Act. A violation of the bill may result in an administrative
  1 16 fine of not more than $5,000 for each violation and the cost of
  1 17 investigation.
