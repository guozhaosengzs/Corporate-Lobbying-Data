  2  3    BILL'S PROVISIONS.  This bill provides that a person who
  2  4 holds an ownership interest in a dairy animal classified
  2  5 as a cow, sheep, or goat is not prohibited from using milk
  2  6 derived from that animal for personal consumption or other
  2  7 personal use.  It amends a number of Code chapters specifically
  2  8 regulating the production and sale of milk, by providing that
  2  9 the provisions of those Code chapters do not apply to such
  2 10 milk.

  2 13 stewardship.  The department regulates the processing of milk
  2 14 and the manufacture of milk or milk products and dairy products
  2 15 under a number of Code chapters, including Code chapter 159
  2 16 which sets forth its general regulatory authority, Code chapter
  2 17 189 which provides for the regulation of agriculture=related
  2 18 products and activities, Code chapter 190 which describes and
  2 19 prohibits the sale of adulterated food, Code chapter 191 which
  2 20 relates to the labeling of food, and Code chapter 192 which
  2 21 relates to milk production and processing, the "Iowa Grade 'A'
  2 22 Milk Inspection Law" (Code section 192.101).
