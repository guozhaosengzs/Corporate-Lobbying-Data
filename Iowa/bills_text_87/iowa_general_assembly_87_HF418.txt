  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    This bill provides that a student who, at any time
  1 23 during the student's course of study in a practitioner
  1 24 preparation program, achieves a score above the 25th percentile
  1 25 nationally on a valid and reliable subject=area=specific,
  1 26 performance=based assessment for preservice teacher candidates,
  1 27 shall be able to use the score to complete the program even
  1 28 if the department of education subsequently recalculates and
  1 29 updates the passing score based on the most recent data on
  1 30 scores achieved nationally on the assessment. In lieu of
  1 31 the assessment requirements, the bill allows a student who
  1 32 is preparing to teach in a hard=to=staff subject area as
  1 33 identified by the department to demonstrate mastery of pedagogy
  1 34 and knowledge of subject area through a student portfolio.
