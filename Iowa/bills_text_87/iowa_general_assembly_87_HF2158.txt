  2  5 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  6 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  7    Under current law, the driver of an authorized emergency
  2  8 vehicle may exercise certain driving privileges relating to
  2  9 parking and directional signs when responding to an emergency
  2 10 call. In addition, certain types of authorized emergency
  2 11 vehicles including fire department vehicles, police vehicles,
  2 12 and ambulances, as well as peace officers riding on police
  2 13 bicycles, may proceed past red stop signals or stop signs
  2 14 and exceed speed limits while exercising necessary safety
  2 15 precautions and using an audible or visual signal.  This bill
  2 16 includes rescue vehicles in the list of authorized emergency
  2 17 vehicles that may ignore red stop signals or stop signs and
  2 18 exceed speed limits in emergency situations.  Under Code
  2 19 section 321.1, a "rescue vehicle" is a motor vehicle which is
  2 20 equipped with rescue, fire, or life support equipment used to
  2 21 assist and rescue persons in emergencies or support emergency
  2 22 personnel in the performance of their duties. The penalty for
  2 23 a violation of the provision remains a scheduled fine of $100.
  2 24    Under current law, privately owned rescue vehicles and
  2 25 certain other privately owned vehicles may be designated by
  2 26 the department of transportation as authorized emergency
  2 27 vehicles. The bill requires an application for a certificate
  2 28 of designation for a rescue vehicle to be accompanied by a
  2 29 letter of approval from the sheriff or emergency management
  2 30 coordinator of the county of the vehicle owner's residence.
  2 31 This provision of the bill does not apply to a rescue vehicle
  2 32 designated as an authorized emergency vehicle prior to July
  2 33 1, 2018, or to any other rescue vehicle owned by a person who
  2 34 owns or has owned a rescue vehicle designated as an authorized
  2 35 emergency vehicle prior to July 1, 2018.
