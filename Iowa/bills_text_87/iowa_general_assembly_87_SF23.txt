  1 26 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 27 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 28    Current law requires the commissioner of public safety
  1 29 to maintain a permanent record of all valid permits to carry
  1 30 weapons and of current permit revocations.  This bill provides
  1 31 that the commissioner of public safety and any issuing officer
  1 32 (county sheriff) shall keep confidential any information that
  1 33 would personally identify holders of nonprofessional permits to
  1 34 carry weapons or of permits to acquire pistols or revolvers,
  1 35 including but not limited to the name, social security number,
  2  1 date of birth, driver's license or other identification number,
  2  2 and residential or business address of the permit holder.  The
  2  3 bill does not prohibit the release of statistical information
  2  4 relating to the issuance, denial, revocation, or administration
  2  5 of nonprofessional permits to carry weapons or of permits to
  2  6 acquire pistols or revolvers if such information does not
  2  7 reveal the identity of any individual permit holder, or the
  2  8 release of information to any law enforcement agency, or an
  2  9 employee or agent thereof, when necessary for an investigation
  2 10 of a possible violation of law or for conducting a lawfully
  2 11 authorized background investigation.
