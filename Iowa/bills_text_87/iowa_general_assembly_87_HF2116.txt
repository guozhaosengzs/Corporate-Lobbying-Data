  1  5    Sec. 2.  EMERGENCY RULES.  The public employment relations
  1  6 board may adopt emergency rules under section 17A.4, subsection
  1  7 3, and section 17A.5, subsection 2, paragraph "b", to implement
  1  8 the provisions of this Act and the rules shall be effective
  1  9 immediately upon filing unless a later date is specified in the

  1 11 also be published as a notice of intended action as provided

  1 13    Sec. 3.  EFFECTIVE DATE.  This Act, being deemed of immediate
  1 14 importance, takes effect upon enactment.
  1 15    Sec. 4.  APPLICABILITY.  This Act applies to collective
  1 16 bargaining procedures pursuant to chapter 20 initiated on or
  1 17 after the effective date of this Act.
  1 18                           EXPLANATION
  1 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 21    This bill designates employees of the department of
  1 22 corrections as public safety employees for the purposes of
  1 23 collective bargaining pursuant to Code chapter 20, the public
  1 24 employment relations Act. Code chapter 20 and Code chapter
  1 25 400 include collective bargaining procedures specifically
  1 26 applicable to public safety employees that are not applicable
  1 27 to other public employees, including procedures relating to
  1 28 the scope of collective bargaining negotiations, arbitration
  1 29 procedures, and certain matters relating to city civil service
  1 30 employment.
  1 31    The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies to
  1 32 collective bargaining procedures pursuant to Code chapter 20
  1 33 initiated on or after the effective date of the bill.
