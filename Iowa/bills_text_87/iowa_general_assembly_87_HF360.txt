 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to the open season for hunting squirrels.
  2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2373HH (3) 87
    av/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  SQUIRREL HUNTING SEASON.  The natural resource
  1  2 commission shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 17A
  1  3 establishing an open season to hunt fox squirrels and gray
  1  4 squirrels from the first Saturday in August through January 31
  1  5 of the succeeding year.
  1  6                           EXPLANATION
  1  7 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  8 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  9    This bill requires the natural resource commission to
  1 10 adopt rules establishing an open season to hunt fox squirrels
  1 11 and gray squirrels from the first Saturday in August through
  1 12 January 31 of the succeeding year.
