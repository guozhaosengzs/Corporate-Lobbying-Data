  2  4 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  5 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  6    This bill relates to energy conservation requirements
  2  7 contained within the state building code or adopted by
  2  8 governmental subdivisions.
  2  9    Code chapter 103A requires new single=family or two=family
  2 10 residential construction commenced on or after July 1, 2008,
  2 11 to comply with energy conservation requirements adopted by
  2 12 the state building code commissioner. "New construction", as
  2 13 defined in the Code chapter, is expressly made inapplicable
  2 14 to building renovations and repairs. The energy conservation
  2 15 requirements adopted by the state building code commissioner
  2 16 supersede and replace any minimum energy conservation
  2 17 requirements adopted by a governmental subdivision.
  2 18    The bill amends Code section 103A.8A to provide that all
  2 19 single=family or two=family residential construction on or
  2 20 after July 1, 2010, shall comply with the energy conservation
  2 21 requirements, extending the requirements to existing residence
  2 22 renovations and repairs.
  2 23    The bill removes the language from Code section 103A.8A that
  2 24 provides that the energy conservation requirements adopted
  2 25 by the state building code commissioner are to supersede any
  2 26 minimum requirements adopted by a governmental subdivision.
  2 27 The bill makes conforming changes consistent with this
  2 28 modification by striking Code section 103A.10(5).
  2 29    The bill provides that if a governmental subdivision adopts
  2 30 minimum energy conservation requirements that are not less
  2 31 restrictive than the 2009 edition of the international energy
  2 32 conservation code, then applicable residential construction
  2 33 must comply with the energy conservation requirements
  2 34 adopted by the governmental subdivision in lieu of the energy
  2 35 conservation requirements adopted by the commissioner.
