  1 21 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 22 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 23    This bill relates to grounds for the termination of parental
  1 24 rights under Code chapter 232 when a child has been adjudicated
  1 25 a child in need of assistance.
  1 26    The bill amends existing grounds for termination of parental
  1 27 rights that each include as an element that the child has been
  1 28 removed from the physical custody of the child's parent for
  1 29 a specified period of time.  The bill amends this element to
  1 30 require that either the child has been removed from or was not
  1 31 placed in the physical custody of the child's parent for a
  1 32 specified period of time.
