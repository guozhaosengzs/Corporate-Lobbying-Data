  1 31 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 32 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 33    This bill relates to an extension granted by the economic
  1 34 development authority for agreements with eligible housing
  1 35 businesses. The authority's program to provide incentives
  2  1 and assistance to eligible housing businesses to build or
  2  2 rehabilitate single=family homes, or multiple dwelling unit
  2  3 buildings, in economic development enterprise zones pursuant to
  2  4 Code section 15E.193B, Code 2014, was terminated in 2014 when
  2  5 Code section 15E.193B was repealed.
  2  6    Under the 2014 law, agreements with eligible housing
  2  7 businesses required the project or rehabilitation to be
  2  8 completed within two years of the date the eligible housing
  2  9 business began the building or rehabilitation. The bill
  2 10 allows an extension to be granted by the economic development
  2 11 authority, as approved by the economic development board,
  2 12 for up to one year from the date the extension is granted
  2 13 for the eligible housing business to complete its building
  2 14 or rehabilitation project. The bill provides that only one
  2 15 extension may be granted and only for a project in a distressed
  2 16 county within a community with a demonstrated need for housing.
  2 17    The bill prohibits the agreement extension from increasing
  2 18 the amount of incentives or the type of incentives originally
  2 19 awarded under the agreement.
