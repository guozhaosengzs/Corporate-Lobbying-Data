  1 16 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 17 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 18    This bill makes a voluntary reporting requirement relating
  1 19 to independent private instruction mandatory, and establishes a
  1 20 notice requirement for the parent, guardian, or legal custodian
  1 21 of a child who places the child under independent private
  1 22 instruction.
  1 23    Currently, the authority responsible for independent private
  1 24 instruction must submit a report relating to the instructors,
  1 25 instruction, and the children placed, to the  superintendent
  1 26 of the school district in which the independent private
  1 27 instruction is provided or to the director of the department of
  1 28 education upon receiving a written request.  The bill requires
  1 29 that the report be provided to the superintendent by September
  1 30 1, annually, and eliminates reference to the director.
  1 31    The bill also requires the parent, guardian, or legal
  1 32 custodian of a child who intends to place the child under
  1 33 independent private instruction for the school year to provide
  1 34 to the superintendent of the school district in which the child
  1 35 resides written notice of such intent by September 1, annually.
