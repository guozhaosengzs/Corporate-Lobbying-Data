  2 11                           EXPLANATION
  2 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 13 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 14    Code section 257.11(5) provides supplementary weighting
  2 15 for school districts and area education agencies that share
  2 16 operational functions. A district that shares with a political
  2 17 subdivision one or more operational functions of a curriculum
  2 18 director or school counselor, or one or more operational
  2 19 functions in the areas of superintendent management, business
  2 20 management, human resources, transportation, or operation and
  2 21 maintenance for at least 20 percent of the school year is
  2 22 assigned a supplementary weighting for each shared operational
  2 23 function. Supplementary weighting under this provision is
  2 24 available for a maximum of five school budget years beginning
  2 25 July 1, 2014, through the budget year beginning July 1, 2019.
  2 26    This bill adds the position of licensed social worker to the
  2 27 category of operational functions that receives supplementary
