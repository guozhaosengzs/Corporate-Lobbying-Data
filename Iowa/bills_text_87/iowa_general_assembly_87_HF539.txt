  3  8                           EXPLANATION
  3  9 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3 10 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3 11    This bill modifies the procedure required to be followed
  3 12 before a person can pursue a private action in district court
  3 13 regarding an unfair or discriminatory practice under Code
  3 14 chapter 216, the Iowa civil rights Act.
  3 15    Under current Code section 216.16, a person pursuing relief
  3 16 from an alleged unfair or discriminatory practice must file a
  3 17 complaint with the Iowa civil rights commission. The person
  3 18 may request an administrative release after a timely filed
  3 19 complaint has been on file with the commission for 60 days.
  3 20    The bill requires a person to file an action for relief in
  3 21 district court within 90 days of issuance of the release by the
  3 22 commission. Correspondingly, the bill also removes the current
  3 23 restriction that the commission cannot issue an administrative
  3 24 release relating to a complaint that has been administratively
  3 25 closed for two years.
