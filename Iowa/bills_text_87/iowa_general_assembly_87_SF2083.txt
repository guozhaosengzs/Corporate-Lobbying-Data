 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to reimbursement of in=network and
  2    out=of=network providers under Medicaid managed care.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5966XS (1) 87
    pf/rh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  MEDICAID MANAGED CARE ==== REIMBURSEMENT OF
  1  2 IN=NETWORK AND OUT=OF=NETWORK PROVIDERS.  The department of
  1  3 human services shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 17A and
  1  4 shall amend all Medicaid managed care contracts to require that
  1  5 beginning July 1, 2018, for each covered service, Medicaid
  1  6 providers that have contracted with a Medicaid managed care
  1  7 organization as an in=network provider be reimbursed at one
  1  8 hundred percent of the fee=for=service reimbursement rate
  1  9 established by the general assembly through the appropriations
  1 10 process for the respective fiscal year, and that Medicaid
  1 11 providers that have not contracted with a Medicaid managed care
  1 12 organization and are considered an out=of=network provider
  1 13 be reimbursed at ninety=five percent of the fee=for=service
  1 14 reimbursement rate established by the general assembly through
  1 15 the appropriations process for the respective fiscal year.
  1 16    Sec. 2.  EFFECTIVE DATE.  This Act, being deemed of immediate
  1 17 importance, takes effect upon enactment.
  1 18                           EXPLANATION
  1 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 21    This bill directs the department of human services (DHS)
  1 22 to adopt rules pursuant to Code chapter 17A and amend all
  1 23 Medicaid managed care contracts to require that beginning July
  1 24 1, 2018, for each covered service,  Medicaid providers that
  1 25 have contracted with a Medicaid managed care organization
  1 26 as an in=network provider be reimbursed at 100 percent of
  1 27 the fee=for=service reimbursement rate established by the
  1 28 general assembly through the appropriations process for the
  1 29 respective fiscal year, and that Medicaid providers that have
  1 30 not contracted with a Medicaid managed care organization and
  1 31 are considered an out=of=network provider be reimbursed at 95
  1 32 percent of the fee=for=service reimbursement rate established
  1 33 by the general assembly through the appropriations process for
  1 34 the respective fiscal year.
  1 35    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
