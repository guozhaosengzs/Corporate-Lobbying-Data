  3 27                           EXPLANATION
  3 28 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3 29 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3 30    This bill concerns city and county hospital boards of
  3 31 trustees.
  3 32    New Code section 66.31 allows a county or city hospital board
  3 33 of trustees to adopt bylaws to establish a process for the
  3 34 removal of a trustee for cause.
  3 35    Code section 347.9, concerning the number and appointment
  4  1 of county hospital board trustees, is amended to authorize the
  4  2 establishment of five or seven=member trustee boards and to
  4  3 provide for four=year terms of office.  Current law provides
  4  4 for only seven=member trustee boards and for a six=year term of
  4  5 office.  The bill establishes the process and initial terms of
  4  6 office for an initial five or seven=member trustee board, and
  4  7 provides for the ability to change a seven=member trustee board
  4  8 to a five=member trustee board.
  4  9    Code section 347.10, concerning vacancies, is amended to
  4 10 provide that the board of trustees may establish more stringent
  4 11 attendance requirements for regular board meetings that can
  4 12 result in a vacancy on the board.
  4 13    Code section 347.11, concerning quorum requirements, is
  4 14 amended to provide that a majority of the members of the board
  4 15 constitute a quorum.
  4 16    Code section 347.13, concerning board of trustee duties, is
  4 17 amended to provide that the board may include term limits for
  4 18 service on the board in the bylaws governing the board.
  4 19    Code section 347A.1, concerning certain county hospitals
  4 20 payable from revenue bonds, is amended to provide for
  4 21 four=year, and not six=year, terms of office.  The bill further
  4 22 provides that a vacancy on the board of trustees can occur
  4 23 if a member is absent for four consecutive regular board
  4 24 meetings without excuse, or fails to comply with more stringent
  4 25 attendance requirements for regular board meetings included in
  4 26 the bylaws governing the board.
  4 27    Code section 392.6, concerning city hospital or health care
  4 28 facility trustees, is amended to provide that the board of
  4 29 trustees may establish more stringent attendance requirements
  4 30 for regular board meetings that can result in a vacancy on the
  4 31 board.
