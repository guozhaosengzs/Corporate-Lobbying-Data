  2 24                           EXPLANATION
  2 25 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 26 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 27    This bill makes jurisdictional changes to small claims
  2 28 court cases. The bill provides that a small claims court case
  2 29 commenced on or after July 1, 2018, shall not involve damages
  2 30 or value in excess of $7,500. Under existing law, a small
  2 31 claims court case shall not involve damages or value in excess
  2 32 of $5,000. The bill further provides that the jurisdictional
  2 33 amount shall revert to $5,000 if a court finds the $7,500
  2 34 amount unconstitutional. By increasing the jurisdictional
  2 35 amount for small claims court the bill expands the jurisdiction
  3  1 of a magistrate or district associate judge to hear and
  3  2 assess judgment on certain actions, including county and city
  3  3 violations.
