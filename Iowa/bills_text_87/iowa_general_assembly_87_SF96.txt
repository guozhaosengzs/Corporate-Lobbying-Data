  2  7                           EXPLANATION
  2  8 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  9 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 10    This bill prohibits a person under 18 years of age from
  2 11 operating a motorcycle or motorized bicycle on the highways
  2 12 unless the person is wearing a safety helmet. The bill also
  2 13 prohibits any person from operating a motorcycle or motorized
  2 14 bicycle on the highways accompanied by a passenger under 18
  2 15 years of age unless the passenger is wearing a safety helmet.
  2 16 The bill provides that a violation of these provisions is a
  2 17 simple misdemeanor punishable by a scheduled fine of $35.
  2 18    In addition, the bill prohibits a person under 18 years
  2 19 of age from operating an all=terrain vehicle, including an
  2 20 off=road motorcycle, on a designated riding area or designated
  2 21 riding trail or on public land or public ice unless the person
  2 22 is wearing a safety helmet. The bill also prohibits any
  2 23 person from operating an all=terrain vehicle, including an
  2 24 off=road motorcycle, on a designated riding area or designated
  2 25 riding trail or on public land or public ice accompanied by a
  2 26 passenger under 18 years of age unless the passenger is wearing
  2 27 a safety helmet. The bill provides that a violation of these
  2 28 provisions is a simple misdemeanor punishable by a scheduled
  2 29 fine of $35.
  2 30    Under the bill, "wearing a safety helmet" means having
  2 31 a safety helmet that complies with the standards and
  2 32 specifications established in the Code of Federal Regulations
  2 33 on the person's head that is fastened with the helmet straps
  2 34 and that is of a size that fits the person's head securely
  2 35 without excessive lateral or vertical movement.
