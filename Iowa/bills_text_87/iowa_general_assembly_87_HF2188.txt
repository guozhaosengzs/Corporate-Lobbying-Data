  2 18                           EXPLANATION
  2 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 21    This bill relates to insurance coverage for a mammogram as
  2 22 recommended by a woman's health care professional.
  2 23    The bill defines "mammogram" as a radiographic image
  2 24 obtained using a procedure, including two=dimensional
  2 25 mammography or three=dimensional mammography, as recommended by
  2 26 a woman's health care professional. The bill does not change
  2 27 the minimum mammography examination coverage requirements. The
  2 28 bill requires, however, that the insurance coverage apply to
  2 29 two=dimensional mammograms and three=dimensional mammograms.
  2 30 The bill also allows a woman's health care professional,
  2 31 defined to include licensed health care practitioners other
  2 32 than a woman's physician, to recommend a mammogram.
  2 33    The bill applies to the classes of third=party payment
  2 34 provider contracts or policies specified in Code section 514C.4
  2 35 that are delivered, issued for delivery, continued, or renewed
  3  1 in this state on or after July 1, 2018.
