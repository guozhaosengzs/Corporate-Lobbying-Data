                                 SENATE FILE                                  SENATE FILE                                  BY  DANIELSON

 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to reimbursement under the Medicaid program
  2    for residential care facilities provided at certain county
  3    facilities.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5774SS (2) 87
    pf/rh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  MEDICAID REIMBURSEMENT == RESIDENTIAL CARE
  1  2 FACILITY SERVICES AT COUNTY FACILITIES.  The department of
  1  3 human services shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 17A and
  1  4 shall amend all Medicaid managed care contracts to provide
  1  5 cost=based reimbursement for one hundred percent of the
  1  6 reasonable cost of services provided to a Medicaid recipient
  1  7 for the residential care facility level of care provided by a
  1  8 county=supported hospital or other county=supported facility.
  1  9                           EXPLANATION
  1 10 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 11 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 12    This bill requires the department of human services to
  1 13 adopt administrative rules and amend managed care contracts to
  1 14 provide for cost=based reimbursement for 100 percent of the
  1 15 reasonable cost of services provided to a  Medicaid recipient
  1 16 for the residential care facility level of care provided by a
  1 17 county=supported hospital or other county=supported facility.
       LSB 5774SS (2) 87
       pf/rh
