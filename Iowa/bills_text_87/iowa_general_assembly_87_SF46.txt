  1  3    1.  Commencing January 1, 2017, the commission shall not
  1  4 issue a license to conduct gambling games on an excursion
  1  5 gambling boat, gambling structure, or at a pari=mutuel
  1  6 racetrack pursuant to this chapter.
  1  7    2.  This section does not affect the validity of a license
  1  8 issued by the commission pursuant to this chapter before
  1  9 January 1, 2017, or the authority of the commission to suspend,
  1 10 revoke, transfer, or renew a license to conduct gambling games
  1 11 issued before January 1, 2017, pursuant to chapter 99D or this
  1 12 chapter.
  1 13    Sec. 2.  EFFECTIVE UPON ENACTMENT.  This Act, being deemed of
  1 14 immediate importance, takes effect upon enactment.
  1 15    Sec. 3.  RETROACTIVE APPLICABILITY.  This Act applies
  1 16 retroactively to January 1, 2017.

  1 23 structure, or at a pari=mutuel racetrack on and after
  1 24 January 1, 2017.  The bill takes effect upon enactment and is
  1 25 retroactively applicable to January 1, 2017.
