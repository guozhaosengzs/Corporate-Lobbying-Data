 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to subacute care facilities licensed by the
  2    department of inspections and appeals.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2382XS (2) 87
    hb/rh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 135G.6, subsection 2, paragraph b, Code
  1  2 2017, is amended by striking the paragraph.
  1  3                           EXPLANATION
  1  4 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  5 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  6    This bill relates to subacute care facilities licensed
  1  7 by the department of inspections and appeals. Under current
  1  8 law, the department of inspections and appeals is required to
  1  9 license a subacute care facility if the department of humans
  1 10 services has submitted a written approval of the facility's
  1 11 application for licensure based upon the process used by the
  1 12 department of human services to identify the best qualified
  1 13 providers and certain additional conditions are met.
  1 14    Under Code section 135G.1, a subacute care facility means
  1 15 an institution, place, building, or agency with restricted
  1 16 means of egress providing subacute mental health services for
  1 17 a period exceeding 24 consecutive hours to persons in need
  1 18 of such services. The bill strikes the provision that the
  1 19 department of human services shall not give approval to an
  1 20 application which would cause the number of publicly funded
  1 21 subacute care facility beds to exceed 75 beds.
       LSB 2382XS (2) 87
       hb/rh
