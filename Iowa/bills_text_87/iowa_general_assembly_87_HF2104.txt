 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to the termination of Medicaid managed care
  2    contracts, and including effective date provisions.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5994YH (4) 87
    pf/rh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  TERMINATION OF MEDICAID MANAGED CARE CONTRACTS
  1  2 ==== ALTERNATIVES FOR HEALTH CARE DELIVERY AND PAYMENT REFORM.
  1  3    1.  The department of human services shall, upon the
  1  4 effective date of this Act, terminate the contracts executed
  1  5 with managed care organizations to administer the Iowa
  1  6 high quality health care initiative, in accordance with the
  1  7 termination provisions of the contract.
  1  8    2.  The department shall continue to pursue other
  1  9 initiatives to realign the health care delivery system and
  1 10 provide holistic, integrated, patient=centered care while
  1 11 moving toward a value=based model of payment reform, including
  1 12 but not limited to the healthiest state initiative, the Iowa
  1 13 health and wellness plan created pursuant to chapter 249N, the
  1 14 state innovation models initiative utilizing accountable care
  1 15 organizations, and integrated health homes.
  1 16    Sec. 2.  EFFECTIVE UPON ENACTMENT.  This Act, being deemed of
  1 17 immediate importance, takes effect upon enactment.
  1 18                           EXPLANATION
  1 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 21    This bill directs the department of human services
  1 22 to terminate the contracts executed with managed care
  1 23 organizations to administer the Iowa high quality health care
  1 24 initiative, in accordance with the termination provisions of
  1 25 the contract upon the effective date of the Act.
  1 26    The bill directs the department to continue to pursue
  1 27 initiatives to realign the health care delivery system and
  1 28 provide holistic, integrated, patient=centered care while
  1 29 moving toward a value=based model of payment, including but not
  1 30 limited to the healthiest state initiative, the Iowa health
  1 31 and wellness plan created pursuant to Code chapter 249N, the
  1 32 state innovation models initiative utilizing accountable care
  1 33 organizations, and integrated health homes.
  1 34    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
