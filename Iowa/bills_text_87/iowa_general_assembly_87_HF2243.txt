  1  1    Section 1.    1  1    Section 1.    1  2 and instructors == annual report.
  1  3    1.  The board of directors of each school district shall
  1  4 compile statistics and data relating to physical education
  1  5 classes and instruction offered by the school district as a
  1  6 whole and by attendance center, as appropriate, in a format
  1  7 as determined by the department of education, which shall be
  1  8 submitted annually to the department as a component of the
  1  9 comprehensive school improvement plan submitted pursuant to
  1 10 section 256.7, subsection 21, paragraph "a".  The department
  1 11 shall make the information submitted in the reports publicly
  1 12 available and searchable by school district and attendance
  1 13 center on the department's internet site.
  1 14    2.  Each school district report required under subsection 1
  1 15 shall include all of the following information:
  1 16    a.  The number of physical education classes offered at
  1 17 each attendance center in the school district, detailed by the
  1 18 number of days, classes, and minutes offered each week by each
  1 19 attendance center.
  1 20    b.  The ratio of students enrolled in physical education
  1 21 classes in the school district compared to the overall
  1 22 enrollment.
  1 23    c.  The average physical education class size at each
  1 24 attendance center in the school district.
  1 25    d.  The number of practitioners, as defined in section 272.1,
  1 26 in the school district who teach physical education and who are
  1 27 endorsed to teach physical education, arranged by credential
  1 28 and grade levels taught.
  1 29    e.  Whether each attendance center in the school district has
  1 30 the appropriate equipment and adequate facilities for students
  1 31 to engage in the amount of physical activity required under
  1 32 section 256.11 or required under rules adopted by the state
  1 33 board of education to implement section 256.11.
  1 34    f.  Whether the school district permits modifications or
  1 35 accommodations that allow physical education classes to meet
  2  1 the needs of students with disabilities.
  2  2    g.  Whether the school district has a policy that allows
  2  3 teachers or administrators in the school district to withhold
  2  4 physical activity from a student as punishment.
