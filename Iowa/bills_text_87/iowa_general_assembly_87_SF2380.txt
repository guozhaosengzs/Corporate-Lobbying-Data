  2  8                           EXPLANATION
  2  9 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 10 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 11    This bill relates to energy conservation requirements
  2 12 contained within the state building code or adopted by
  2 13 governmental subdivisions.
  2 14    Code section 103A.8A requires new single=family or
  2 15 two=family residential construction commenced on or after
  2 16 July 1, 2008, to comply with energy conservation requirements
  2 17 adopted by the state building code commissioner and approved
  2 18 by the state building code advisory council. The energy
  2 19 conservation requirements adopted by the commissioner and
  2 20 approved by the council supersede and replace any minimum
  2 21 energy conservation requirements adopted by a governmental
  2 22 subdivision.
  2 23    The bill amends Code section 103A.8A to provide that the
  2 24 energy conservation requirements shall be adopted by the
  2 25 commissioner and approved by the council no later than July
  2 26 1, 2018, and shall be based upon the 2018 edition of the
  2 27 international energy conservation code. In approving the
  2 28 requirements, the council may make modifications by a majority
  2 29 vote of the members. The bill provides that new residential
  2 30 construction commenced on or after July 1, 2018, shall comply
  2 31 with the energy conservation requirements, extending the
  2 32 applicability of such requirements to any type of residential
  2 33 building.
  2 34    The bill removes language from Code section 103A.8A that
  2 35 provides that the energy conservation requirements adopted by
  3  1 the commissioner and approved by the council are to supersede
  3  2 any minimum requirements adopted by a governmental subdivision.
  3  3 The bill makes conforming changes consistent with this
  3  4 modification in Code section 103A.10(5).
  3  5    The bill provides that a governmental subdivision may, no
  3  6 later than December 21, 2018, adopt minimum energy conservation
  3  7 requirements for residential construction that are not less
  3  8 restrictive than the minimum energy conservation requirements
  3  9 adopted by the commissioner and approved by the council. If
  3 10 a governmental subdivision adopts such minimum requirements,
  3 11 then applicable residential construction must comply with the
  3 12 requirements adopted by the governmental subdivision.
  3 13    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
