  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    This bill relates to the criminal offense of unauthorized
  1 23 computer access.
  1 24    The bill increases the criminal penalties for committing
  1 25 unauthorized computer access.
  1 26    The bill increases the criminal penalty from an aggravated
  1 27 misdemeanor to a class "C" felony if computer data is accessed
  1 28 that contains a confidential record, a trade secret, or
  1 29 operational or support data of a public utility, rural water
  1 30 district, municipal utility, or public airport.
  1 31    The bill increases the criminal penalty from a serious
  1 32 misdemeanor to a class "D" felony if computer data is copied,
  1 33 altered, or deleted.
  1 34    The bill increases the criminal penalty from a simple
  1 35 misdemeanor to an aggravated misdemeanor for any access which
  2  1 is not a felony under the bill.
  2  2    A person who violates the bill is excluded from providing a
  2  3 DNA sample under Code chapter 81.
  2  4    A class "C" felony is punishable by confinement for no more
  2  5 than 10 years and a fine of at least $1,000 but not more than
  2  6 $10,000.  A class "D" felony is punishable by confinement for
  2  7 no more than five years and a fine of at least $750 but not
  2  8 more than $7,500.  An aggravated misdemeanor is punishable
  2  9 by confinement for no more than two years and a fine of at
  2 10 least $625 but not more than $6,250.  A simple misdemeanor is
  2 11 punishable by confinement for no more than 30 days or a fine of
  2 12 at least $65 but not more than $625 or by both.
