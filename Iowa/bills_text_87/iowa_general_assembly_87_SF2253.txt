  1  3    The board of directors of a school district shall establish
  1  4 a security plan for individual school buildings in the school
  1  5 district in which students are educated no later than June 30,
  1  6 2019. The plan shall include but not be limited to responses
  1  7 to active shooter scenarios and natural disasters. The
  1  8 board shall consider any recommendations of the department
  1  9 of education relating to school security planning and shall
  1 10 consult with local law enforcement agencies and the local
  1 11 emergency management coordinator in the development of the
  1 12 plan. The board shall ensure that the plan is distributed to
  1 13 the parents or guardians of all students enrolled in the school
  1 14 district at least once per school year. Such distribution may
  1 15 be electronic. The board shall ensure that the plan is shared
  1 16 with students enrolled in the school district at a school
  1 17 assembly at least once per school year.
  1 18                           EXPLANATION
  1 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 21    This bill requires the board of directors of a school
  1 22 district to establish a security plan for individual school
  1 23 buildings in the school district in which students are educated
  1 24 no later than June 30, 2019. The plan shall include but
  1 25 not be limited to responses to active shooter scenarios and
  1 26 natural disasters. The bill requires the board to consider
  1 27 any recommendations of the department of education relating to
  1 28 school security planning and consult with local law enforcement
  1 29 agencies and the local emergency management coordinator in the
  1 30 development of the plan. The bill requires the board to ensure
  1 31 that the plan is distributed to the parents or guardians of
  1 32 all students enrolled in the school district at least once per
  1 33 school year. The bill requires the board to ensure that the
  1 34 plan is shared with students enrolled in the school district at
  1 35 a school assembly at least once per school year.
