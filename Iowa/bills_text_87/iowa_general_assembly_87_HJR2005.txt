 HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION

  1 A Joint Resolution proposing an amendment to the Constitution
  2    of the State of Iowa limiting years of service for members
  3    of the general assembly.
  4 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5807YH (3) 87
    ss/jh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  The following amendment to the Constitution of
  1  2 the State of Iowa is proposed:
  1  3    1.  Section 3 of Article III of the Constitution of the State
  1  4 of Iowa is repealed and the following adopted in lieu thereof:
  1  5 Representatives.SEC. 3.  The members of the house of
  1  6 representatives shall be chosen every second year, by the
  1  7 qualified electors of their respective districts, and their
  1  8 term of office shall commence on the first day of January next
  1  9 after their election, and continue two years, and until their
  1 10 successors are elected and qualified. A person shall not be
  1 11 elected for a term as representative if the term would result
  1 12 in the person serving more than a total of sixteen years in the
  1 13 general assembly. This limitation on years of service applies
  1 14 to terms of office beginning on or after January 1, 2023, and
  1 15 for years of service occurring on or after January 1, 2023.
  1 16    2.  Section 5 of Article III of the Constitution of the State
  1 17 of Iowa is repealed and the following adopted in lieu thereof:
  1 18 Senators ==== qualifications.SEC. 5.  Senators shall be
  1 19 chosen for the term of four years, at the same time and place as
  1 20 representatives; they shall be twenty=five years of age, and
  1 21 possess the qualifications of representatives as to residence
  1 22 and citizenship. A person shall not be elected for a term as
  1 23 senator if the term would result in the person serving more
  1 24 than a total of sixteen years in the general assembly. This
  1 25 limitation on terms of service applies to terms of office
  1 26 beginning on or after January 1, 2023, and for years of service
  1 27 occurring on or after January 1, 2023.
  1 28    Sec. 2.  REFERRAL AND PUBLICATION.  The foregoing amendment
  1 29 to the Constitution of the State of Iowa is referred to the
  1 30 general assembly to be chosen at the next general election
  1 31 for members of the general assembly, and the secretary of
  1 32 state is directed to cause the same to be published for three
  1 33 consecutive months previous to the date of that election as
  1 34 provided by law.
  1 35                           EXPLANATION
  2  1 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  2 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  3    This joint resolution proposes an amendment to the
  2  4 Constitution of the State of Iowa limiting years of service
  2  5 for persons elected to the general assembly. The amendment
  2  6 provides that a person shall not be elected for a term as a
  2  7 representative or senator if the term would result in the
  2  8 person serving more than 16 years in the general assembly. The
  2  9 amendment applies to terms of office beginning on or after
  2 10 January 1, 2023, and for years of service occurring on or after
  2 11 January 1, 2023.
  2 12    The resolution, if adopted, would be referred to the next
  2 13 general assembly for adoption before being submitted to the
  2 14 electorate for ratification.
