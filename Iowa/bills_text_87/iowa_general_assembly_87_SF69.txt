  1 23                           EXPLANATION
  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    Under current law, the preschool budget enrollment figure
  1 27 used to calculate the amount of preschool foundation aid
  1 28 paid to a school district participating in the statewide
  1 29 preschool program for four=year=old children is equal to 50
  1 30 percent of the actual enrollment of eligible students in the
  1 31 preschool programming provided by a school district approved to
  1 32 participate in the preschool program on October 1 of the base
  1 33 year.
  1 34    This bill modifies the method of calculating the preschool
  1 35 budget enrollment by adding to the current calculation an
  2  1 amount equal to 50 percent of the actual enrollment of eligible
  2  2 students in the preschool programming provided by a school
  2  3 district approved to participate in the preschool program on
  2  4 October 1 of the base year, whose family income is at or below
  2  5 200 percent of the federal poverty level as defined by the most
  2  6 recently revised poverty income guidelines published by the
  2  7 United States department of health and human services.
  2  8    The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies to school
  2  9 budget years beginning on or after July 1, 2017.
