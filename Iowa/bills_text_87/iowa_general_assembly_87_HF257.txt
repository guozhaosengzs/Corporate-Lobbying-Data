  1  1    Section 1.  DIRECTIVE TO DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES ==
  1  2 PSYCHIATRIC BED TRACKING SYSTEM.  The department of human
  1  3 services shall amend its administrative rules pursuant to
  1  4 chapter 17A to require the state mental health institutes
  1  5 and hospitals licensed to provide inpatient psychiatric
  1  6 treatment and services to participate in the psychiatric bed
  1  7 tracking system and to report the number of beds available for
  1  8 persons with a co=occurring mental illness and substance abuse
  1  9 disorder.
  1 10                           EXPLANATION
  1 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 12 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 13    This bill relates to facilities licensed to provide
  1 14 inpatient psychiatric treatment and services and the
  1 15 psychiatric bed tracking system.  The bill directs the
  1 16 department of human services to amend its administrative rules
  1 17 to require the state mental health institutes and hospitals
  1 18 licensed to provide inpatient psychiatric treatment and
  1 19 services to participate in the psychiatric bed tracking system
  1 20 and to report the number of beds available for persons with a
  1 21 co=occurring mental illness and substance abuse disorder.
