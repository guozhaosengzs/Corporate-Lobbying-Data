  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    This bill exempts from the sales tax the sales price of
  1 27 chemicals, sorbents, or reagents sold, or test laboratory
  1 28 services furnished, to a nonprofit human blood collection
  1 29 and processing establishment that meets the definition of
  1 30 establishment in 21 C.F.R. {607.3(c). That federal regulation
  1 31 defines "establishment" to mean a place of business under
  1 32 one management at one general physical location.  The term
  1 33 includes, among others, human blood and plasma donor centers,
  1 34 blood banks, transfusion services, other blood product
  1 35 manufacturers, and independent laboratories that engage in
  2  1 quality control and testing for registered blood product
  2  2 establishments.
  2  3    By operation of Code section 423.6, an item exempt from the
  2  4 imposition of the sales tax is also exempt from the use tax
  2  5 imposed in Code section 423.5.
  2  6    The bill limits refunds of taxes, interest, or penalties
  2  7 arising from claims resulting from the enactment of the bill
  2  8 for sales occurring between July 1, 2005, and the effective
  2  9 date of the bill to $25,000 in the aggregate, and requires such
  2 10 claims to be filed prior to October 1, 2017. If the aggregate
  2 11 amount of refund claims exceeds $25,000, the department of
  2 12 revenue is required to prorate the $25,000 among all claimants
  2 13 in relation to each claim amount.
  2 14    The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies
  2 15 retroactively to July 1, 2005.
