                                 HOUSE FILE                                  HOUSE FILE                                  BY  KAUFMANN

 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to the placement of sex offenders who qualify
  2    for release from the custody of the department of human
  3    services or the department of corrections.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5875YH (4) 87
    jm/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  SEX OFFENDER ==== HOUSING.  The department of human
  1  2 services and the department of corrections shall cooperate in
  1  3 the coordination of resolving the lack of sufficient housing
  1  4 for placement of aging sex offenders who qualify for release
  1  5 from the custody of the department of human services or the
  1  6 department of corrections.  The cooperation shall lead to the
  1  7 issuance of a request for proposal process that gives due
  1  8 consideration to the solicitation of in=state and out=of=state
  1  9 placement options utilizing existing facilities including
  1 10 vacant facilities.
  1 11                           EXPLANATION
  1 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 13 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 14    This bill relates to the placement of sex offenders who
  1 15 qualify for release from the custody of the department of human
  1 16 services or the department of corrections.
  1 17    The bill provides that the department of human services
  1 18 and the department of corrections shall cooperate in the
  1 19 coordination of resolving the lack of sufficient housing for
  1 20 aging sex offenders who qualify for release from the custody
  1 21 of the department of human services or the department of
  1 22 corrections.  The cooperation shall lead to the implementation
  1 23 of a request for proposal process that gives due consideration
  1 24 to in=state and out=of=state placement options utilizing
  1 25 existing facilities including vacant facilities.
       LSB 5875YH (4) 87
       jm/rj
