                                 HOUSE FILE 

                                 HOUSE FILE 
                                 HOUSE FILE                                  HOUSE FILE                                  BY  COMMITTEE ON PUBLIC
                                     SAFETY

                                 (SUCCESSOR TO HF 2167)

 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act studying placement options for sex offenders who qualify
  2    for release from the custody of the department of human
  3    services or the department of corrections.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5875HV (2) 87
    jm/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  SEX OFFENDER == HOUSING == WORK GROUP.  The
  1  2 department of corrections shall lead a work group to study
  1  3 the issue of housing for the placement of aging sex offenders
  1  4 who qualify for release from the custody of the department of
  1  5 corrections or the department of human services.  The work
  1  6 group shall consist of representatives of the departments
  1  7 of inspections and appeals, public health, and justice, the
  1  8 department on aging, the state public defender, the office of
  1  9 ombudsman, the office of long=term care ombudsman, the judicial
  1 10 branch, Iowa legal aid, and AARP.  The work group shall meet to
  1 11 study this issue and submit a report to the general assembly
  1 12 by January 2019, with recommendations containing housing
  1 13 options for the placement of aging sex offenders who qualify
  1 14 for release from custody.
  1 15                           EXPLANATION
  1 16 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 17 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 18    This bill relates to studying placement options for sex
  1 19 offenders who qualify for release from the custody of the
  1 20 department of human services or the department of corrections.
  1 21    The bill provides that the department of corrections
  1 22 shall lead a work group to study the issue of housing for the
  1 23 placement of aging sex offenders who qualify for release from
  1 24 the custody of the department of corrections or the department
  1 25 of human services. The work group shall meet to study this
  1 26 issue and submit a report to the general assembly by January
  1 27 2019, with recommendations containing housing options for the
  1 28 placement of aging sex offenders who qualify for release from
  1 29 custody.
