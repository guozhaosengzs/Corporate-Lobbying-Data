  1  3    The administrator in charge of any secure or nonsecure
  1  4 facility for the detention or custody of juveniles shall have
  1  5 the authority to lock a door within the facility provided that
  1  6 any juvenile locked within the facility is not a juvenile who
  1  7 has been placed in the facility pursuant to any child in need
  1  8 of assistance proceeding. For purposes of this section, "lock"
  1  9 means a device to secure a door that is operated by a key, a
  1 10 combination, or an electronic system.
