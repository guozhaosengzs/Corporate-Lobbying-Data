  2 32 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 33 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 34    This bill relates to gifts of real property offered to, and
  2 35 contracts for the purchase of real property entered into by,
  3  1 county and city entities.
  3  2    The bill requires the governing body of a county (board of
  3  3 supervisors) or city (city council) to approve the acceptance
  3  4 of gifts of real property by resolution before acceptance of
  3  5 such a gift by an entity of the county or city. The bill also
  3  6 requires such approval before a county or city entity may
  3  7 enter into a contact for the purchase of real property, if the
  3  8 contract price for the real property is less than 50 percent of
  3  9 the fair market value for that property.
