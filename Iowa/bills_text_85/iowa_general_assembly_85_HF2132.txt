.
13B.12
Gideon

The
state

defender

Gideon
fellowship


program
for
the
entry

public


defender

The

public
defender

Each

shall

a
licensed




House
File
2132,
p.
2


attorney

to
practice
law
in
this
state
prior

commencement
of
the
fellowship.
