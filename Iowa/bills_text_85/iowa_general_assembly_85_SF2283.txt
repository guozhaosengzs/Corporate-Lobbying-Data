.

United

1.
Notwithstanding

United

or
owing
in
this
state

or

or
owed
in
the
course

owed

the

three

presumed
abandoned
property
under


section
556.9,
subsection
1.
All
property
rights
and
legal


title
to
and
ownership
of
such
United
States
savings
bonds


or
proceeds
from
such
bonds,
including
all
rights,
powers,


and
privileges
of
survivorship
of
any
owner,
co-owner,
or


beneficiary,

vest
solely
in
the
state.


3.
Within

eighty

the
three-year


period
referred

in
subsection
2,

to


the
state.
The

state

the
bringing


of

action
until
sufficient
United
States
savings
bonds

accumulated

the
treasurer
of
state’s

to
justify




Senate

the

of
state

the

of

The

“b”

(2)

(3)

(2)
