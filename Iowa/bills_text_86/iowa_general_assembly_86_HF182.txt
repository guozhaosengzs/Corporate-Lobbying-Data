                                 HOUSE FILE                                  HOUSE FILE                                  BY  MASCHER

 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to the development of a proposal to establish a
  2    family court in the judicial branch.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2165YH (2) 86
    rh/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  FAMILY COURTS.
  1  2    1.  The supreme court shall develop a proposal to establish
  1  3 a family court in the judicial branch.  The proposal shall
  1  4 include a description of the powers and responsibilities of
  1  5 the family court, an implementation schedule, budget, and an
  1  6 outline for the relationship of the family court with the
  1  7 department of human services, child welfare and delinquency
  1  8 services providers, and other publicly funded providers of
  1  9 services intended to stabilize families in this state.  The
  1 10 proposal shall include proposed statutory changes to implement
  1 11 family courts and other recommendations identified by the
  1 12 supreme court.
  1 13    2.  The supreme court shall submit the proposal to establish
  1 14 a family court to the governor and general assembly on or
  1 15 before December 1, 2015.
  1 16                           EXPLANATION
  1 17 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 18 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 19    This bill requires the supreme court to develop a proposal
  1 20 to establish a family court in the judicial branch.  Certain
  1 21 elements are to be included in the proposal.  The proposal is
  1 22 required to be submitted to the governor and general assembly
  1 23 on or before December 1, 2015.
