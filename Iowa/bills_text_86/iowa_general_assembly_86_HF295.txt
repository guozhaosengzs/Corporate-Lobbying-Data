  1 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 25    The bill provides that a person who will reside or who
  1 26 resides in a child care home that is not registered by the
  1 27 department of human services pursuant to Code chapter 237A and
  1 28 that does not receive public funding for providing child care
  1 29 shall be subject to a record check. The bill provides that the
  1 30 department is not responsible for the costs of such records
  1 31 checks.
  1 32    Under Code section 237A.5, the department is required to
  1 33 conduct a criminal and child abuse record check in this state
  1 34 for a person who is subject to a record check under Code
  1 35 chapter 237A and may conduct such a check in other states.
  2  1 In addition, the department may conduct a dependent adult
  2  2 abuse, sex offender registry, or other public or civil offense
  2  3 record check in this state or in other states for a person
  2  4 who is subject to a record check.  In addition, the person's
  2  5 fingerprints shall be provided to the department of public
  2  6 safety for submission through the state criminal history
  2  7 repository to the United States department of justice, federal
  2  8 bureau of investigation for a national criminal history check.

  2 10 or program providing child care to five or fewer children at
  2 11 any one time that is not registered to provide child care under
