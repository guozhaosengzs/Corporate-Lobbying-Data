  1 18                           EXPLANATION
  1 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 21    This bill relates to the Iowa educational savings plan
  1 22 trust, also known as the Iowa college savings 529 plan.
  1 23    The bill amends the tax year for which a contribution to
  1 24 the trust may be deducted for Iowa income tax purposes.  Under
  1 25 current law, a contribution to the trust must be made during
  1 26 the calendar year in order to be deductible for Iowa income
  1 27 tax purposes for that tax year.  The bill allows a participant
  1 28 who makes a contribution to the trust on or before the Iowa
  1 29 income tax return filing deadline (April 30 for calendar year
  1 30 tax filers), excluding extensions, to elect to have that
  1 31 contribution treated as though it was made on the last day of
  1 32 the preceding calendar year, thereby allowing the participant
  1 33 to claim the income tax deduction for the most recently
  1 34 completed tax year.  The bill requires the director of revenue,
  1 35 after consultation with the treasurer of state, to prescribe by
  2  1 rule the manner and method by which a participant may make this
  2  2 election.  The bill applies retroactively to January 1, 2015,
  2  3 for tax years beginning on or after that date.
