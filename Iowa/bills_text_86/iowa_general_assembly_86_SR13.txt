  1  1 A Resolution requesting the legislative council to
  1  2    establish an interim study committee to study local
  1  3    government mandates.
  1  4    WHEREAS, counties in this state are required by
  1  5 law to house a variety of state agencies, including
  1  6 employees of the department of human services and the
  1  7 judicial branch, and to provide such agencies with
  1  8 supplies while receiving little or no reimbursement
  1  9 from the state; and
  1 10    WHEREAS, the cost of paper document storage required
  1 11 by the state is a significant burden on Iowa counties,
  1 12 and the implementation of paperless filing systems can
  1 13 encourage government efficiency in document storage and
  1 14 retention; and
  1 15    WHEREAS, publishing costs of meeting records and
  1 16 legal notices pose a significant burden to local
  1 17 governments; and
  1 18    WHEREAS, the current formula for local emergency
  1 19 management agency funding does not adequately address
  1 20 the needs of Iowa's more populous counties; NOW
  1 21 THEREFORE,
  1 22    BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE, That the Senate
  1 23 requests the legislative council to authorize and
  1 24 establish an interim study committee, composed of
  1 25 members of the Senate and the House of Representatives,
  1 26 to commence meeting during the 2015 legislative
  1 27 interim. The purpose of the interim study committee
  1 28 is to study unfunded mandates referred to in this
  2  1 resolution and to receive and, if appropriate, make
  2  2 recommendations to the general assembly related to
  2  3 those mandates before January 1, 2016.
