  1  7 expenses shall not impose a copayment or coinsurance amount
  1  8 on an insured for services provided by a physical therapist
  1  9 licensed pursuant to chapter 148A, by an occupational therapist
  1 10 licensed pursuant to chapter 148B, or by a speech pathologist

  1 14 or osteopathic medicine and surgery under chapter 148 for the
  1 15 same or a similar diagnosed condition even if a different
  1 16 nomenclature is used to describe the condition for which the
  1 17 services are provided.

  1 19 third=party payment provider policies, contracts, or plans
  1 20 delivered, issued for delivery, continued, or renewed in this
  1 21 state on or after July 1, 2015:
  1 22    a.  Individual or group accident and sickness insurance
  1 23 providing coverage on an expense=incurred basis.
  1 24    b.  An individual or group hospital or medical service
  1 25 contract issued pursuant to chapter 509, 514, or 514A.
  1 26    c.  An individual or group health maintenance organization
  1 27 contract regulated under chapter 514B.
  1 28    d.  A plan established pursuant to chapter 509A for public
  1 29 employees.
  1 30    e.  An organized delivery system licensed by the director of
  1 31 public health.

  1 33 specified disease, short=term hospital or medical, hospital
  1 34 confinement indemnity, credit, dental, vision, Medicare
  1 35 supplement, long=term care, basic hospital and medical=surgical
  2  1 expense coverage as defined by the commissioner, disability
  2  2 income insurance coverage, coverage issued as a supplement
  2  3 to liability insurance, workers' compensation or similar
  2  4 insurance, or automobile medical payment insurance.
