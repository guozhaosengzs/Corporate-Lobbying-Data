 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to speech therapy benefits for children under
  2    Medicaid managed care.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5666XS (2) 86
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  HAWK=I COVERED SPEECH THERAPY BENEFIT UNDER
  1  2 MEDICAID MANAGED CARE CONTRACT.  The hawk=i covered speech
  1  3 therapy benefit under Medicaid managed care shall provide for
  1  4 coverage of both habilitative and rehabilitative services
  1  5 for children with speech, language, and hearing disorders as
  1  6 medically necessary. The determination that a service is
  1  7 medically necessary shall not be interpreted to require loss
  1  8 or impairment as the result of a stroke, accidental injury, or
  1  9 surgery to the head or neck as a prerequisite to coverage of
  1 10 services.
  1 11                           EXPLANATION
  1 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 13 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 14    This bill requires that the hawk=i covered speech therapy
  1 15 benefit under Medicaid managed care provide for coverage of
  1 16 both habilitative and rehabilitative services for children with
  1 17 speech, language, and hearing disorders as medically necessary.
  1 18 The bill prohibits the determination of a service as medically
  1 19 necessary from being interpreted to require loss or impairment
  1 20 as the result of a stroke, accidental injury, or surgery to the
  1 21 head or neck as a prerequisite to coverage of services.
       LSB 5666XS (2) 86
       pf/nh
