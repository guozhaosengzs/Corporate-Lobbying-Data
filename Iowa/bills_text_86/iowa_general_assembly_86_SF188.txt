  1 16 APPROPRIATION.
  1 17    1.  There is appropriated from the general fund of the state
  1 18 for deposit in the statewide fire and police retirement fund
  1 19 created in section 411.8, for the fiscal year beginning July 1,
  1 20 2014, and ending June 30, 2015, the following amount:
  1 21 .................................................. $  9,800,000
  1 22    2.  Moneys appropriated by the state pursuant to this section
  1 23 shall not be used to reduce the normal rate of contribution of
  1 24 any city below 17 percent.

  1 26 or provisions of this Act, being deemed of immediate
  1 27 importance, take effect upon enactment:
  1 28    1.  The section of this Act providing for an appropriation to
  1 29 the statewide fire and police retirement fund for the fiscal
  1 30 year beginning July 1, 2014.
  1 31                           EXPLANATION
  1 32 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 33 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 34    This bill provides for a state appropriation to the
  1 35 statewide fire and police retirement system established in
  2  1 Code chapter 411 equal to 3.79 percent of the covered earnable
  2  2 compensation of the members.  The percentage represents the
  2  3 cost of benefits provided by Acts of the 66th General Assembly.
  2  4 The bill restores the standing state appropriation to the
  2  5 retirement system that was repealed by 2010 Acts, chapter 1167.
  2  6 The repealed language had set the state appropriation based
  2  7 upon the cost of benefits provided by the Acts of the 66th
  2  8 General Assembly but did not specify a percentage.
  2  9    The bill also provides for an appropriation from the general
  2 10 fund of the state to the statewide fire and police retirement
  2 11 system fund for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2014, for
  2 12 $9.8 million.  This provision of the bill takes effect upon
  2 13 enactment.
