  1  5    1.  The board of directors of a school district that wishes
  1  6 to receive the budget adjustment under this section may adopt a
  1  7 resolution by May 15 preceding the budget year and shall notify
  1  8 the department of management of the adoption of the resolution
  1  9 and the amount of the budget adjustment to be received.
  1 10    2.  a.  For budget years beginning on or after July 1,
  1 11 2016, each school district that satisfies the requirements of
  1 12 subsection 1 shall be eligible for a budget adjustment for
  1 13 that budget year in an amount not to exceed an amount equal to
  1 14 the district's budget enrollment multiplied by the quotient
  1 15 obtained by dividing the sum of the maximum amount of the cash
  1 16 reserve levy available to each school district in the state as
  1 17 determined under section 298.10, subsection 3, for the budget
  1 18 year by the statewide budget enrollment for the base year.
  1 19    b.  The resolution adopted under subsection 1 may specify
  1 20 a budget adjustment amount that is less than the amount
  1 21 authorized under paragraph "a".
  1 22    c.  The school district shall fund the budget adjustment
  1 23 increase either by using moneys from its unexpended fund
  1 24 balance or by using cash reserve moneys subject to the
  1 25 limitation in section 298.10.
  1 26    3.  A budget adjustment received under this section shall
  1 27 not affect the eligibility for or amount of any other budget
  1 28 adjustment authorized by law for the same budget year. In
  1 29 addition, a budget adjustment under this section shall be
  1 30 limited to the budget year for which the adjustment was
  1 31 authorized and shall not be included in any computation of a
  1 32 school district's cost for any future budget year.
