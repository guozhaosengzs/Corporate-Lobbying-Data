  2 25 rhinoceros horn is guilty of a class "D" felony.  The sentence
  2 26 for a class "D" felony includes imprisonment for no more than
  2 27 five years and a fine of at least $750 but not more than $7,500.

  2 32 and the buyer or seller establishes that the antique is at
  2 33 least 100 years old, distributed for educational or scientific
  2 34 purposes, distributed to a legal beneficiary of a trust or an
  2 35 heir to an estate, or part of a musical instrument manufactured
  3  1 no later than 1975.  The department shall issue permits for the
  3  2 sale of such items.
