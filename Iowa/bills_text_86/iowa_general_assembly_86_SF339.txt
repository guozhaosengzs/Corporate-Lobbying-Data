  2  6 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  7 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  8    This bill provides that a commercial breeder of dogs or cats
  2  9 holding a state license issued by the department of agriculture
  2 10 and land stewardship may elect to have an annual inspection
  2 11 conducted by a licensed veterinarian in lieu of the department.
  2 12 In order to exercise this election, the commercial breeder must
  2 13 not have committed an inspection violation during the past four
  2 14 years and must comply with notice requirements established
  2 15 by the department.  The veterinarian must file an inspection
  2 16 report with the department.  The cost of the inspection is
  2 17 to be borne by the commercial breeder.  The department must
  2 18 conduct an inspection every other year.
