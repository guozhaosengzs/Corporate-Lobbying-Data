 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act requesting a legislative interim committee related to
  2    the competitive bidding law of this state.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 1536SV (1) 86
    tr/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  PUBLIC CONSTRUCTION BIDDING == LEGISLATIVE
  1  2 STUDY.  The legislative council is requested to establish a
  1  3 legislative interim study committee during the 2015 legislative
  1  4 interim to review and make recommendations concerning the
  1  5 competitive bidding law of this state. The study committee
  1  6 shall consider the status of current law, identify concerns or
  1  7 problems related to current law, and consider alternatives or
  1  8 alterations to current law. The study committee may require
  1  9 the cooperation of state and local government entities in
  1 10 conducting the study.
  1 11                           EXPLANATION
  1 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 13 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 14    This bill requests the legislative council to establish a
  1 15 legislative interim study committee during the 2015 legislative
  1 16 interim to review and make recommendations concerning the
  1 17 competitive bidding law of this state. The study committee
  1 18 shall consider the status of current law, identify concerns
  1 19 or problems related to current law, and consider alternatives
  1 20 or alterations to current law. The bill allows the study
  1 21 committee to require cooperation of state and local government
  1 22 entities in conducting the study.
