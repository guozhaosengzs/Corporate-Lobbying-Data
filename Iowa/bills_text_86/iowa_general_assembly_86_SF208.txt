 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act concerning local ordinances regulating access to wine,
  2    beer, and liquor control licensed establishments by any
  3    person nineteen years of age or older.

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 123.39, subsection 2, Code 2015, is
  1  2 amended to read as follows:
  1  3    2.  Local authorities may suspend any retail wine or
  1  4 beer permit or liquor control license for a violation of
  1  5 any ordinance or regulation adopted by the local authority.
  1  6 Local authorities may adopt ordinances or regulations for the
  1  7 location of the premises of retail wine or beer and liquor
  1  8 control licensed establishments and local authorities may adopt
  1  9 ordinances, not in conflict with this chapter and that do
  1 10 not diminish the hours during which beer, wine, or alcoholic
  1 11 beverages may be sold or consumed at retail, governing any
  1 12 other activities or matters which may affect the retail sale
  1 13 and consumption of beer, wine, and alcoholic liquor and the
  1 14 health, welfare and morals of the community involved.
