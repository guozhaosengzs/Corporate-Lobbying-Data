  1  3    1.  As used in this section, unless the context otherwise
  1  4 requires "employment transportation" means an urban or
  1  5 rural program or service that provides an individual with
  1  6 transportation solely to or from a workplace, including but not
  1  7 limited to the following programs and services:
  1  8    a.  Expanding or sustaining existing transportation services
  1  9 or service hours.
  1 10    b.  Coordinating ride share services, including car pool or
  1 11 van pool services.
  1 12    c.  Shuttle services.
  1 13    2.  The Iowa employment rides initiative is established in
  1 14 the department to provide funds to public transit systems for
  1 15 programs and services that provide employment transportation
  1 16 to Iowans.
  1 17    3.  The department shall award funds from the initiative
  1 18 on a competitive grant basis. A grant shall not exceed one
  1 19 hundred fifty thousand dollars. A grant application shall
  1 20 contain a commitment from the public transit system of at least
  1 21 a dollar=for=dollar match of the grant funds awarded. Moneys
  1 22 charged to individuals receiving employment transportation
  1 23 services cannot be used as matching funds. Grant funds shall
  1 24 be used only for operational costs directly associated with
  1 25 providing employment transportation and shall not be used for
  1 26 capital expenditures or construction.
  1 27    4.  A public transit system may coordinate with other local,
  1 28 state, or federal governmental agencies and private nonprofit
  1 29 organizations in the administration of a program or service
  1 30 receiving a grant under the initiative and in expenditure of
  1 31 grant funds.
  1 32    5.  The department shall, by January 1 each year, submit
  1 33 a report to the general assembly on the outcomes of the
  1 34 initiative, including the grant amount, the type of program or
  1 35 service receiving funds, and the number of individuals served
  2  1 for each grant awarded by the initiative. As a condition of
  2  2 having received a grant from the initiative, a public transit
  2  3 system shall provide the department with information on any
  2  4 program or service for which the public transit system is
  2  5 awarded a grant from the initiative.
  2  6    6.  The department shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 17A
  2  7 to administer the initiative, including but not limited to an
  2  8 application process and grant award criteria.
  2  9    7.  a.  An Iowa employment rides fund is created in the state
  2 10 treasury under the control of the department. The fund shall
  2 11 consist of moneys appropriated to the department and any other
  2 12 moneys available to, obtained, or accepted by the department
  2 13 for placement in the fund.
  2 14    b.  Moneys in the fund shall be used to provide grants
  2 15 under the Iowa employment rides initiative established in this
  2 16 section.
  2 17    c.  Moneys in the fund are not subject to section 8.33.
  2 18 Notwithstanding section 12C.7, subsection 2, interest or
  2 19 earnings on moneys in the fund shall be credited to the fund.
