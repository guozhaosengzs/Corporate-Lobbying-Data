  1  9 from the ground. For a pop=up blind or stand, the metal plate

  1 11 of the pop=up blind or stand.

  1 13 a device or an enclosure used for concealment while hunting
  1 14 deer, constructed either wholly or partially from man=made
  1 15 materials, and used by a person who is hunting deer for the
  1 16 purpose of hiding from sight.  A "blind or stand" is not a
  1 17 naturally occurring landscape feature or an arrangement of
  1 18 natural or agricultural plant material that a deer hunter uses
  1 19 for concealment.  The natural resource commission shall adopt
  1 20 rules pursuant to chapter 17A to implement this section.

  1 26                           EXPLANATION
  1 27 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 28 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 29    This bill requires that a blind or stand placed on public
  1 30 land for hunting deer be affixed with and exhibit a metal plate
  1 31 which plainly shows the hunting license number of the owner of
  1 32 the blind or stand.
  1 33    For a tree blind or stand, the metal plate must be affixed
  1 34 on the front of the foot platform of the blind or stand on the
  1 35 third step up from the ground.  For a pop=up blind or stand, the
  2  1 metal plate must be affixed to or hung from the zipper head or
  2  2 access hook of the blind or stand.
  2  3    For purposes of the bill, a "blind or stand" is a device
  2  4 or an enclosure used for concealment while hunting deer,
  2  5 constructed either wholly or partially from man=made materials,
  2  6 and used by a person who is hunting deer for the purpose of
  2  7 hiding from sight.  A "blind or stand" does not include a
  2  8 naturally occurring landscape feature or an arrangement of
  2  9 natural or agricultural plant material that a deer hunter uses
  2 10 for concealment.  The natural resource commission is required
  2 11 to adopt rules to implement the provisions of the bill.  A
  2 12 violation of the provisions of the bill is punishable by a
  2 13 scheduled fine of $20.
