  1 30 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 31 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 32    This bill makes changes to program limitations within the
  1 33 national guard educational assistance program.
  1 34    Under current law, participation in the national guard
  1 35 educational assistance program is limited by semesters of
  2  1 attendance, or the trimester or quarter equivalent. The bill
  2  2 replaces these limitations by providing that an eligible member
  2  3 of the Iowa national guard may not receive assistance under the
  2  4 program for more than 120 credit hours of undergraduate study.
  2  5 The bill includes transition provisions relating to educational
  2  6 assistance received under the program prior to July 1, 2015.
