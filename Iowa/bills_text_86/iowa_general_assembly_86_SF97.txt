  2  3 collected under chapter 105 shall be prorated to June 30, 2015,

  2  7                           EXPLANATION
  2  8 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  9 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 10    This bill repeals the Iowa plumber, mechanical professional,
  2 11 and contractor licensing Act. This repeal also eliminates
  2 12 language in Code section 105.17 that provides that Code
  2 13 chapter 105 supersedes and preempts all plumbing, mechanical,
  2 14 HVAC=refrigeration, sheet metal, hydronic, and contracting
  2 15 licensing provisions of cities and counties.
  2 16    The bill provides that all licensing fees collected shall
  2 17 be prorated to June 30, 2015, and that the portion of the
  2 18 licensing fee that represents the remaining period of the
  2 19 license, following June 30, 2015, shall be returned to the
  2 20 licensee.
