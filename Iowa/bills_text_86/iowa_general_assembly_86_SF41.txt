PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  SECONDARY ROAD BRIDGE PROJECTS ====
  1  2 APPROPRIATION.  There is appropriated from the general
  1  3 fund of the state to the road use tax fund for the fiscal year
  1  4 beginning July 1, 2015, and ending June 30, 2016, the following
  1  5 amount, to be used for the purposes designated:
  1  6 .................................................. $200,000,000
  1  7    Notwithstanding section 312.2, 312.3, 312.3C, or 312.3D, or
  1  8 any other provision to the contrary, the moneys appropriated
  1  9 pursuant to this section shall be used for construction,
  1 10 reconstruction, and improvement projects on bridges within the
  1 11 secondary road system.  The treasurer of state shall distribute
  1 12 the amount appropriated under this section for deposit in the
  1 13 secondary road funds of the counties as follows:
  1 14    1.  The first $99,000,000 shall be allocated to each county
  1 15 on an equal basis.
  1 16    2.  The remaining $101,000,000 shall be allocated to the
  1 17 counties on a per capita basis.
  1 18                           EXPLANATION
  1 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 21    This bill appropriates $200 million from the general fund
  1 22 of the state to the road use tax fund for FY 2015=2016 for
  1 23 construction, reconstruction, and improvement projects on
  1 24 bridges within the secondary road system.  The bill directs
  1 25 the treasurer of state to distribute the first $99 million to
  1 26 each county equally, and the remaining $101 million is to be
  1 27 distributed to the counties on a per capita basis.
