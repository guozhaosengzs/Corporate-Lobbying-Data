  1 23                           EXPLANATION
  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    Currently, the state board of regents and the department
  1 27 of transportation are exempted from Code chapter 26, the
  1 28 Iowa Construction Bidding Procedures Act, through a specific
  1 29 exemption in the Code chapter's definition of "governmental
  1 30 entity".
  1 31    This bill makes Code chapter 26 applicable to both the state
  1 32 board of regents and the department of transportation and
  1 33 specifically requires the state board of regents to comply with
  1 34 Code chapter 26 when advertising for bids and selecting the
  1 35 lowest responsible bidder as governed by Code section 262.34,
  2  1 subsection 1.
  2  2    The bill requires governmental entities seeking to contract
  2  3 for a public improvement project to notify bidders that bids
  2  4 covering all labor and materials related to the project will
  2  5 be accepted, but also authorizes government entities to allow
  2  6 bidders to submit separate bids for separate portions of the
  2  7 project.
