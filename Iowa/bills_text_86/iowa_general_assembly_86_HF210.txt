  1 29 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 30 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 31    BILL'S PROVISIONS.  This bill provides that a person who
  1 32 holds an ownership interest in an animal classified as a cow,
  1 33 sheep, or goat is not prohibited from using milk derived from
  1 34 that animal for personal consumption or other personal use.  It
  1 35 amends a number of Code chapters specifically regulating the
  2  1 production and sale of milk, by providing that the provisions
  2  2 of those Code chapters do not apply to such milk.
  2  3    BACKGROUND.  Generally, the production and sale of milk
  2  4 is regulated by the department of agriculture and land
  2  5 stewardship.  The department regulates the processing of milk
  2  6 and the manufacture of milk or milk products and dairy products
  2  7 under a number of Code chapters, including Code chapter 159
  2  8 which sets forth its general regulatory authority, Code chapter
  2  9 190 which relates to adulteration of food, Code chapter 191
  2 10 which relates to the labeling of food, and Code chapter 192
  2 11 which relates to milk production and processing, the "Iowa
  2 12 Grade 'A' Milk Inspection Law" (Code section 192.101).
