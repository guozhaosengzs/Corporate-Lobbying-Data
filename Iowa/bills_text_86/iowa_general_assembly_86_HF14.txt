 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act requiring newborn metabolic screenings to include
  2    screening for certain lysosomal storage disorders.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 1156YH (3) 86
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  NEWBORN METABOLIC SCREENING == INCLUSION OF
  1  2 LYSOSOMAL STORAGE DISORDERS.  Pursuant to section 136A.8,
  1  3 the center for congenital and inherited disorders, with the
  1  4 assistance of the department of public health, shall adopt
  1  5 rules to require the screening of newborns for the following
  1  6 lysosomal storage disorders as part of the newborn metabolic
  1  7 screening pursuant to section 136A.5:
  1  8    1.  Globoid cell leukodystrophy (Krabbe).
  1  9    2.  Fabry.
  1 10    3.  Pompe.
  1 11    4.  Niemann=Pick.
  1 12    5.  Gaucher.
  1 13    6.  Hurler syndrome (MPS I).
  1 14                           EXPLANATION
  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    This bill directs the center for congenital and inherited
  1 18 disorders with the assistance of the department of public
  1 19 health to adopt rules to require the screening of newborns
  1 20 for the six specified lysosomal storage disorders (LSDs) as
  1 21 part of the newborn metabolic screening.  The LSDs specified
  1 22 are:  Globoid cell leukodystrophy (Krabbe), Fabry, Pompe,
  1 23 Niemann=Pick, Gaucher, and Hurler syndrome (MPS I).
       LSB 1156YH (3) 86
       pf/nh
