  1 15                           EXPLANATION
  1 16 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 17 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 18    This bill modifies the criminal penalties for a controlled
  1 19 substance that contains cocaine base.
  1 20    The bill increases the amounts of controlled substances
  1 21 containing cocaine base (crack cocaine) that determine the
  1 22 criminal penalty for certain offenses.  Under the bill, if a
  1 23 person unlawfully manufactures, delivers, or possesses with the
  1 24 intent to deliver a controlled substance containing cocaine
  1 25 base, or unlawfully acts with, enters into a common scheme
  1 26 or design with, or conspires with one or more persons to
  1 27 manufacture, deliver, or possess such a controlled substance,
  1 28 the person commits the following offenses: a class "B" felony
  1 29 punishable by confinement of no more than 50 years and a
  1 30 fine of not more than $1 million if the controlled substance
  1 31 containing cocaine base is more than 125 grams rather than the
  1 32 current 50 grams; a class "B" felony punishable by confinement
  1 33 of no more than 25 years and a fine of not less than $5,000 but
  1 34 not more than $100,000 if the controlled substance is more than
  1 35 35 grams but not more than 125 grams rather than the current 10
  2  1 to 50 grams; or a class "C" felony punishable by a fine of not
  2  2 less than $1,000 but not more than $50,000 if the controlled
  2  3 substance containing cocaine base is 35 grams or less rather
  2  4 than the current 10 grams or less.
       LSB 5986SV (2) 86
       jm/rj
