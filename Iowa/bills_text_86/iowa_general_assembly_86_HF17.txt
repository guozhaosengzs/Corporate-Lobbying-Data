  1  6                           EXPLANATION
  1  7 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  8 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  9    This bill provides for recision of administrative driver's
  1 10 license revocations in operating=while=intoxicated (OWI) cases
  1 11 where there are no longer reasonable grounds to believe the
  1 12 person was operating the motor vehicle.
  1 13    Current law provides that the department of transportation
  1 14 (DOT) may revoke an individual's driver's license for OWI
  1 15 before the completion of the individual's criminal OWI
  1 16 proceeding. However, current law also provides that the DOT
  1 17 must rescind the revocation where the court in the individual's
  1 18 criminal OWI proceeding makes certain findings.
  1 19    The bill provides for an additional finding under which the
  1 20 DOT must rescind the revocation of an individual's driver's
  1 21 license. The bill requires the DOT to rescind the revocation
  1 22 where the court dismisses the individual's OWI charge with
  1 23 the stated reason for such dismissal being that new evidence
  1 24 establishes there are no longer reasonable grounds to believe
  1 25 the individual was operating the motor vehicle.
