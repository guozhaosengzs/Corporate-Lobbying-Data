 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to disclosure of ownership and control
  2    information for nonprofit corporations under the Medicaid
  3    program.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5608XS (3) 86
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  DISCLOSURE OF OWNERSHIP AND CONTROL INFORMATION
  1  2 UNDER MEDICAID PROGRAM == NONPROFIT CORPORATIONS.  The
  1  3 department of human services shall contract with a third=party
  1  4 certified public accounting organization to collect and verify
  1  5 information submitted by a disclosing entity to comply with the
  1  6 requirements for disclosure of information on ownership and
  1  7 control pursuant to 42 C.F.R. {455.104, if any person with an
  1  8 ownership or control interest in the disclosing entity is an
  1  9 officer or director of a nonprofit corporation. The certified
  1 10 public accounting organization shall notify the department
  1 11 upon verification of the information. A Medicaid managed care
  1 12 organization contracting with the state shall not require
  1 13 disclosure of such ownership and control information from
  1 14 nonprofit corporations if the entity is approved and enrolled
  1 15 by the department as a provider under the Medicaid program.
  1 16                           EXPLANATION
  1 17 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 18 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 19    This bill requires the department of human services to
  1 20 contract with a third=party certified public accounting
  1 21 organization to collect and verify information submitted to
  1 22 comply with the requirements for disclosure of information on
  1 23 ownership and control pursuant to 42 C.F.R. {455.104, if any
  1 24 person with an ownership or control interest in the disclosing
  1 25 entity is an officer or director of a nonprofit corporation.
  1 26 The certified public accounting organization shall notify the
  1 27 department upon verification of the information. The bill also
  1 28 provides that a Medicaid managed care organization contracting
  1 29 with the state shall not require disclosure of the ownership
  1 30 and control information from nonprofit corporations if the
  1 31 entity is approved and enrolled by the department as a provider
  1 32 under the Medicaid program.
  1 33    Under the federal Patient Protection and Affordable Care
  1 34 Act, state Medicaid agencies are required to collect ownership
  1 35 and control information and conduct enhanced screenings of all
  2  1 Medicaid providers.  Under 42 U.S.C. {1320a=3, a disclosing
  2  2 entity is required to report certain information regarding each
  2  3 person with an ownership or control interest in the entity.
  2  4 A person with an ownership or control interest includes an
  2  5 officer or director of the entity, if the entity is organized
  2  6 as a corporation, whether the corporation is a for=profit or
  2  7 not=for=profit corporation.
