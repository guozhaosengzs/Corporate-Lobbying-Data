  1 14    Under current Code section 103.15, subsection 3, an
  1 15 apprentice electrician or unclassified person is required to
  1 16 be in the presence of a licensed electrician at all times to
  1 17 perform electrical wiring. This bill amends the definition
  1 18 of "the direct personal on=the=job supervision and control

  1 20 licensed electrician to be present for a minimum of 25 percent

  1 22 unclassified person to perform electrical wiring.
