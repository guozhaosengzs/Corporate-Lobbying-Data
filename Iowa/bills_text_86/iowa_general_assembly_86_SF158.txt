  2 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 21    This bill provides for the issuance of special vehicle
  2 22 registration plates with a disabled veteran processed
  2 23 emblem, to be designed by the department of transportation in
  2 24 consultation with the adjutant general.  The special disabled
  2 25 veteran plates are available to the owner of a motor truck,
  2 26 motor home, multipurpose vehicle, motorcycle, trailer, or
  2 27 travel trailer who is a veteran of the armed forces of the
  2 28 United States and, due to a service=connected disability, meets
  2 29 the definition of a person with a disability eligible to use
  2 30 persons with disabilities parking spaces.  Application for
  2 31 the disabled veteran registration plates must be accompanied
  2 32 by documentation identifying the person as a disabled veteran
  2 33 with a service=connected disability along with a statement
  2 34 from a physician, physician assistant, advanced registered
  2 35 nurse practitioner, or chiropractor stating the nature of the
  3  1 applicant's service=connected disability and any additional
  3  2 information required by rules adopted by the department.  Upon
  3  3 approval of the application by the department, the disabled
  3  4 veteran registration plates shall be issued without any
  3  5 additional fee, other than the regular registration fee for
  3  6 the vehicle.  The disabled veteran registration plates shall
  3  7 serve as a persons with a disability parking permit for the
  3  8 disabled veteran.  As with regular persons with disabilities
  3  9 special plates, the disabled veteran plates are required to be
  3 10 surrendered in exchange for regular registration plates when
  3 11 the owner of the vehicle no longer qualifies as a person with a
  3 12 disability for purposes of special parking privileges.
