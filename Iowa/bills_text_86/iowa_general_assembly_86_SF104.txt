  2 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 13 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 14    This bill reduces by approximately 20 percent the tax rate
  2 15 for each of the nine tax brackets of the individual income tax
  2 16 and for each of the four tax brackets of the corporate income
  2 17 tax.
  2 18    The current individual income tax rates range from a low of
  2 19 .36 percent to a high of 8.98 percent.  The bill changes these
  2 20 rates to a low of .29 percent and a high of 7.18 percent.
  2 21    The current corporate income tax rates range from a low of 6
  2 22 percent to a high of 12 percent.  The bill changes these rates
  2 23 to a low of 4.80 percent and a high of 9.60 percent.
  2 24    The bill takes effect January 1, 2016, and applies to tax
  2 25 years beginning on or after that date.
