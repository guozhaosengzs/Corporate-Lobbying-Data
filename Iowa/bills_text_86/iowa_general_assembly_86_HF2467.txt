  1 21                           EXPLANATION
  1 22 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 23 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 24    This bill relates to the automatic repeal of income tax
  1 25 checkoffs.
  1 26    Code section 422.12E limits to four the number of income tax
  1 27 checkoffs that can appear on the income tax return.  When the
  1 28 same four income tax return checkoffs have been provided on the
  1 29 income tax return for two consecutive years, the two checkoffs
  1 30 for which the least amount has been contributed through March
  1 31 15 of the second tax year are repealed.  The bill strikes the
  1 32 automatic repeal provision.
  1 33    As a result, the four current income tax checkoffs that were
  1 34 subject to the automatic repeal provision (Iowa state fair
  1 35 foundation checkoff, veterans trust fund and volunteer fire
  2  1 fighter preparedness joint checkoff, child abuse prevention
  2  2 checkoff, and fish and game protection checkoff) will become
  2  3 permanent on the income tax return unless they are repealed by
  2  4 the general assembly.
  2  5    The bill applies retroactively to January 1, 2016, for tax
  2  6 years beginning on or after that date.
