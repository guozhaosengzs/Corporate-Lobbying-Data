  2 20                           EXPLANATION
  2 21 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 22 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 23    This bill relates to the commission of an assault with sexual
  2 24 intent.
  2 25    The bill provides that a person who commits an assault
  2 26 as defined in Code section 708.1, by touching the breast of
  2 27 another, whether or not the touching was through the clothing
  2 28 or other covering, with the intent to arouse or gratify the
  2 29 sexual desires of either party, or for purposes of sexually
  2 30 degrading or humiliating the other person, commits assault with
  2 31 sexual intent.
  2 32    Under the bill, a person who commits assault with sexual
  2 33 intent commits a class "C" felony if the person causes serious
  2 34 injury to any person, a class "D" felony if the person causes
  2 35 bodily injury, and an aggravated misdemeanor if no injury
  3  1 results. A person who commits an assault with sexual intent
  3  2 under the bill must register as a sex offender and is subject
  3  3 to a special sentence under Code section 903B.1 or 903B.2
  3  4 depending on the severity of the offense.
  3  5    If a person touches the breast of another and commits an
  3  6 assault as defined in Code section 708.1, whether or not the
  3  7 touching was through the clothing or other covering, and the
  3  8 touching is without the intent to arouse or gratify the sexual
  3  9 desires of either party and not for the purpose of sexually
  3 10 degrading or humiliating the other person, the person commits
  3 11 a serious misdemeanor.
  3 12    The bill also makes numerous conforming changes in Code
  3 13 chapter 692A.
