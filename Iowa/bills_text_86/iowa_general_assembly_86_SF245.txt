  2 16                           EXPLANATION
  2 17 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 18 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 19    This bill relates to limited English proficient education
  2 20 by modifying the supplementary weighting for limited English
  2 21 proficient students.
  2 22    Current Code section 280.4 provides supplementary weighting
  2 23 for the excess costs of instruction of limited English
  2 24 proficient students.  The current amount of the supplementary
  2 25 weighting for students identified as limited English proficient
  2 26 is 0.22 per student.  The weighting is provided for those
  2 27 students first determined to be limited English proficient for
  2 28 a budget year beginning on or after July 1, 2010, and may be
  2 29 included in the weighted enrollment of the school district for
  2 30 a period not exceeding five years.
  2 31    The bill increases the amount of the supplementary weighting
  2 32 for those students identified as limited English proficient to
  2 33 0.30 per student.
  2 34    The bill also specifies the number of years during which the
  2 35 additional weighting may be included in the school district's
  3  1 weighted enrollment.  For students first determined to be
  3  2 limited English proficient for a budget year beginning on or
  3  3 after July 1, 2010, but before July 1, 2015, the additional
  3  4 weighting may be included for a period not exceeding five
  3  5 years. For students first determined to be limited English
  3  6 proficient for the budget year beginning on July 1, 2015, the
  3  7 additional weighting may be included for a period not exceeding
  3  8 six years.  For students first determined to be limited English
  3  9 proficient for a budget year beginning on or after July 1,
  3 10 2016, the additional weighting may be included for a period not
  3 11 exceeding seven years.
  3 12    The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies to school
  3 13 budget years beginning on or after July 1, 2015.
