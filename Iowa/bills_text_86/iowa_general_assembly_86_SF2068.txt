  2 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 25    This bill repeals Code chapter 455C of the 2016 Iowa Code,
  2 26 titled "Beverage Containers Control". Code chapter 455C
  2 27 provides for a 5 cent refund value paid by redemption centers
  2 28 to consumers upon the return of a beverage container sold in
  2 29 this state. In addition, the bill removes numerous references
  2 30 to Code chapter 455C throughout the Code, including a provision
  2 31 creating a simple misdemeanor penalty for distributors that
  2 32 sell beverage containers connected to other beverage containers
  2 33 by a nonbiodegradable or nonphotodegradable device.
