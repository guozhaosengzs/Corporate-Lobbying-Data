  4 13                           EXPLANATION
  4 14 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  4 15 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  4 16    Current law provides that fire apparatus are not subject
  4 17 to certain motor vehicle size, weight, and load limit
  4 18 requirements, or the permit requirements of Code chapter 321E.
  4 19 Current law also provides that fire apparatus are not subject
  4 20 to local ordinances or departmental regulations restricting
  4 21 vehicle weight.
  4 22    The bill broadens these exceptions to include all
  4 23 self=propelled authorized emergency vehicles which are owned
  4 24 and operated by a state, county, or municipal law enforcement
  4 25 agency. Authorized emergency vehicles include fire department
  4 26 vehicles, police vehicles, ambulances, and other emergency
  4 27 vehicles owned by the government.
