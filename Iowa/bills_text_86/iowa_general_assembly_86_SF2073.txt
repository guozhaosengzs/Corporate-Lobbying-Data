  2 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 17    This bill concerns the department of public defense.
  2 18    Code section 29.1 is amended to provide that the department
  2 19 of public defense is also composed of the Iowa gold star
  2 20 military museum and that the adjutant general shall perform all
  2 21 functions, responsibilities, powers, and duties concerning the
  2 22 museum.
  2 23    New Code section 29.4 provides that the department of public
  2 24 defense shall establish a military museum located at Camp Dodge
  2 25 which shall be known as the Iowa gold star military museum.
  2 26 The bill provides that the adjutant general shall administer
  2 27 the museum and may appoint a museum director and such other
  2 28 personnel for the purposes of maintaining and operating the
  2 29 museum.
  2 30    Code section 261.86, concerning the national guard education
  2 31 assistance program, is amended.  The bill eliminates the
  2 32 provision that moneys appropriated for purposes of this program
  2 33 shall not revert for the following fiscal year only until one
  2 34 year after operation Iraqi freedom, operation new dawn, and
  2 35 operation enduring freedom are concluded.  This provision takes
  3  1 effect upon enactment and applies retroactively to June 30,
  3  2 2015.  The Code section is further amended to provide that the
  3  3 college student aid commission may use no more than 1 percent
  3  4 of the moneys appropriated for the program for costs associated
  3  5 with administering the program.
