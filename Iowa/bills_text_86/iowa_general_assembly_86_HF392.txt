 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to certification of outpatient diabetes
  2    education programs in the state.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2448YH (1) 86
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  OUTPATIENT DIABETES EDUCATION PROGRAMS ==
  1  2 CERTIFICATION.  The department of public health shall adopt
  1  3 rules to establish certification requirements for outpatient
  1  4 diabetes education programs as a basis for third=party
  1  5 reimbursement.  The rules shall eliminate state=specific
  1  6 requirements for certification of such programs and instead
  1  7 shall accept recognition by the American diabetes association
  1  8 or accreditation by the American association of diabetic
  1  9 educators as the basis for granting state certification.
  1 10                           EXPLANATION
  1 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 12 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 13    This bill directs the department of public health to adopt
  1 14 rules for certification requirements for outpatient diabetes
  1 15 education programs as a basis for third=party reimbursement.
  1 16 The bill requires that the rules eliminate state=specific
  1 17 requirements for certification of such programs and instead
  1 18 accept recognition by the American diabetes association or
  1 19 accreditation by the American association of diabetic educators
  1 20 as the basis for granting state certification.
