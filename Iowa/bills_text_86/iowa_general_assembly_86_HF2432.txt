 A BILL FOR

    tm/rj/md

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 602.1501, Code 2016, is amended by
  1  2 striking the section and inserting in lieu thereof the
  1  3 following:
  1  4    602.1501  Judicial salaries.
  1  5    The salaries of all judicial officers as defined in section
  1  6 602.1101 shall be set by the supreme court and paid from the
  1  7 general operating moneys appropriated to the judicial branch.
  1  8 A salary increase approved by the supreme court shall be made a
  1  9 public record through a court order no less than thirty days
  1 10 prior to the effective date of the increase.  A salary increase
  1 11 shall not take effect in a fiscal year until the general
  1 12 operating moneys for the judicial branch have been appropriated
  1 13 for that fiscal year.
  1 14    Sec. 2.  Section 602.6201, subsections 5, 6, 7, and 10, Code
  1 15 2016, are amended by striking the subsections.
  1 16    Sec. 3.  Section 602.6301, Code 2016, is amended by striking
  1 17 the section and inserting in lieu thereof the following:
  1 18    602.6301  Number and apportionment of district associate
  1 19 judges.
  1 20    The supreme court shall prescribe, subject to the
  1 21 restrictions of this section, a formula to determine the
  1 22 number of district associate judges serving in each judicial
  1 23 election district.  The formula shall be based on a model that
  1 24 measures and applies an estimated case=related workload formula
  1 25 of judicial officers, and shall account for administrative
  1 26 duties, travel time, and other judicial duties not related to a
  1 27 specific case.
  1 28    Sec. 4.  Section 602.6401, subsection 1, Code 2016, is
  1 29 amended to read as follows:
  1 30    1.                                       APPROPRIATIONS
