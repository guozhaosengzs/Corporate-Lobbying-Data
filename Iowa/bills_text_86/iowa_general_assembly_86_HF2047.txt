  1 26                           EXPLANATION
  1 27 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 28 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 29    This bill requires the natural resource commission to adopt
  1 30 rules allowing the use of straight wall cartridge rifles
  1 31 to hunt deer by adults who have a permanent disability or
  1 32 are nonambulatory, or by youths under 18 years of age. The
  1 33 straight wall cartridge rifles allowed must be of the same
  1 34 caliber and use the same straight wall ammunition as is allowed
  1 35 for use in a pistol or revolver for hunting deer.
  2  1 A violation of the bill's provisions is punishable with a
  2  2 scheduled fine of $100.
