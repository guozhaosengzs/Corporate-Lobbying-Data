 HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION

  1 A Joint Resolution proposing an amendment to the Constitution
  2    of the State of Iowa restricting the use of revenues derived
  3    from a certain increase in the state sales and use tax rates
  4    for school infrastructure purposes.
  5 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5643YH (4) 86
    md/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.    The following amendment to the Constitution of
  1  2 the State of Iowa is proposed:
  1  3    Article VII of the Constitution of the State of Iowa is
  1  4 amended by adopting the following new section:
  1  5 School infrastructure revenues.  SEC. 11.  Beginning July 1,
  1  6 2019, all revenue derived from the increase in the state sales
  1  7 or use tax rate from five to six percent as established in 2008
  1  8 Iowa Acts, chapter 1134, and until December 31, 2029, or a
  1  9 later date established by law, shall be used exclusively for
  1 10 purposes of funding school district infrastructure needs or for
  1 11 providing school district property tax relief as the general
  1 12 assembly may provide by law.
  1 13    Sec. 2.  REFERRAL AND PUBLICATION.  The foregoing proposed
  1 14 amendment to the Constitution of the State of Iowa is referred
  1 15 to the general assembly to be chosen at the next general
  1 16 election for members of the general assembly, and the secretary
  1 17 of state is directed to cause the proposed amendment to be
  1 18 published for three consecutive months previous to the date of
  1 19 the election as provided by law.
  1 20                           EXPLANATION
  1 21 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 22 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 23    This joint resolution proposes an amendment to the
  1 24 Constitution of the State of Iowa.  The amendment provides that
  1 25 beginning July 1, 2919, all revenue derived from the increase
  1 26 in the state sales or use tax rate from 5 to 6 percent as
  1 27 established in 2008 Iowa Acts, chapter 1134, and until December
  1 28 31, 2029, or a later date established by law, shall be used
  1 29 exclusively for funding school district infrastructure needs or
  1 30 for providing school district property tax relief as general
  1 31 assembly may provide by law.
  1 32    The resolution, if adopted, will be referred to the next
  1 33 general assembly.  If the next general assembly adopts the
  1 34 resolution, the amendment will be submitted to the voters for
  1 35 ratification.

  1  4 amended by adopting the following new section:
  1  5 School infrastructure revenues.  SEC. 11.  Beginning July 1,
  1  6 2019, all revenue derived from the increase in the state sales
  1  7 or use tax rate from five to six percent as established in 2008
  1  8 Iowa Acts, chapter 1134, and until December 31, 2029, or a
  1  9 later date established by law, shall be used exclusively for
  1 10 purposes of funding school district infrastructure needs or for
  1 11 providing school district property tax relief as the general
  1 12 assembly may provide by law.
  1 13    Sec. 2.  REFERRAL AND PUBLICATION.  The foregoing proposed
  1 14 amendment to the Constitution of the State of Iowa is referred
  1 15 to the general assembly to be chosen at the next general
  1 16 election for members of the general assembly, and the secretary
  1 17 of state is directed to cause the proposed amendment to be
  1 18 published for three consecutive months previous to the date of
  1 19 the election as provided by law.

  1 30 for providing school district property tax relief as general
