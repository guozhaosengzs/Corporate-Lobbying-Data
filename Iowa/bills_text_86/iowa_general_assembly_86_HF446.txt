                                 HOUSE FILE 

                                 HOUSE FILE 
                                 HOUSE FILE                                  HOUSE FILE                                  BY  COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION

                                 (SUCCESSOR TO HSB 172)

 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to a statewide assessment of student progress
  2    on the core academic indicators in mathematics, reading, and
  3    science administered by school districts.
  4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2568HV (1) 86
    kh/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  LEGISLATIVE INTENT == STATEWIDE ASSESSMENT OF
  1  2 STUDENT PROGRESS.  It is the intent of the general assembly to
  1  3 address during the 2015 legislative session the administration
  1  4 by school districts of a statewide assessment of student
  1  5 progress which, in accordance with section 256.7, subsection
  1  6 21, paragraph "b", at a minimum assesses student progress
  1  7 on the core academic indicators in mathematics and reading
  1  8 in grades four, eight, and eleven, and the core academic
  1  9 indicators in science in grades eight and eleven.
  1 10                           EXPLANATION
  1 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 12 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 13    This bill establishes legislative intent to address during
  1 14 the 2015 legislative session the administration by school
  1 15 districts of a statewide assessment of student progress on
  1 16 core academic indicators which is required under current law.
  1 17 Currently, the state board is required to provide in rule for a
  1 18 set of core academic indicators in mathematics and reading in
  1 19 grades 4, 8, and 11, and for science in grades 8 and 11.
       LSB 2568HV (1) 86
