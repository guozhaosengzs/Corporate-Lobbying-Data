  1 10                           EXPLANATION
  1 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 12 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 13    This bill relates to interference with official acts.
  1 14    Under the bill, a person under the custody, control, or
  1 15 supervision of a county jail or municipal holding facility who
  1 16 knowingly resists, obstructs, or interferes with a jailer,
  1 17 agent, employee, or contractor in the performance of the
  1 18 official duties of the jailer, agent, employee, or contractor
  1 19 commits interference with official acts.
  1 20    The criminal offense for the commission of interference
  1 21 with official acts pursuant to the bill ranges from a serious
  1 22 misdemeanor through a class "C" felony depending upon the facts
  1 23 of the offense under Code section 719.1(2).
  1 24    A person who assaults a jailer may also be punished pursuant
  1 25 to the existing provisions of Code section 708.3A.
