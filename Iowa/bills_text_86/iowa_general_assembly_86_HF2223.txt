  2  9 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 10 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 11    This bill relates to public notice and the accessibility of
  2 12 meetings of governmental bodies.
  2 13    Under Code chapter 21 (Iowa's open meetings law), a
  2 14 governmental body is required to give reasonable notice of
  2 15 the time, date, and place of each meeting of the governmental
  2 16 body, and the tentative agenda of the meeting at least 24 hours
  2 17 before commencement of the meeting unless for good cause such
  2 18 notice is impossible or impractical. Each meeting shall be
  2 19 held at a place reasonably accessible to the public and at a
  2 20 time reasonably convenient to the public, unless for good cause
  2 21 such a place or time is impossible or impracticable.
  2 22    The bill provides an exception to the 24=hour notice
  2 23 requirement when a governmental body is prevented from
  2 24 convening an otherwise properly noticed meeting for any
  2 25 reason, the governmental body may convene the meeting if the
  2 26 governmental body posts an amended notice of the meeting
  2 27 conforming with the otherwise applicable notice requirements
  2 28 under the open meetings law.
