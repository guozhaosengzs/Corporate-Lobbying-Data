  2 31                           EXPLANATION
  2 32 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 33 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 34    Current law provides that fire apparatus, certain road
  2 35 maintenance equipment, certain implements of husbandry, and
  3  1 certain construction equipment are exempt from motor vehicle
  3  2 size, weight, and load limitations and the permit requirements
  3  3 of Code chapter 321E.  This bill expands that provision to also
  3  4 exempt law enforcement vehicles. The bill also provides that
  3  5 motor vehicle weight restrictions imposed by local authorities
  3  6 and the department of transportation shall not apply to law
  3  7 enforcement vehicles.
