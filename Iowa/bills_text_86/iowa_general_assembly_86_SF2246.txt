  3  3                           EXPLANATION
  3  4 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3  5 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3  6    This bill provides for the defeasance or redemption of
  3  7 vision Iowa program bonds and for the deposit of certain
  3  8 wagering tax receipts and other funds into the water quality
  3  9 initiative fund.
  3 10    Code section 8.57(5), concerning the distribution of
  3 11 wagering tax receipts received pursuant to Code sections 99D.17
  3 12 and 99F.11, is amended.  The bill provides that the annual
  3 13 fiscal year distribution of $15 million for deposit in the
  3 14 vision Iowa fund shall end following the distribution for FY
  3 15 2016=2017.  Instead, the bill provides that beginning with FY
  3 16 2017=2018, and each fiscal year thereafter, $15 million of
  3 17 wagering tax receipts will be deposited in the water quality
  3 18 initiative fund created in Code section 466B.45.
  3 19    Code section 8.57F, concerning the state bond repayment
  3 20 fund, is amended to allow moneys credited to and available in
  3 21 the fund to be used for the defeasance or redemption of vision
  3 22 Iowa program bonds along with costs related to the defeasance
  3 23 or redemption of such bonds.
  3 24    Concerning the state bond repayment fund, the bill provides
  3 25 for an appropriation of $30.4 million from the general fund
  3 26 for FY 2015=2016 to the treasurer of state for deposit in
  3 27 the state bond repayment fund. In addition, to the extent
  3 28 the vision Iowa program bonds are defeased or redeemed by
  3 29 moneys in the state bond repayment fund, transfers are made
  3 30 to the water quality initiative fund from the revenue source
  3 31 otherwise designated by law or existing practice for payment
  3 32 of the vision Iowa program bonds, an amount equal to that
  3 33 which otherwise would have been paid in connection with the
  3 34 bonds from such revenue source for FY 2016=2017 as provided
  3 35 in Code section 8.57.  The bill further provides that for
  4  1 FY 2017=2018, any unobligated and unencumbered moneys in the
  4  2 state bond repayment fund are appropriated to the department
  4  3 of agriculture and land stewardship for deposit in the water
  4  4 quality initiative fund.
  4  5    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
