  1 29 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 30 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 31    This bill extends veterans reporting requirements of
  1 32 postsecondary educational institutions to include certain
  1 33 members of the reserve forces of the United States and certain
  1 34 members of the national guard.
  1 35    Current law requires that community colleges, accredited
  2  1 private postsecondary institutions, and the board of regents
  2  2 file annual reports with the governor and the general assembly
  2  3 relating to the award of educational credits to veterans for
  2  4 military education, training, and experience. The bill amends
  2  5 the definition of veteran to include members of the reserve
  2  6 forces of the United States and of the national guard who have
  2  7 served at least one year of the service commitment and are
  2  8 eligible for, or have exhausted, federal education benefits for
  2  9 veterans.
