 SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION

  1 A Joint Resolution proposing an amendment to the Constitution
  2    of the State of Iowa providing that the people of the state
  3    have a right to hunt, fish, and harvest wildlife.
  4 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5371SV (1) 86
    av/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  The following amendment to the Constitution of
  1  2 the State of Iowa is proposed:
  1  3    Article I of the Constitution of the State of Iowa is amended
  1  4 by adding the following new section:
  1  5 Right to hunt, fish, and harvest wildlife.SEC. 24A.
  1  6    1.  The citizens of this state have the right to hunt, fish,
  1  7 and harvest wildlife, including by the use of traditional
  1  8 methods, subject to reasonable laws enacted by the general
  1  9 assembly and reasonable rules adopted by the natural resource
  1 10 commission to promote wildlife conservation and management,
  1 11 to maintain natural resources held in trust by the state for
  1 12 public use, and to preserve the future of hunting, fishing, and
  1 13 harvesting wildlife.
  1 14    2.  Public hunting, fishing, and harvesting of wildlife
  1 15 shall be a preferred means of managing and controlling
  1 16 wildlife.
  1 17    3.  This section shall not be construed to modify any
  1 18 provision of law relating to eminent domain, trespass, or
  1 19 property rights.
  1 20    Sec. 2.  REFERRAL AND PUBLICATION.  The foregoing proposed
  1 21 amendment to the Constitution of the State of Iowa is referred
  1 22 to the general assembly to be chosen at the next general
  1 23 election for members of the general assembly and the secretary
  1 24 of state is directed to cause it to be published for three
  1 25 consecutive months before the date of the election as provided
  1 26 by law.
  1 27                           EXPLANATION
  1 28 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 29 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 30    This joint resolution proposes an amendment to the
  1 31 Constitution of the State of Iowa providing that the citizens
  1 32 of this state have the right to hunt, fish, and harvest
  1 33 wildlife, using traditional methods, subject to reasonable
  1 34 laws enacted by the general assembly and reasonable rules
  1 35 adopted by the natural resource commission to promote wildlife
  2  1 conservation and management, to maintain natural resources
  2  2 held in trust by the state for public use, and to preserve
  2  3 the future of hunting, fishing, and harvesting wildlife.  The
  2  4 proposed amendment further states that hunting, fishing, and
  2  5 harvesting wildlife shall be a preferred means of managing and
  2  6 controlling wildlife and that the proposed amendment shall not
  2  7 be construed to modify any provision of law relating to eminent
  2  8 domain, trespass, or property rights.
  2  9    The proposed amendment, if adopted, would be referred to the
  2 10 next general assembly for adoption a second time, before being
  2 11 submitted to the electorate for ratification.
