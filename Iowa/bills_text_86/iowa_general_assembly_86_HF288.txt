  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    This bill allows a person to take catfish with a specified
  1 27 bow and arrow in state parks and preserves as provided by rule
  1 28 of the commission.  A violation of this provision is punishable
  1 29 with a scheduled fine of $50.  The bill also requires the
  1 30 natural resource commission to allow the taking of catfish by
  1 31 means of bow and arrow and with artificial light in a manner
  1 32 and at times and places as determined by the commission by
  1 33 rule.  A violation of this provision is punishable with a
  1 34 scheduled fine of $50.
  1 35    Currently, only the taking of rough fish in state parks
  2  1 and preserves is allowed by use of a specified bow and arrow
  2  2 as provided by rule of the commission, and the commission is
  2  3 allowed, but not required, to designate fish that can be taken
  2  4 by bow and arrow.
