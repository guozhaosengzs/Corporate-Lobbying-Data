  1 31 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 32 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 33    This bill allows hunting, taking, or attempting to take
  1 34 deer on or in a baited area on private property. A person may
  1 35 place, expose, deposit, distribute, or scatter feed on private
  2  1 property under the person's control or allow another person to
  2  2 do so.

  2  4 vegetables, nuts, hay, salt, mineral blocks, or any other
  2  5 natural food materials; commercial products containing natural
  2  6 food materials; or by=products of such materials, that are
  2  7 capable of luring, attracting, or enticing deer to a specific
  2  8 location. "Feed" does not include food placed during normal
  2  9 agricultural activities. A "baited area" is any area where
  2 10 feed is placed, exposed, deposited, distributed, or scattered
  2 11 with the intent to lure, attract, or entice deer to a specific
  2 12 location.
  2 13    A violation of the bill's provisions is subject to a
  2 14 scheduled fine of $100.
