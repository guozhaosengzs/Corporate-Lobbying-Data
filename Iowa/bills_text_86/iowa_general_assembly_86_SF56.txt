  1 27                           EXPLANATION
  1 28 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 29 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 30    This bill repeals Code chapter 103, providing for statewide
  1 31 licensure of electricians and electrical contractors.  The
  1 32 effect of this repeal would be to allow political subdivisions
  1 33 to administer and regulate electricians, electrical
  1 34 contractors, and electrical inspections. The bill directs the
  1 35 division of state fire marshal of the department of public
  2  1 safety to adopt rules providing for refunding license fees on
  2  2 a prorated basis corresponding to the period remaining after
  2  3 the repeal and prior to a license's expiration date. The
  2  4 bill makes conforming changes within Code chapter 100C, which
  2  5 regulates the licensing of fire extinguishing and alarm system
  2  6 contractors and installers and Code chapter 105 regulating
  2  7 plumbers, mechanical professionals, and contractors.
  2  8    Provisions of the bill regarding the adoption of rules for
  2  9 prorating license fees take effect July 1 after the bill's
  2 10 enactment. The remaining provisions of the bill take effect
  2 11 December 31, 2015.
