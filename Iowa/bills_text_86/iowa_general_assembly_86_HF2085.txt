  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    Code sections 321J.2 and 907.3 prohibit a court from
  1 27 deferring judgment, deferring a sentence, or suspending a
  1 28 sentence for a person convicted of a first offense of operating
  1 29 while intoxicated (OWI) if the offense resulted in bodily
  1 30 injury to another person. Code section 321J.2 also prohibits
  1 31 a court from reducing the fine for a first offense of OWI from
  1 32 $1,250 to $625 if the offense resulted in personal injury or
  1 33 property damage.
  1 34    This bill eliminates those prohibitions. Under the bill,
  1 35 a person convicted of a first offense of OWI is eligible to
  2  1 receive a deferred judgment, deferred sentence, or suspended
  2  2 sentence, and a reduced fine, regardless of whether the offense
  2  3 resulted in personal injury or property damage, as long as
  2  4 the person meets the other requirements for receiving those
  2  5 benefits.
