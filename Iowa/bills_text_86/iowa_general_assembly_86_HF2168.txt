  1 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 24 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 25    This bill modifies penalties for trespassing.
  1 26    Under current law, a trespass that does not involve injury
  1 27 to a person or damages of more than $200, an intent to commit a
  1 28 hate crime, or public utility property, is a simple misdemeanor
  1 29 and is punishable by a fine between $65 and $625, as determined
  1 30 by a court. A court may also order imprisonment not to exceed
  1 31 30 days in lieu of or in addition to a fine.
  1 32    The bill amends current law by modifying the penalty for
  1 33 a trespass which does not involve damages of more than $200,
  1 34 an intent to commit a hate crime, or public utility property.
  1 35 Under the bill, such a trespass remains a simple misdemeanor,
  2  1 but is punishable as a scheduled fine. The fine schedule is
  2  2 $200 for the first violation, $500 for the second violation,
  2  3 and $1,000 for third or subsequent violations.
