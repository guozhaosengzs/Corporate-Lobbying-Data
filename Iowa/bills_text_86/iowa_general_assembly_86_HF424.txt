 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act making text=messaging while driving a primary offense.
  2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2312YH (2) 86
    ns/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 321.276, subsection 5, Code 2015, is
  1  2 amended by striking the subsection.
  1  3                           EXPLANATION
  1  4 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  5 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  6    Current law provides that a peace officer shall not stop
  1  7 or detain a person solely for a suspected violation of Code
  1  8 section 321.276, which prohibits text=messaging while driving.
  1  9 Code section 321.276 is enforceable by a peace officer only as
  1 10 a secondary action when the driver of a motor vehicle has been
  1 11 stopped or detained for a suspected violation of another law.
  1 12    This bill strikes these provisions and makes a violation of
  1 13 Code section 321.276 a primary offense. The scheduled fine for
  1 14 violating Code section 321.276 is $30.
       LSB 2312YH (2) 86
       ns/nh
