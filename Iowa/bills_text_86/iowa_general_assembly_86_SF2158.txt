  1 26 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 27 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 28    This bill relates to the placement of shorthand reporters in
  1 29 exempt status and to the supervision of the board of examiners
  1 30 of shorthand reporters.
  1 31    The bill provides that the supreme court shall supervise the
  1 32 board of examiners of shorthand reporters and have authority to
  1 33 review, approve, modify, or reject a board action, procedure,
  1 34 or decision. The bill also provides that the supreme court
  1 35 may adopt rules relating to the supervision of the board of
  2  1 examiners of shorthand reporters.
  2  2    The bill provides that if a shorthand reporter's
  2  3 certification is placed in exempt status, the reporter
  2  4 may nonetheless transcribe or certify a proceeding that
  2  5 was reported while the reporter was certified as an active
  2  6 shorthand reporter.  A reporter transcribing or certifying a
  2  7 proceeding while in exempt status shall remain subject to the
  2  8 jurisdiction of the board of examiners of shorthand reporters.
