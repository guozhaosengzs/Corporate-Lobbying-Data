  1 15                           EXPLANATION
  1 16 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 17 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 18    This bill allows a hunter to carry a handgun while hunting.
  1 19    The bill adds new Code section 481A.48(6) which provides
  1 20 that a person who legally possesses a handgun may carry that
  1 21 handgun while hunting during any hunting season so long as
  1 22 the person is otherwise legally qualified to carry a handgun.
  1 23 However, a person shall not use a handgun to hunt unless the
  1 24 person has a valid hunting license with an unfilled tag that
  1 25 permits the use of that handgun to hunt during the season in
  1 26 which the handgun is being used.  A violation of the bill's
  1 27 provisions is punishable with a scheduled fine of $100.
