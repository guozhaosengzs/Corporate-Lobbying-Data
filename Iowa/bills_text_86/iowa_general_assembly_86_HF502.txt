  1  1    Section 1.    1  1    Section 1.    1  2 access to certain personal information == internet sites.
  1  3    1.  Notwithstanding chapter 22, an individual may file a
  1  4 written request with a local official on a form prescribed by
  1  5 the department of public safety, in consultation with the Iowa
  1  6 county recorders association and the Iowa state association
  1  7 of assessors, to prohibit the general public from accessing,
  1  8 on an internet site maintained by the local official, the
  1  9 individual's name, residential address, and telephone number,
  1 10 as identified by the individual, that are contained in
  1 11 instruments, books, records, indexes, and other information
  1 12 under the authority of the local official.
  1 13    2.  Within fifteen days of receiving the request, the
  1 14 local official shall ensure that the individual's information
  1 15 identified by the individual under subsection 1 does not appear
  1 16 in search results on an internet site maintained by the local
  1 17 official.
  1 18    3.  For purposes of this section, "local official" means a
  1 19 county auditor, county treasurer, county recorder, or local
  1 20 assessor.
  1 21    Sec. 2.  IMPLEMENTATION OF ACT.  Section 25B.2, subsection
  1 22 3, shall not apply to this Act.
  1 23    Sec. 3.  EFFECTIVE UPON ENACTMENT.  This Act, being deemed of
  1 24 immediate importance, takes effect upon enactment.
  1 25                           EXPLANATION
  1 26 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 27 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 28    This bill provides that an individual may file a written
  1 29 request with one or more local officials to prohibit the
  1 30 general public from accessing on an internet site maintained by
  1 31 the local official the individual's name, residential address,
  1 32 and telephone number, as identified by the individual, that are
  1 33 contained in instruments, books, records, indexes, and other
  1 34 information under the authority of the local official.
  1 35    The bill requires the local official to, within 15 days of
  2  1 receiving a request, ensure that the individual's information
  2  2 does not appear in search results on an internet site
  2  3 maintained by the local official.
  2  4    The bill may include a state mandate as defined in Code
  2  5 section 25B.3.  The bill makes inapplicable Code section 25B.2,
  2  6 subsection 3, which would relieve a political subdivision from
  2  7 complying with a state mandate if funding for the cost of
  2  8 the state mandate is not provided or specified.  Therefore,
  2  9 political subdivisions are required to comply with any state
  2 10 mandate included in the bill.
  2 11    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
