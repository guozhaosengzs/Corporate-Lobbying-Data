  1 22 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 23 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 24    This bill authorizes the division of soil conservation
  1 25 and water conservation of the department of agriculture and
  1 26 land stewardship to establish projects and award project
  1 27 grants to support privately sponsored innovative water quality
  1 28 practices, including new methods to reduce the transport of
  1 29 nutrients to surface water from nonpoint sources. The division
  1 30 would administer the grant project as part of the state's
  1 31 water quality initiative. The division supports initiative
  1 32 projects by using moneys appropriated to the water quality
  1 33 initiative fund (Code section 466B.45).  Moneys in the fund
  1 34 are used to finance projects in subwatersheds that are part
  1 35 of high=priority watersheds identified by the water resources
  2  1 coordinating council (2015 Iowa Acts, chapter 132, sections 8
  2  2 and 31).
