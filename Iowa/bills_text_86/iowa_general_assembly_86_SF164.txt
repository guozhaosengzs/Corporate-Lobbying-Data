  1  9 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 10 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 11    Code section 400.1 currently requires a city having a
  1 12 population of 8,000 or more according to the federal census
  1 13 conducted in 1980 and having a paid fire department or a paid
  1 14 police department, to appoint a civil service commission. Code
  1 15 chapter 400 also establishes several other powers and duties of
  1 16 cities based on population as determined by the federal census
  1 17 conducted in 1980.
  1 18    This bill requires the most recent decennial federal census
  1 19 to be used in determining the population of a city.
  1 20    The bill may include a state mandate as defined in Code
  1 21 section 25B.3.  The bill makes inapplicable Code section 25B.2,
  1 22 subsection 3, which would relieve a political subdivision from
  1 23 complying with a state mandate if funding for the cost of
  1 24 the state mandate is not provided or specified.  Therefore,
  1 25 political subdivisions are required to comply with any state
  1 26 mandate included in the bill.
