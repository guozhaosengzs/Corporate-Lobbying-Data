  2 30                           EXPLANATION
  2 31 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 32 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 33    This bill relates to continuation or reenrollment of certain
  2 34 adult children of insureds or enrollees in accident or health
  2 35 care coverage.  The bill requires accident or health care
  3  1 coverage to allow an adult child to continue or reenroll in
  3  2 such coverage regardless of whether the child is married or
  3  3 is a resident of the state, until the date the child attains
  3  4 the age of 26 years old.  The bill also deletes a provision
  3  5 that requires such accident or health care coverage to allow an
  3  6 unmarried child to continue or reenroll in such coverage for
  3  7 so long as the unmarried child is a full=time student in an
  3  8 accredited institution of postsecondary education.
  3  9    The bill is effective upon enactment.
