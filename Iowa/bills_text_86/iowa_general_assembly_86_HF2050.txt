  1  3    An employee of the department of human services shall not
  1  4 provide advance notice of a drug test to an individual required
  1  5 to submit to a drug test for purposes of participating in a
  1  6 program administered by the department.  Any person knowingly
  1  7 violating or attempting to violate this section shall be guilty
  1  8 of a simple misdemeanor.

  1 10 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 11 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 12    This bill prohibits an employee of the department of human
  1 13 services from providing advance notice to an individual
  1 14 required to submit to a drug test for purposes of participating
  1 15 in a program administered by the department. The bill provides
  1 16 that a person violating this new Code section is guilty of
  1 17 a simple misdemeanor. A simple misdemeanor is punishable by
  1 18 confinement for no more than 30 days or a fine of at least $65
  1 19 but not more than $625 or by both.
