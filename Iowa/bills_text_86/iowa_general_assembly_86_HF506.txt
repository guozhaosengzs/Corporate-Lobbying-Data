  2 13 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 14 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 15    This bill relates to absentee ballot deadlines. Under
  2 16 current law, an absentee ballot received by a county
  2 17 commissioner of elections is valid if it is received by the
  2 18 commissioner before the polls close on election day or if it is
  2 19 clearly postmarked by an officially authorized postal service
  2 20 not later than the day before the election and is received by
  2 21 the commissioner not later than noon on the Monday following
  2 22 the election. Under the bill, for an absentee ballot to be
  2 23 valid it must be received by the commissioner before the polls
  2 24 close on election day, unless the absentee ballot is completed
  2 25 by a military or overseas voter. The bill provides that for
  2 26 a military or overseas voter's ballot to be valid, it must be
  2 27 received by noon on the day before the supervisors canvass the
  2 28 votes.
  2 29    The bill makes additional corresponding changes.
