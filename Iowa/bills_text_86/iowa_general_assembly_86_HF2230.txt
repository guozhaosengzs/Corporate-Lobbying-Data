  2 13 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 14 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 15    This bill requires the board of directors of a school
  2 16 district to integrate biennial, evidence=based training on
  2 17 suicide prevention for all school personnel who hold a license,
  2 18 certificate, authorization, or statement of recognition
  2 19 issued by the board of educational examiners and who have
  2 20 regular contact with students in grades 6 through 12 into the
  2 21 school district's comprehensive school improvement plan by
  2 22 July 1, 2017. The content of the training shall be based on
  2 23 nationally recognized best practices.  The bill provides a
  2 24 similar requirement for training on trauma=informed care for
  2 25 school personnel who have regular contact with students in
  2 26 kindergarten through grade 12.
  2 27    The bill requires the department of education to adopt rules
  2 28 by July 1, 2017, to require school districts to adopt protocols
  2 29 for suicide prevention and postvention and trauma=informed care
  2 30 based on nationally recognized best practices.
  2 31    The bill defines "trauma=informed care" as services that are
  2 32 based on an understanding of the vulnerabilities and triggers
  2 33 of individuals who have experienced trauma, recognize the role
  2 34 trauma has played in the lives of those individuals, recognize
  2 35 the presence of trauma symptoms and their onset, are supportive
  3  1 of trauma recovery, and avoid further traumatization. The bill
  3  2 defines "postvention" as the provision of crisis intervention,
  3  3 support, and assistance for those affected by a suicide or
  3  4 suicide attempt to prevent further risk of suicide.
  3  5    The bill may include a state mandate as defined in Code
  3  6 section 25B.3.  The bill makes inapplicable Code section 25B.2,
  3  7 subsection 3, which would relieve a political subdivision from
  3  8 complying with a state mandate if funding for the cost of
  3  9 the state mandate is not provided or specified.  Therefore,
  3 10 political subdivisions are required to comply with any state
  3 11 mandate included in the bill.
