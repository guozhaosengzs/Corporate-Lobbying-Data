  2  8                           EXPLANATION
  2  9 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 10 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 11    This bill requires the department of transportation, in
  2 12 consultation with the department of public safety, to establish
  2 13 a certified driver safety education course to be offered
  2 14 throughout the state.  A person who successfully completes the
  2 15 driver safety education course shall be issued a certificate.
  2 16    Beginning January 1, 2016, the commissioner of insurance
  2 17 shall require insurers transacting business in this state to
  2 18 provide a 10 percent automobile insurance premium reduction
  2 19 for persons 55 years of age or older who have obtained a
  2 20 driver safety education course certificate within the last
  2 21 three years.  The premium reduction may be prorated based on
  2 22 the number of insured drivers under the policy who qualify
  2 23 for the reduction.  In addition, the insurer may revoke the
  2 24 premium reduction for a driver who is involved in a motor
  2 25 vehicle accident if the driver committed a violation of the
  2 26 state's motor vehicle laws that was a contributing factor in
  2 27 the accident.
  2 28    The bill authorizes the department of transportation to
  2 29 develop refresher courses which may be taken by a person in
  2 30 lieu of the full driver safety education course in order to
  2 31 continue to qualify for the insurance premium reduction.
