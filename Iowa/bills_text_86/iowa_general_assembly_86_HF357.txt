  1 21    Sec. 3.  LOCAL FOOD AND FARM INNOVATION FUND ==== APPROPRIATION
  1 22 TO SUPPORT FOOD HUBS OR FARMING INNOVATION ZONES PROJECTS.
  1 23    1.  There is appropriated from the general fund of the state
  1 24 to the local food and farm innovation fund created in section
  1 25 267A.5, as amended by this Act, for the fiscal year beginning
  1 26 July 1, 2015, and ending June 30, 2016, the following amount,
  1 27 or so much thereof as is necessary, to be used for the purposes
  1 28 designated:
  1 29    For purposes of supporting food hubs or farming innovation
  1 30 zones projects as provided in this section:
  1 31 .................................................. $  1,000,000
  1 32    2.  Moneys appropriated in subsection 1 shall be allocated by
  1 33 the local food and farm program council established pursuant
  1 34 to section 267A.3 to support projects for the development or
  1 35 expansion of food hubs or farming innovation zones in this
  2  1 state.
  2  2    a.  A food hub must be a centrally located facility with a
  2  3 business management structure facilitating the aggregation,
  2  4 storage, processing, distribution, or marketing of locally or
  2  5 regionally produced food or food products.
  2  6    b.  A farming innovation zone must be a publicly recognized
  2  7 location for research, development, demonstration, and
  2  8 education that does all of the following:
  2  9    (1)  Prepares beginning farmers for the production of
  2 10 diversified food products in Iowa for processing, wholesaling,
  2 11 and retailing on a local or regional basis.
  2 12    (2)  Fosters the diversification of farm operations in which
  2 13 existing producers are engaged.
  2 14    (3)  Supports the creation and expansion of production and
  2 15 market infrastructure for a local foods economy, including but
  2 16 not limited to food hubs.
  2 17    3.  Notwithstanding section 8.33, moneys that remain
  2 18 unencumbered or unobligated at the end of the fiscal year shall
  2 19 not revert but shall remain available to support the purposes
  2 20 of this section for the succeeding fiscal year.
