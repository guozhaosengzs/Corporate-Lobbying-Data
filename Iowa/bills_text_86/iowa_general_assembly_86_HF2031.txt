  1 14                           EXPLANATION
  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    This bill relates to the limitation of action (statute of
  1 18 limitations) for the criminal offense of accessory after the
  1 19 fact involving kidnapping.
  1 20    The bill provides that an indictment or information for
  1 21 the offense of accessory after the fact in violation of Code
  1 22 section 703.3 that involves a kidnapping in violation of Code
  1 23 section 710.2 (kidnapping in the first degree), Code section
  1 24 710.3 (kidnapping in the second degree), or Code section 710.4
  1 25 (kidnapping in the third degree) shall be found within 10
  1 26 years after the commission of the offense of accessory after
  1 27 the fact.  Current law provides that such violation shall be
  1 28 found within three years after the commission of the offense of
  1 29 accessory after the fact.
  1 30    A person who commits the offense of accessory after the fact
  1 31 involving kidnapping in the first degree, second degree, or
  1 32 third degree commits an aggravated misdemeanor.
  1 33    An aggravated misdemeanor is punishable by confinement for
  1 34 no more than two years and a fine of at least $625 but not more
  1 35 than $6,250.
