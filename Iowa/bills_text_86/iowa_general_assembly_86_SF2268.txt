  1  8 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with

  1 10    This bill relates to the filing of applications to modify the
  1 11 sex offender registration requirements of Code chapter 692A.
  1 12    The bill provides that a sex offender applying to modify the
  1 13 sex offender registry requirements pursuant to Code section
  1 14 692A.128 may file the application in the county where the
  1 15 conviction for the sex offense occurred if the offender has
  1 16 been placed on inactive status under Code section 692A.106(7)
  1 17 and no longer resides in this state. Current law requires
  1 18 the application to be filed in the sex offender's county of
  1 19 principal residence.
