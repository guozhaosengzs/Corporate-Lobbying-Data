  1 35 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  1 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  2    This bill requires that the state building code provide
  2  3 uniform standards and requirements for construction,
  2  4 construction materials, and equipment through the adoption
  2  5 by reference of the most recent international building code
  2  6 developed by the international code council. The bill provides
  2  7 that the state building code is applicable to all newly
  2  8 constructed residential and all newly constructed or renovated
  2  9 commercial buildings and structures in the state. The bill
  2 10 also provides that a governmental subdivision of the state may
  2 11 adopt and enforce higher standards than those required under
  2 12 the state building code.
  2 13    The bill may include a state mandate as defined in Code
  2 14 section 25B.3.  The bill makes inapplicable Code section 25B.2,
  2 15 subsection 3, which would relieve a political subdivision from
  2 16 complying with a state mandate if funding for the cost of
  2 17 the state mandate is not provided or specified.  Therefore,
  2 18 political subdivisions are required to comply with any state
  2 19 mandate included in the bill.
