  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    This bill increases penalties for littering.
  1 23    Under Code section 455B.363, a person shall not discard
  1 24 any litter onto or in any water or land of this state. Under
  1 25 current law, a person who violates Code section 455B.363 is
  1 26 guilty of a simple misdemeanor, and shall be fined at least $65
  1 27 but no more than $625. The bill provides that a person who
  1 28 violates Code section 455B.363 shall be fined at least $250.
  1 29    Under Code sections 321.369 and 321.370, a person shall not
  1 30 litter on a highway. Under current law, the scheduled fine for
  1 31 violating Code sections 321.369 and 321.370 is $70, and the
  1 32 bill increases the scheduled fine to $250.
  1 33    Under Code section 461A.43, a person shall not litter in
  1 34 a state park or preserve. Under current law, the scheduled
  1 35 fine for violating Code section 461A.43 is $30, and the bill
  2  1 increases the scheduled fine to $250.
