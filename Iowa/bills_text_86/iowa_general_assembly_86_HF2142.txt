  1  3    The sovereign state of Iowa recognizes that life is valued
  1  4 and protected from the moment of conception, and each life,
  1  5 from that moment, is accorded the same rights and protections
  1  6 guaranteed to all persons by the Constitution of the United
  1  7 States, the Constitution of the State of Iowa, and the laws of
  1  8 this state.  The Iowa supreme court shall not have appellate
  1  9 jurisdiction over the provisions of this section.
  1 10    Sec. 2.  EFFECTIVE UPON ENACTMENT.  This Act, being deemed of
  1 11 immediate importance, takes effect upon enactment.
  1 12                           EXPLANATION
  1 13 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 14 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 15    This bill establishes that the sovereign state of Iowa
  1 16 recognizes that life is valued and protected from the moment
  1 17 of conception and each life is accorded the same rights and
  1 18 protections guaranteed to all persons by the constitutions of
  1 19 the state of Iowa and the United States, and by the laws of
  1 20 the state.  The bill also provides that the Iowa supreme court
  1 21 does not have appellate jurisdiction over the provisions of the
  1 22 bill.
  1 23    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
