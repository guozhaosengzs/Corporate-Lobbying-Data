  3 15                           EXPLANATION
  3 16 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  3 17 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  3 18    This bill relates to the filling of school district board
  3 19 vacancies.
  3 20    The bill maintains current law for the filling of a vacancy
  3 21 that occurs not later than 45 days before a regular school
  3 22 election. The bill also maintains current law requiring that
  3 23 the school board fill all other vacancies occurring among the
  3 24 officers and members of the school board by appointment.
  3 25    The bill requires that the board publish notice of the intent
  3 26 of the board to fill a vacancy among the members of a school
  3 27 board by appointment, but that the electors of the school
  3 28 district have the right to file a petition requiring that the
  3 29 vacancy be filled by a special election.  The bill allows the
  3 30 board to publish the notice in advance of a vacancy if the
  3 31 officer or member of the board submits a resignation that will
  3 32 take effect at a future date. The bill allows the board to
  3 33 make an appointment after the notice is published or after the
  3 34 vacancy occurs, whichever is later.  The bill provides that the
  3 35 appointment is considered temporary if a valid petition to call
  4  1 a special election is filed with the secretary of the school
  4  2 board within 14 days after publication of the notice or within
  4  3 14 days after the appointment is made.
  4  4    The bill provides that a valid petition must be signed by
  4  5 at least 100 eligible electors or eligible electors equaling
  4  6 at least 30 percent of the number of voters at the preceding
  4  7 regular school election, whichever is greater.
  4  8    The bill requires that a special election be called by the
  4  9 school board upon the filing of a valid petition for a vacancy
  4 10 that occurs more than 180 days before the next regular school
  4 11 election. The special election shall be held in accordance
  4 12 with requirements under current law for filling school district
  4 13 board vacancies. The bill requires that a school district
  4 14 fill such a vacancy at the next regular school election if a
  4 15 valid petition is filed for a vacancy that occurs 180 days or
  4 16 less before the next regular school election. If the board
  4 17 is reduced below a quorum, the secretary of the board, or if
  4 18 there is no secretary, the area education agency administrator,
  4 19 shall call a special election in the district, subdistrict, or
  4 20 subdistricts, as the case may be, to fill the vacancies.
