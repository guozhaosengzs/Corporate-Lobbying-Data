  2 14 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 15 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 16    This bill requires the board of directors of each public
  2 17 school district to provide for short=term testing and retesting
  2 18 for radon gas at each attendance center under its control.
  2 19 The rules relating to the testing are to be adopted by the
  2 20 departments of education and public health.
  2 21    The bill adds radon testing pursuant to the bill to the list
  2 22 of permissible uses of a physical plant and equipment levy and
  2 23 revenues from the secure an advanced vision for education fund
  2 24 by a school district.
  2 25    The bill may include a state mandate as defined in Code
  2 26 section 25B.3.  The bill provides that the cost of any state
  2 27 mandate included in the bill may be paid by a school district
  2 28 from a physical plant and equipment levy under Code chapter
  2 29 298, as amended by the bill, the secure an advanced vision for
  2 30 education fund under Code chapter 423F, as amended by the bill,
  2 31 or a modified supplemental amount under Code section 257.31,
  2 32 subsection 6.  The specification is deemed to constitute state
  2 33 compliance with any state mandate funding=related requirements
  2 34 of Code section 25B.2.  The inclusion of this specification is
  2 35 intended to reinstate the requirement of political subdivisions
  3  1 to comply with any state mandates included in the bill.
