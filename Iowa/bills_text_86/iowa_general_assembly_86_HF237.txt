  1 11    1.  As used in this section, "peace officer" means the same
  1 12 as defined in section 801.4.
  1 13    2.  If the actions of a peace officer, under color of law,
  1 14 result in the death of another, the attorney general shall be
  1 15 designated a special prosecutor and convene a grand jury to
  1 16 investigate the incident unless the attorney general proceeds
  1 17 by the filing of a trial information. The county attorney
  1 18 shall not have jurisdiction over the prosecution of the peace
  1 19 officer.
  1 20    3.  The county where the incident occurred shall pay the
  1 21 expenses of the attorney general in the prosecution of the
  1 22 peace officer.
  1 23    Sec. 3.  IMPLEMENTATION OF ACT.  Section 25B.2, subsection
  1 24 3, shall not apply to this Act.
  1 25                           EXPLANATION
  1 26 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 27 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 28    This bill relates to the appointment of a special prosecutor
  1 29 after a death results from the actions, under color of law, of
  1 30 a peace officer.
  1 31    Under the bill, if the actions of a peace officer as defined
  1 32 in Code section 801.4 and under color of law result in the
  1 33 death of another, the attorney general shall be designated a
  1 34 special prosecutor and convene a grand jury to investigate the
  1 35 incident unless the attorney general proceeds by the filing
  2  1 of a trial information. The bill specifies that the county
  2  2 attorney shall not have jurisdiction over the prosecution.
  2  3    The bill further specifies that the county where the
  2  4 incident occurred shall pay the expenses of the attorney
  2  5 general in the prosecution of the peace officer.
  2  6    The bill may include a state mandate as defined in Code
  2  7 section 25B.3.  The bill makes inapplicable Code section 25B.2,
  2  8 subsection 3, which would relieve a political subdivision from
  2  9 complying with a state mandate if funding for the cost of
  2 10 the state mandate is not provided or specified.  Therefore,
  2 11 political subdivisions are required to comply with any state
  2 12 mandate included in the bill.
