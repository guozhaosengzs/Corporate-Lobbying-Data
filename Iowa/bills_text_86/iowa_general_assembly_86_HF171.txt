  2 14    This bill establishes a notification requirement for
  2 15 mammogram reports to patients.  The bill directs the department
  2 16 of public health to adopt rules that require a facility
  2 17 performing mammography services to include information on
  2 18 breast density in reports sent to patients pursuant to federal
  2 19 law and rules.  If a patient is categorized by the facility
  2 20 as having heterogeneously dense breasts or extremely dense
  2 21 breasts based on national standards the report to the patient
  2 22 must include notice that the patient has dense breast tissue,
  2 23 that this may make it more difficult to detect cancer on a
  2 24 mammogram, and that it may increase the patient's risk of
  2 25 breast cancer.  The bill provides language that such notice may
  2 26 contain.
  2 27    The bill's provisions are not to be construed to impose
  2 28 liability on the facility performing mammography services
  2 29 beyond the duty to provide the breast density information.
  2 30    Facilities providing mammography services must comply with
  2 31 the bill's requirements by January 1, 2016.
