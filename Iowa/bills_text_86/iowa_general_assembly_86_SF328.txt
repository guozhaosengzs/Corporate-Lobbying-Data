  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    This bill relates to absentee ballot deadlines. Under
  1 18 current law, an absentee ballot received by a county
  1 19 commissioner of elections is valid if it is received by the
  1 20 commissioner before the polls close on election day or if it is
  1 21 clearly postmarked by an officially authorized postal service
  1 22 not later than the day before the election and is received by
  1 23 the commissioner not later than noon on the Monday following
  1 24 the election. Under the bill, for an absentee ballot to be
  1 25 counted it must be received in the commissioner's office
  1 26 before the polls close on election day or it must be clearly
  1 27 postmarked not later than the day before the election and be
  1 28 received by the commissioner by noon on the Monday following
  1 29 the election or it must be received by the commissioner through
  1 30 an officially authorized postal service not later than 5:00
  1 31 p.m. on the day after the election.
