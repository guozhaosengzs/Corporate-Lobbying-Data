  1 34                           EXPLANATION
  1 35 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  1 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  2    This bill amends Code section 481A.48(5) to provide that
  2  3 a person with a valid permit to carry weapons may possess a
  2  4 pistol or revolver pursuant to that permit while hunting deer.
  2  5 However, a person shall not use a pistol or revolver to hunt
  2  6 deer unless the pistol or revolver meets the specifications of
  2  7 the statute and the person has a valid hunting license with an
  2  8 unfilled tag that permits use of that pistol or revolver to
  2  9 hunt deer. A person using a pistol or revolver in violation of
  2 10 the bill's provisions commits a scheduled violation punishable
  2 11 with a fine of $100.
