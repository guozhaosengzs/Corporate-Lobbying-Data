  1 10    The bill changes the criminal penalty for invasion

  1 12 misdemeanor. An aggravated misdemeanor is punishable by

  1 18 903B.2. The bill continues those consequences for the offense
  1 19 of invasion of privacy punishable as an aggravated misdemeanor.
