  2 31    This bill provides that a county board of supervisors
  2 32 may elect not to accept certain documents related to the
  2 33 regulation of animal feeding operations, and specifically
  2 34 confinement feeding operations under the "Animal Agriculture
  2 35 Compliance Act" (Code chapter 459).  Generally, the Code
  3  1 chapter is administered and enforced by the department
  3  2 of natural resources.  The bill allows a county board of
  3  3 supervisors to adopt a resolution to forego receiving an
  3  4 application or related materials for a permit to construct a
  3  5 confinement feeding operation structure (Code section 459.303
  3  6 or 459.304) or a manure management plan governing the storage
  3  7 and application of manure from a confinement feeding operation
  3  8 (Code section 459.312).  The board may request the department
  3  9 to transmit the document to the county if it is not available
  3 10 on the department's internet site.
