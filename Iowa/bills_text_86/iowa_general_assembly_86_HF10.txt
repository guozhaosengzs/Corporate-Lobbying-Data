  1 27 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 28 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 29    This bill eliminates the ability of an applicant for a
  1 30 liquor control license, wine permit, or beer permit to appeal
  1 31 the denial of the license or permit by the applicable local
  1 32 authority to the administrator of the alcoholic beverages
  1 33 division of the department of commerce.  Instead, the bill
  1 34 provides that an applicant may seek judicial review of the
  1 35 denial of the license or permit by filing an action in the
  2  1 district court of the county where the premises covered by the
