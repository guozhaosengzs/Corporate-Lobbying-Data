 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to Medicaid special needs trusts.
  2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 1066YH (3) 86
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 633C.1, subsection 8, Code 2015, is
  1  2 amended by striking the subsection.
  1  3    Sec. 2.  Section 633C.2, Code 2015, is amended by striking
  1  4 the section and inserting in lieu thereof the following:
  1  5    633C.2  Disposition of medical assistance special needs
  1  6 trusts.
  1  7    Any income or assets added to or received by and any income
  1  8 or principal retained in a medical assistance special needs
  1  9 trust shall be used in accordance with a standard that is
  1 10 no more restrictive than specified under federal law.  The
  1 11 department shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 17A for the
  1 12 establishment and disposition of medical assistance special
  1 13 needs trusts in accordance with this section.
  1 14                           EXPLANATION
  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    This bill amends provisions relating to Medicaid special
  1 18 needs trusts. The bill eliminates the definition of "special
  1 19 needs of the beneficiary attributable to the beneficiary's
  1 20 disability" which was the basis for the specifications for the
  1 21 disposition of the corpus of the trust, and replaces these
  1 22 specifications with a requirement that any income or assets
  1 23 added to or received by and any income or principal retained
  1 24 in a medical assistance special needs trust shall be used in
  1 25 accordance with a standard that is no more restrictive than
  1 26 specified under federal law for such trusts.  The bill directs
  1 27 the department of human services to adopt rules pursuant to
  1 28 Code chapter 17A for the establishment and disposition of
  1 29 medical assistance special needs trusts in accordance with the
  1 30 specifications of the bill.
