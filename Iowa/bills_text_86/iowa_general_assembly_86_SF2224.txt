  1 35 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  1 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  2    Code section 321.281 prohibits a person operating a motor
  2  3 vehicle from steering the vehicle unreasonably close to or
  2  4 toward a person riding a bicycle on a highway. This bill
  2  5 broadens that prohibition to include a person riding a bicycle
  2  6 in a lane designated for the travel of bicycles.
  2  7    The bill requires the driver of a vehicle overtaking a person
  2  8 riding a bicycle proceeding in the same direction on a roadway
  2  9 to pass to the left of the bicycle in an adjacent travel lane or
  2 10 on the opposite side of the roadway, and prohibits the driver
  2 11 from returning to the right side of the roadway until safely
  2 12 clear of the overtaken bicycle in accordance with Code section
  2 13 321.281. This requirement does not apply if the bicycle is
  2 14 traveling on a paved shoulder or in a lane designated for the
  2 15 travel of bicycles.
  2 16    Under current law, a violation of Code section 321.281 is
  2 17 punishable by a scheduled fine of $250 and a violation of Code
  2 18 section 321.299 is punishable by a scheduled fine of $100.
