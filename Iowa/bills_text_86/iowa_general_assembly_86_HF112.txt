  1 23      1 23      1 24                           EXPLANATION
  1 25 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 26 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 27    This bill prohibits a person from operating a motor vehicle
  1 28 with an exhaust system that produces engine noise of 95
  1 29 decibels or more unless the person has reasonable grounds to
  1 30 believe that the operation of the vehicle will not result
  1 31 in the vehicle's exhaust system producing engine noise of
  1 32 95 decibels or more. The bill also prohibits a person from
  1 33 modifying the exhaust system of a motor vehicle to produce
  1 34 engine noise of 95 decibels or more. The bill provides that
  1 35 the decibel output of the exhaust system of a motor vehicle
  2  1 shall be measured at a distance of 50 feet in a manner that
  2  2 complies with the standards and testing procedures established
  2  3 by the society of automotive engineers.
  2  4    The bill does not apply to motorcycles.
  2  5    The bill provides that the scheduled fine for violating
  2  6 these provisions is $20.
