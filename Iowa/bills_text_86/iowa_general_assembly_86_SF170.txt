  1 35 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  1 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  2    This bill affects public construction projects set for
  2  3 bidding.
  2  4    Currently, the state board of regents is exempted from Code
  2  5 chapter 26, the Iowa construction bidding procedures Act,
  2  6 through a specific exemption in the chapter's definition of
  2  7 "governmental entity". The bill eliminates the exemption for
  2  8 the state board of regents, thus requiring the state board of
  2  9 regents to comply with the requirements established by the Code
  2 10 chapter.
  2 11    The bill also defines "construction manager", "general
  2 12 contractor", and "public school corporation".
  2 13    Currently, when a governmental entity seeks separate bids
  2 14 for parts of a public improvement project, general contractors
  2 15 may be prohibited from submitting bids for the project as a
  2 16 whole. The bill requires general contractors to be allowed
  2 17 to submit bids for an entire project in instances where the
  2 18 governmental entity has decided to accept bids for separate
  2 19 parts of the public improvement project.
