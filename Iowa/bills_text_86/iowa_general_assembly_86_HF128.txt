  1 14 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 15    Current law prohibits the owner of a motor vehicle from
  1 16 allowing the operation of the motor vehicle by a person whose
  1 17 driver's license has been suspended, denied, revoked, or
  1 18 barred due to a conviction for operating=while=intoxicated. A
  1 19 violation of this provision is a simple misdemeanor. A simple
  1 20 misdemeanor is punishable by up to 30 days in prison and a $625
  1 21 fine under Code section 903.1.
  1 22    This bill increases the penalty for a violation of the
  1 23 provision to a serious misdemeanor. A serious misdemeanor is
  1 24 punishable by up to one year in prison and a $1,875 fine under
  1 25 Code section 903.1.
