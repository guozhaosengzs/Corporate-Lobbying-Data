PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  HOSPITAL AND LONG=TERM CARE PHARMACY PRACTICE ==
  1  2 PNEUMOCOCCAL VACCINES == BOARD OF PHARMACY RULES.  The board
  1  3 of pharmacy shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 17A relating
  1  4 to hospital and long=term care pharmacy practices that allow,
  1  5 as authorized by federal law, that in addition to influenza
  1  6 and pneumococcal polysaccharide vaccines, a written or verbal
  1  7 patient=specific medication administration order shall not
  1  8 be required prior to administration to an adult patient of
  1  9 pneumococcal conjugate vaccine pursuant to physician=approved
  1 10 hospital or facility policy and after the patient has been
  1 11 assessed for contraindications.
  1 12                           EXPLANATION
  1 13 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 14 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 15    This bill directs the board of pharmacy to adopt rules
  1 16 pursuant to chapter 17A relating to hospital and long=term
  1 17 care pharmacy practice that allow, as authorized by
  1 18 federal law, that in addition to influenza and pneumococcal
  1 19 polysaccharide vaccines, a written or verbal patient=specific
  1 20 medication administration order shall not be required
  1 21 prior to administration to an adult patient of pneumococcal
  1 22 conjugate vaccine pursuant to physician=approved hospital or
  1 23 facility policy and after the patient has been assessed for
  1 24 contraindications.
