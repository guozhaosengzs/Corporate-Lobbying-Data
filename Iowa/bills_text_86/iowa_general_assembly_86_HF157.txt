  1 33                           EXPLANATION
  1 34 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 35 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  1 This bill amends and repeals portions of Code chapter
  2  2 252 (support of the poor) and makes other conforming changes
  2  3 to eliminate the liability of any relative (father, mother,
  2  4 children, grandparent, grandchild, or more distant relative)
  2  5 of the poor person for support of the poor person or recovery
  2  6 from such relative of money expended by the county for the
  2  7 poor person. Under the bill, only the poor person is liable
  2  8 for repayment of money expended by the county on behalf of the
  2  9 poor person.  The county may recover moneys expended for the
  2 10 assistance or support of a poor person from the poor person
  2 11 while living, if the person becomes able and the action to
  2 12 recover is filed within two years after the person becomes
  2 13 able; or, if the poor person is deceased, from the person's
  2 14 estate by filing the claim as provided by law.
