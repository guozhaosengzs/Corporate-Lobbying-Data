  1  7                           EXPLANATION
  1  8 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  9 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 10    This bill amends the "Iowa Farmers' Market Nutrition Program
  1 11 Act" (Code chapter 175B) administered by the department of
  1 12 agriculture and land stewardship.  One program administered
  1 13 by the department is the senior farmers' market nutrition
  1 14 program (SFMNP) as authorized by the federal Farm Security and
  1 15 Rural Investment Act of 2002 (7 U.S.C. {3007 and 7 C.F.R. pt.
  1 16 249).  The bill requires all categories of participants under 7
  1 17 C.F.R. {249.6 are eligible to receive assistance.  That section
  1 18 mandates that generally participants must be at least 60 years
  1 19 old, but that states may include persons who are less than that
  1 20 age, including Native Americans who are at least 55 years old
  1 21 and certain disabled individuals who are less than 60 years
  1 22 old living in housing facilities occupied primarily by older
  1 23 individuals where congregate nutrition services are provided.
