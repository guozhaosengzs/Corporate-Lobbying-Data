  1 11                           EXPLANATION
  1 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 13 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 14    Current law provides that a creditor of a service member who,
  1 15 prior to entering military service, entered into a mortgage
  1 16 contract with the service member for the purchase of real
  1 17 or personal property shall not foreclose on the mortgage or
  1 18 repossess the property for nonpayment or for any breach during
  1 19 the service member's military service without an order from
  1 20 a court of competent jurisdiction.  The bill extends this
  1 21 protection against mortgage foreclosures for nine months
  1 22 after the service member's release or discharge from military
  1 23 service.
