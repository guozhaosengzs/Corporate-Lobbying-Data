  1 19                           EXPLANATION
  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    Under current law, the statute of limitations period in
  1 23 an action arising out of the unsafe or defective condition
  1 24 of an improvement to real property must be brought no later
  1 25 than 15 years after the date on which the act or omission of
  1 26 the defendant alleged in the action to have been the cause
  1 27 of the injury or death occurred.  The bill provides that an
  1 28 action relating to improvements to real property arising out
  1 29 of the unsafe or defective condition of an improvement to real
  1 30 property must be brought within the 15=year limitation period
  1 31 or within two years after the act or omission of the defendant
  1 32 alleged in the action to have been the cause of the injury or
  1 33 death is discovered or by the exercise of reasonable diligence
  1 34 should have been discovered, whichever is earlier.
