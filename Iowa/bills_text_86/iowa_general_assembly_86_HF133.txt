  1  7 medical assistance to any authorized employee or agent of the
  1  8 department or to the applicant or recipient.
  1  9    2.  The department shall develop and make available an easily
  1 10 downloadable request form bearing an electronic signature of an

  1 12 applicant or recipient in requesting the records specified in
  1 13 this section.
  1 14    3.  A financial institution that unreasonably refuses to
  1 15 provide the records specified in this section within the time
  1 16 limit provided in the request or that willfully provides false
  1 17 information in the response to the request shall be assessed a
  1 18 civil penalty of not more than fifty dollars.
  1 19    4.  For the purposes of this section, "financial institution"
  1 20 means financial institution as defined in section 252I.1.
