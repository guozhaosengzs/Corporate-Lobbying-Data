  1 13                           EXPLANATION
  1 14 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 15 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 16    This bill relates to the limitation of action (statute of
  1 17 limitations) for the criminal offense of accessory after the
  1 18 fact.
  1 19    The bill provides that an indictment or information for
  1 20 the offense of accessory after the fact in violation of Code
  1 21 section 703.3 that involves a murder in the first degree
  1 22 shall be found within 10 years after the commission of the
  1 23 offense of accessory after the fact.  Current law provides that
  1 24 such violation shall be found within three years after the
  1 25 commission of the offense of accessory after the fact.
  1 26    A person who commits the offense of accessory after the fact
  1 27 involving a murder in the first degree commits an aggravated
  1 28 misdemeanor.
  1 29    An aggravated misdemeanor is punishable by confinement for
  1 30 no more than two years and a fine of at least $625 but not more
  1 31 than $6,250.
