  1  3    1.  The board of directors of a school district shall
  1  4 operate, control, and supervise all public schools located
  1  5 within its district boundaries and may exercise any broad and
  1  6 implied power, not inconsistent with the laws of the general
  1  7 assembly, related to the operation, control, and supervision
  1  8 of those public schools.
  1  9    2.  Notwithstanding subsection 1, the board of directors of
  1 10 a school district shall not have power to levy any tax unless
  1 11 expressly authorized by the general assembly.
  1 12    3.  This chapter, chapter 257, chapter 257B, and chapters
  1 13 275 through 301, and other statutes relating to the boards of
  1 14 directors of school districts and to school districts shall be
  1 15 liberally construed to effectuate the purposes of subsection 1.

  1 18 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.

  1 20 directors of a school district.

  1 22 implied power, not inconsistent with the laws of the general
  1 23 assembly, related to the operation, control, and supervision
  1 24 of the public schools located within its district boundaries.
  1 25 However, the authority does not encompass the power to levy
  1 26 any tax unless expressly authorized by the general assembly.
  1 27 Statutes relating to school boards and school districts shall
  1 28 be liberally construed to effectuate the authority granted
  1 29 under the bill.
