  1 32                           EXPLANATION
  1 33 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 34 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 35    This bill provides that, beginning January 1, 2015, the
  2  1 racing and gaming commission, in regulating the purse structure
  2  2 for horse racing, shall require that purse moneys designated
  2  3 for horse racing for each calendar year be no less than the
  2  4 purse moneys designated for horse racing during calendar year
  2  5 2014.  Current law provides that the purse moneys designated
  2  6 for horse racing be no less than a certain percentage of gross
  2  7 receipts from gambling games at the gambling games licensee in
  2  8 Polk county.  The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies
  2  9 retroactively to January 1, 2015.
  2 10    The bill further provides that if required purse moneys for
  2 11 horse racing exceed the amount designated for horse purses
  2 12 from gambling game receipts, the commission shall collect
  2 13 the difference, to be used for horse racing, as a fee on new
  2 14 gambling game licensees issued a license under Code chapter 99F
  2 15 after January 1, 2014.

  1 28    Sec. 2.  EFFECTIVE UPON ENACTMENT.  This Act, being deemed of
  1 29 immediate importance, takes effect upon enactment.
  1 30    Sec. 3.  RETROACTIVE APPLICABILITY.  This Act applies
  1 31 retroactively to January 1, 2015.
  1 32                           EXPLANATION
  1 33 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 34 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 35    This bill provides that, beginning January 1, 2015, the
  2  1 racing and gaming commission, in regulating the purse structure
  2  2 for horse racing, shall require that purse moneys designated
  2  3 for horse racing for each calendar year be no less than the
  2  4 purse moneys designated for horse racing during calendar year
  2  5 2014.  Current law provides that the purse moneys designated
  2  6 for horse racing be no less than a certain percentage of gross
  2  7 receipts from gambling games at the gambling games licensee in
  2  8 Polk county.  The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies
  2  9 retroactively to January 1, 2015.
  2 10    The bill further provides that if required purse moneys for
  2 11 horse racing exceed the amount designated for horse purses
  2 12 from gambling game receipts, the commission shall collect
  2 13 the difference, to be used for horse racing, as a fee on new
  2 14 gambling game licensees issued a license under Code chapter 99F
  2 15 after January 1, 2014.
