  1  3    An executive branch administrative unit that regulates a
  1  4 profession shall only assess a fee for renewal of a license,
  1  5 certificate, registration, or other authorization to practice
  1  6 the profession from a person who holds a current authorization

  1  9 the time the person acquired or most recently renewed the
  1 10 authorization, regardless of whether the unit modified a
  1 11 requirement for initially obtaining the license during the
  1 12 period for which the person's authorization was valid.
