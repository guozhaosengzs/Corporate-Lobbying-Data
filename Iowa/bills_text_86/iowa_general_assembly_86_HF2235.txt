  1  1    Section 1.    1  1    Section 1.    1  2    An employee of an appointing authority may be granted leave
  1  3 with pay from work for not more than one hour in any work
  1  4 week to participate in volunteer activities at a nonprofit
  1  5 organization or school upon the approval of the employee's
  1  6 appointing authority without loss of seniority, pay, vacation
  1  7 time, personal days, sick leave, insurance and health coverage
  1  8 benefits, or earned overtime accumulation. The appointing
  1  9 authority shall compensate an employee granted leave under this
  1 10 section at the employee's regular rate of pay for those regular
  1 11 work hours during which the employee is absent from work. An
  1 12 employee deemed to be on leave under this section shall not be
  1 13 deemed to be an employee of the state for purposes of workers'
  1 14 compensation. An employee deemed to be on leave under this
  1 15 section shall not be deemed to be an employee of the state for
  1 16 purposes of the Iowa tort claims Act, chapter 669.

  1 24 lose any pay or benefits as a result of the paid leave and shall
