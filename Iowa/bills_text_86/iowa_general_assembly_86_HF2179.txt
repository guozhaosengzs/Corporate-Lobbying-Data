  1 24 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 25 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 26    This bill provides that for purposes of requirements that
  1 27 a deer carcass must be affixed with a tag before being moved
  1 28 for transport, the carcass can be moved away from an obstacle,
  1 29 entanglement, waterway, or any area, including but not limited
  1 30 to a roadway, if tagging the carcass at that location would be
  1 31 a safety hazard to the hunter or a third person, before the tag
  1 32 is attached.  The bill also provides that the carcass shall
  1 33 not be moved from the immediate vicinity of where the deer was
  1 34 taken, shall be moved only so far as is necessary to avoid the
  1 35 safety hazard, and shall be immediately tagged upon being so
  2  1 moved and before being moved to be transported.  A violation
  2  2 of the bill's provisions is punishable with a scheduled fine
  2  3 of $25.
