  1 19                           EXPLANATION
  1 20 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    Current law provides for farm house exemptions from county
  1 23 building codes and county zoning regulations. The bill removes
  1 24 these exemptions for farm houses while maintaining similar
  1 25 exemptions for other agricultural structures that are not
  1 26 dwellings.
  1 27    Pursuant to Code section 414.23, the changes in the bill to
  1 28 the county zoning exemption for farm houses apply to a city
  1 29 extending city zoning regulations to the unincorporated area
  1 30 of a county up to two miles beyond the limits of such city, as
  1 31 authorized by statute.
