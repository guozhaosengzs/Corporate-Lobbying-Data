  1  4 described in section 708.2, subsection 6, against a sports

  1  6 facility or field at which an athletic contest is being or has
  1  7 recently been held in which the sports official took an active
  1  8 officiating role, is guilty of a serious misdemeanor.

  1 10 a person who serves as a referee, umpire, linesman, or

  1 12 registered member of a local, state, regional, or national
  1 13 organization which is engaged, in part, in providing education
  1 14 and training related to officiating such athletic contests.
