  1 10    Sec. 2.  EFFECTIVE UPON ENACTMENT.  This Act, being deemed of
  1 11 immediate importance, takes effect upon enactment.
  1 12    Sec. 3.  RETROACTIVE APPLICABILITY.  This Act applies

  1 14                           EXPLANATION
  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    This bill makes a change to prior reimbursement rate
  1 18 language in the 2015 health and human services appropriations
  1 19 bill (2015 Iowa Acts, chapter 137 (Senate File 505)) requiring
  1 20 the statewide average reimbursement rates paid through
  1 21 the department of human services to juvenile shelter care
  1 22 providers for FY 2015=2016 to remain at the rate in effect in
  1 23 FY 2014=2015.  The bill amends this provision to require the
  1 24 statewide average reimbursement rate paid to juvenile shelter
  1 25 care providers to be increased by $4.85 over the reimbursement
  1 26 rate in effect for FY 2014=2015.
  1 27    The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies
  1 28 retroactively to July 1, 2015.
