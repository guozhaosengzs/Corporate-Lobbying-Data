 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to Medicaid special needs trusts.
  2 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    HF 648 (3) 86
    pf/nh/md

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 633C.1, subsection 8, Code 2015, is
  1  2 amended by striking the subsection.
  1  3    Sec. 2.  Section 633C.2, Code 2015, is amended by striking
  1  4 the section and inserting in lieu thereof the following:
  1  5    633C.2  Disposition of medical assistance special needs
  1  6 trusts.
  1  7    Any income or assets added to or received by and any income
  1  8 or principal retained in a medical assistance special needs
  1  9 trust shall be used in accordance with a standard that is
  1 10 no more restrictive than specified under federal law. All
  1 11 distributions from a medical assistance special needs trust
  1 12 shall be for the sole benefit of the beneficiary to enhance
  1 13 the quality of life of the beneficiary, and the trustee shall
  1 14 have sole discretion regarding such disbursements to ensure
  1 15 compliance with beneficiary eligibility requirements. Any
  1 16 distinct disbursement in excess of one thousand dollars shall
  1 17 be subject to review by the district court sitting in probate.
  1 18 The department shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 17A for
  1 19 the establishment and disposition of medical assistance special
  1 20 needs trusts in accordance with this section.
  1 21    Sec. 3.  Section 633C.4, subsection 2, Code 2015, is amended
  1 22 to read as follows:
  1 23    2.  The trustee of a medical assistance income trust or
  1 24 a medical assistance special needs trust is a fiduciary for
  1 25 purposes of chapter 633A and, in the exercise of the trustee's
  1 26 fiduciary duties, the state shall be considered a beneficiary
  1 27 of the trust. Regardless of the terms of the trust, the
  1 28 trustee shall not take any action that is not prudent in light
  1 29 of the state's interest in the trust.                                      APPROPRIATIONS
