  1 20                           EXPLANATION
  1 21 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 22 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 23    This bill relates to tanning facilities and creates new Code
  1 24 section 136D.3A prohibiting a tanning facility from allowing a
  1 25 person under 18 years of age to use a tanning device.  The bill
  1 26 also provides that warning signs shall be free from obstruction
  1 27 and that a written warning statement shall be provided to each
  1 28 customer prior to use of a tanning device.
  1 29    A person who violates a provision of Code chapter 136D is
  1 30 subject to a civil penalty and an injunction restraining any
  1 31 violation.
