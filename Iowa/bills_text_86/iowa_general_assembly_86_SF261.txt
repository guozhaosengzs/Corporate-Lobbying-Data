  1 21 retroactively to January 1, 2015, for tax years beginning on
  1 22 or after that date.

  1 29 employers are no longer required to deduct and withhold wages
  1 30 from exempt individuals.

  1 32 retroactively to tax years beginning on or after January 1,
  1 33 2015.
