 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to child care provider reimbursement rates
  2    under the state child care assistance program.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 2148YH (3) 86
    rh/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  CHILD CARE PROVIDER REIMBURSEMENT RATES.  For the
  1  2 fiscal year beginning July 1, 2015, for child care providers
  1  3 reimbursed under the state child care assistance program,
  1  4 the department shall set provider reimbursement rates based
  1  5 on the rate reimbursement survey completed in December 2014.
  1  6 The department shall set rates in a manner so as to provide
  1  7 incentives for a nonregistered provider to become registered
  1  8 by applying the increase only to registered and licensed
  1  9 providers.
  1 10                           EXPLANATION
  1 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 12 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 13    This bill requires the department of human services to set
  1 14 child care provider reimbursement rates under the state child
  1 15 care assistance program based on the rate reimbursement survey
  1 16 completed in December 2014. The department shall set rates
  1 17 in a manner so as to provide incentives for a nonregistered
  1 18 provider to become registered by applying the increase only to
  1 19 registered and licensed providers.
       LSB 2148YH (3) 86
       rh/nh
