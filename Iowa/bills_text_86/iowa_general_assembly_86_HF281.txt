  1 21 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 22    This bill authorizes the paying of awards and prizes to
  1 23 participants in fantasy sports contest.
  1 24    Code section 99B.11, concerning bona fide contests, is
  1 25 amended to provide that a fantasy or simulation sports contest
  1 26 is a bona fide contest, allowing the payment of awards to
  1 27 persons winning the contest, if certain conditions are met.
  1 28 The bill provides that a fantasy sports contest is a bona
  1 29 fide contest if all prizes and awards offered to winning
  1 30 participants are established and made known in advance of the
  1 31 contest, all winning outcomes reflect the relative knowledge
  1 32 and skill of the participants and are determined predominantly
  1 33 by accumulated statistical results of the performance of
  1 34 individuals in multiple actual sporting events, and no winning
  1 35 outcome is based on the score, or performance of any single
  2  1 actual team or combination of such teams or solely on any
  2  2 single performance of an individual athlete in any single
  2  3 actual sporting or other event.
