PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 321.345, Code 2016, is amended to read
  1  2 as follows:
  1  3    321.345  Stop or yield at highways.
  1  4    The department, based on an engineering study, with
  1  5 reference to primary highways, and local authorities with
  1  6 reference to other highways under their jurisdiction may
  1  7 designate through highways and erect stop signs or yield
  1  8 signs, in accordance with specifications established by
  1  9 the department at specified entrances to the highway or may
  1 10 designate any intersection as a stop intersection or as a yield
  1 11 intersection and erect like signs at one or more entrances
  1 12 to such intersection.
