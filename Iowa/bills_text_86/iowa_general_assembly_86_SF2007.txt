  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 17    As part of the county budgeting process, Code section
  1 18 331.433 provides that after the compilation of various county

  1 22 requested amounts.  This bill changes the board of supervisors'
