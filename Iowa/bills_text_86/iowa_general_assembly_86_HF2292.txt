  1 34    This bill relates to carrying weapons on school grounds by
  1 35 a certified peace officer with a professional permit to carry
  2  1 weapons. The bill provides that a certified peace officer
  2  2 who possesses a professional permit to carry weapons does not
  2  3 commit the criminal violation of unlawfully carrying weapons on
  2  4 school grounds under Code section 724.4B.  Under current law, a
  2  5 peace officer while acting within the official duties of the
  2  6 officer may possess a weapon on school grounds. A person who
  2  7 commits the offense of unlawfully carrying weapons on school
  2  8 grounds commits a class "D" felony.
