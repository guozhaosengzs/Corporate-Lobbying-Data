  1 24                           EXPLANATION
  1 25 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 26 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 27    This bill prohibits a physician from performing an abortion,
  1 28 except in the case of a medical emergency, if the fetus has
  1 29 reached a weight of 350 grams or more. A person who performs an
  1 30 abortion in violation of the prohibition is guilty of a class
  1 31 "C" felony.
  1 32    The bill also amends the feticide statute to include
  1 33 intentional terminations of a pregnancy when the fetus has
  1 34 reached a weight of 350 grams or more. Where death of the
  1 35 fetus results, a person commits feticide which is a class "C"
  2  1 felony, and where death of the fetus does not result, the
  2  2 person commits attempted feticide which is a class "D" felony.
  2  3 Currently, a person can only commit feticide or attempted
  2  4 feticide after the second trimester of the pregnancy.
  2  5    A class "C" felony is punishable by confinement for no more
  2  6 than 10 years and a fine of at least $1,000 but not more than
  2  7 $10,000. A class "D" felony is punishable by confinement for
  2  8 no more than five years and a fine of at least $750 but not more
  2  9 than $7,500.
