  2  7 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  8 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  9    This bill relates to the preparation and issuance of vital
  2 10 statistics certificates.
  2 11    Under current law, a person is not allowed to prepare or
  2 12 issue an original, certified copy, or copy of a certificate
  2 13 of birth, death, fetal death, or marriage except as provided
  2 14 in Code chapter 144. The bill maintains these restrictions
  2 15 for originals and certified copies. For other copies, the
  2 16 bill provides that no person, in the person's capacity as an
  2 17 employee or agent of the state, or a political subdivision
  2 18 of the state, shall prepare or issue any certificate which
  2 19 purports to be a copy of such a certificate except as necessary
  2 20 in the scope of the person's employment or agency or as
  2 21 provided in Code chapter 144.
