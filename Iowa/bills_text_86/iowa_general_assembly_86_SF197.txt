 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act concerning the definition of beer for purposes of beer
  2    brewers and wholesalers.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 1435SV (2) 86
    ec/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 123A.2, subsection 3, Code 2015, is
  1  2 amended by striking the subsection and inserting in lieu
  1  3 thereof the following:
  1  4    3.  "Beer" means beer or high alcoholic content beer as
  1  5 defined in section 123.3.
  1  6                           EXPLANATION
  1  7 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  8 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  9    This bill provides that beer, for purposes of Code chapter
  1 10 123A concerning beer brewers and wholesalers, means beer or
  1 11 high alcoholic content beer as defined in Code chapter 123.
  1 12 The current definition of beer in Code chapter 123A does not
  1 13 include high alcoholic content beer.
