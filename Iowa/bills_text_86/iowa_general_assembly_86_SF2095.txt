  1  4    The board of educational examiners shall review and approve,
  1  5 in consultation with the department of public health, the
  1  6 Iowa law enforcement academy, and the crime victim assistance
  1  7 division of the department of justice, training curricula on
  1  8 the subject of human trafficking, as defined in section 710A.1,
  1  9 to be used to train teachers to recognize and report incidents
  1 10 of human trafficking. A teacher shall complete such training
  1 11 which may be delivered via the internet. The board shall
  1 12 verify that a teacher is in compliance with this section prior
  1 13 to renewing the teacher's license.
