  1 18 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 19 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 20    This bill relates to elder abuse.   The bill eliminates
  1 21 a listing of persons who were exempt from the confidential
  1 22 relationship requirement in order to be considered as standing
  1 23 in a position of trust or confidence with a vulnerable elder
  1 24 and thereby potentially subject to an allegation of financial
  1 25 exploitation under the elder abuse Code chapter.  The bill
  1 26 provides instead that the determination of the existence of a
  1 27 confidential relationship is an issue of fact to be determined
  1 28 by the court based upon the totality of the circumstances.
