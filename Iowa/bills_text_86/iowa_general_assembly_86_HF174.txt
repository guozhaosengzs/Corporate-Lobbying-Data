  1 15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 16 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.

  1 18 general fund of the state for FY 2014=2015 to the division of
  1 19 community action agencies of the department of human rights for

  1 21 appropriation being supplemented is from federal block grant
  1 22 funds.
  1 23    The bill takes effect upon enactment.
