  2  2 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  3 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  4    This bill modifies membership and other requirements
  2  5 applicable to the Iowa utilities board.
  2  6    The bill increases the current number of members of the
  2  7 board from three to five members and increases the limitation
  2  8 on the number of members who are permitted to be from the same
  2  9 political party from two to three members.
  2 10    Additionally, the bill adds restrictions relating to
  2 11 membership eligibility and actions after departure from
  2 12 membership.  The bill provides that a person is prohibited
  2 13 from employment with any common carrier or other public
  2 14 utility, or from acquiring any bonds, stock, or property in
  2 15 any railroad company or other public utility for at least two
  2 16 years following their departure from serving as a member of the
  2 17 board or secretary of the board. Further, the bill provides
  2 18 that a person shall not be eligible to become appointed to the
  2 19 board or serve as secretary of the board for at least two years
  2 20 following their departure from service as a member of the Iowa
  2 21 general assembly.
