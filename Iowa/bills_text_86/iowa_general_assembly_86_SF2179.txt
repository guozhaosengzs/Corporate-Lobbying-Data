  2  5                           EXPLANATION
  2  6 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2  7 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2  8    This bill permits the economic development authority
  2  9 (authority) to provide additional tax incentives to businesses
  2 10 using apprenticeship training program contractors and
  2 11 subcontractors for projects in which qualifying investments are
  2 12 made under the high quality jobs program.
  2 13    Under the bill, the authority is permitted to provide an
  2 14 additional investment tax credit to a business if the business
  2 15 contracts with a contractor or subcontractor who operates or
  2 16 participates in an apprenticeship program for the construction
  2 17 or equipping of a facility of the business.
  2 18    Under the bill, if an eligible business directs 70 percent
  2 19 of the qualifying investment to qualifying contractors
  2 20 or subcontractors, the business is eligible to receive an
  2 21 additional investment tax credit of up to 1.40 percent.
  2 22 If an eligible business directs more than 70 percent of
  2 23 the qualifying investment to qualifying contractors or
