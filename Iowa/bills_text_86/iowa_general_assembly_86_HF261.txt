  1  7 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  8 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  9    This bill relates to the regulation of smokeless powder
  1 10 as an explosive material. Under current law the explosive
  1 11 materials regulations under Code chapter 101A do not apply
  1 12 to the possession of 25 pounds or less of smokeless powder
  1 13 if the smokeless powder is intended for handloading or
  1 14 reloading of ammunition for small arms, is for private use, and
  1 15 conforms to certain other regulations. The bill raises the
  1 16 nonapplicability provision to include the possession of 100
  1 17 pounds or less of smokeless powder.
