 SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION

  1 A Joint Resolution proposing an amendment to the Constitution
  2    of the State of Iowa specifying that no fundamental right to
  3    abortion exists under the Constitution of the State of Iowa.
  4 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5364XS (3) 86
    pf/rj

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  The following amendment to the Constitution of
  1  2 the State of Iowa is proposed:
  1  3    Article I of the Constitution of the State of Iowa is amended
  1  4 by adding the following new section:
  1  5 Abortion. SEC. 26.  The Constitution of the State of Iowa
  1  6 does not secure or protect a fundamental right to abortion.
  1  7 The people retain the right through the enactment of laws by
  1  8 members of the general assembly, subject to the approval of the
  1  9 governor, to prohibit or restrict or otherwise regulate the
  1 10 performance of abortions.
  1 11    Sec. 2.  REFERRAL AND PUBLICATION.  The foregoing amendment
  1 12 to the Constitution of the State of Iowa is referred to the
  1 13 general assembly to be chosen at the next general election
  1 14 for members of the general assembly, and the secretary of
  1 15 state is directed to cause the same to be published for three
  1 16 consecutive months previous to the date of that election as
  1 17 provided by law.
  1 18                           EXPLANATION
  1 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 21    This joint resolution proposes an amendment to the
  1 22 Constitution of the State of Iowa specifying that the
  1 23 Constitution of the State of Iowa does not secure or protect
  1 24 a fundamental right to abortion and that the people retain
  1 25 the right through the enactment of laws by members of the
  1 26 general assembly, subject to the approval of the governor, to
  1 27 prohibit or restrict or otherwise regulate the performance of
  1 28 abortions.
  1 29    The joint resolution, if adopted, would be referred to the
  1 30 next general assembly for adoption a second time before being
  1 31 submitted to the electorate for ratification.

  1  2 the State of Iowa is proposed:
  1  3    Article I of the Constitution of the State of Iowa is amended
  1  4 by adding the following new section:
  1  5 Abortion. SEC. 26.  The Constitution of the State of Iowa
  1  6 does not secure or protect a fundamental right to abortion.
  1  7 The people retain the right through the enactment of laws by
  1  8 members of the general assembly, subject to the approval of the
  1  9 governor, to prohibit or restrict or otherwise regulate the
  1 10 performance of abortions.
  1 11    Sec. 2.  REFERRAL AND PUBLICATION.  The foregoing amendment
  1 12 to the Constitution of the State of Iowa is referred to the
  1 13 general assembly to be chosen at the next general election
  1 14 for members of the general assembly, and the secretary of
  1 15 state is directed to cause the same to be published for three
  1 16 consecutive months previous to the date of that election as
  1 17 provided by law.
  1 18                           EXPLANATION
  1 19 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1 20 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1 21    This joint resolution proposes an amendment to the
  1 22 Constitution of the State of Iowa specifying that the
  1 23 Constitution of the State of Iowa does not secure or protect
  1 24 a fundamental right to abortion and that the people retain
  1 25 the right through the enactment of laws by members of the
  1 26 general assembly, subject to the approval of the governor, to
  1 27 prohibit or restrict or otherwise regulate the performance of
  1 28 abortions.
  1 29    The joint resolution, if adopted, would be referred to the
  1 30 next general assembly for adoption a second time before being
  1 31 submitted to the electorate for ratification.
