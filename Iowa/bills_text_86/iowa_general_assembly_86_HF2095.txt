 A BILL FOR

  1 An Act relating to the elimination of the exemption of gaming
  2    floors from the prohibitions of the smokefree air Act.
  3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
    TLSB 5273YH (1) 86
    pf/nh

PAG LIN



  1  1    Section 1.  Section 142D.4, subsection 10, Code 2016, is
  1  2 amended by striking the subsection.
  1  3                           EXPLANATION
  1  4 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  1  5 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  1  6    This bill eliminates the exception under the smokefree air
  1  7 Act (Code chapter 142D), allowing smoking on the gaming floors
  1  8 of the premises licensed pursuant to Code chapter 99F (gambling
  1  9 structures, excursion gambling boats, and racetracks). The
  1 10 bill thereby subjects the entirety of these premises to the
  1 11 smoking prohibitions of the Act.
       LSB 5273YH (1) 86
