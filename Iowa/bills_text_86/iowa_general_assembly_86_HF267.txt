  1 24 when the vehicle is operated on a highway.  The bill prohibits
  1 25 a person from operating a motorcycle or motorized bicycle on
  1 26 a highway if the operator or any passenger is not wearing a
  1 27 safety helmet. The bill also prohibits a person from being a
  1 28 passenger on a motorcycle or motorized bicycle if the operator
  1 29 is not wearing a safety helmet.
  1 30    The safety helmet must be in compliance with federal
  1 31 regulations and must fit the person's head securely and be
  1 32 fastened with helmet straps.  The bill directs the department
  1 33 of transportation to establish guidelines for motorcycle safety
  1 34 helmets and to maintain and publish a list of approved helmets.
  1 35    Pursuant to current law, a violation of motorcycle or
  2  1 motorized bicycle provisions is a simple misdemeanor punishable
