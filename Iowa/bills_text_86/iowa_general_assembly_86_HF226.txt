  2 13 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
  2 14 the explanation's substance by the members of the general assembly.
  2 15    This bill relates to income tax credits for the installation
  2 16 of certain geothermal energy systems in Iowa.
  2 17    Under current law, an individual income tax credit is
  2 18 available for the installation in Iowa of a geothermal heat
  2 19 pump.  The credit is equal to 20 percent of the federal
  2 20 residential energy efficient property tax credit for geothermal
  2 21 heat pumps.  The bill raises the credit amount from 20 percent
  2 22 to 60 percent of the federal credit.
  2 23    The bill also provides an individual and corporate income
  2 24 tax credit in an amount equal to 60 percent of the federal
  2 25 energy credit provided in section 48 of the Internal Revenue
  2 26 Code for the installation of a geothermal energy system, if the
  2 27 property is located in Iowa.  Any credit in excess of the tax
  2 28 liability is nonrefundable, but may be carried forward for up
  2 29 to 10 years.
  2 30    The bill takes effect upon enactment and applies
  2 31 retroactively to tax years beginning on or after January 1,
  2 32 2015.
