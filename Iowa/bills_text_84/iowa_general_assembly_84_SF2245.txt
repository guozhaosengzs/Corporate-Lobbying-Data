1.
IOWA
VETERANS
HOME
——
POSTTRAUMATIC
STRESS

DIAGNOSIS
TREATMENT

1.
The
Iowa

home
shall

the

posttraumatic

dual

and

The

the

of
public

the

assembly
and
the
governor
no

15,
2013,
regarding
the
establishment
of
a


posttraumatic
stress
dual
diagnosis
treatment
program
at

Iowa
veterans
home.
The
report
shall
make

to
